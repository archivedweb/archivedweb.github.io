Frustrated,

You are correct -- the Aztech sound cards were not 100% directX 
compatible at the time of Diablo's release.  In order to get your 
sound to work correctly, you will have to get DirectX compatible 
drivers for your sound card.

The best way to do this is to contact the manufacturer of your 
computer and ask them for the drivers.  If you put together the 
computer yourself, then you should contact Aztech directly for the 
DirectX drivers.  

Aztech's web site can be found here:
http://www.aztechca.com/aztfiles.html#top
