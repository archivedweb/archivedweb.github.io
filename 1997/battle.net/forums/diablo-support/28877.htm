Capt. Haggis,

Before you run Diablo, make sure you have already connected to your 
ISP.  Then, run Diablo, choose multiplayer game, choose your create 
your character, and choose battle.net.

If you are still getting the sympatico access finder screen, then do 
this for me:

click on Start--Setting--control panel.
Open Internet
select the connection tab
uncheck the "connect to internet when needed" box.  If it is 
already unchecked, then check and uncheck it again.  Your registry 
may be configured to connect to the internet when needed, but your 
internet control panel may not be representing this correctly, 
"re-unchecking" should fix things.

If you are still having problems, contact your ISP to make sure your 
connection with them supports 32bit internet applications.  They 
should be able to help you with this.
