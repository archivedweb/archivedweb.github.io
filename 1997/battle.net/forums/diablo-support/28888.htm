Kkinbler,

No, you cannot transfer your single player characters to the 
multiplayer game, I'm afraid.  If you wish to play the multiplayer 
game, you will have to creat a new multiplayer character. 

Because the multiplayer game has 3 different difficulty settings, it 
should not take you too long to bring your new multiplayer character 
to the level of your old single player character (you get more 
experience in Nightmare and Hells games than you do in the single 
player game).
