On Tue Jul 15 12:33, T.j. wrote:
> How do i sign up to play Diablo on battle.net? I am useing AOL and 
> also how much does it cost?
You'll need to acquire AOL 3.0 from America Online.  Basically, this 
is a 32-bit application which allows you to utilize Dial Up 
Networking to connect to the Internet.  Once you have done this, you 
will be able to play on the Battle.net.  However, be warned, that AOL 
has a high latency to the Internet (latency = the time that it takes 
for a piece 
of information to get sent from the source computer to the 
destination computer.) which can cause game performance to be slow.  

Also, Battle.net has no charges or fees associated with it at all.  
The only fees you would pay would be your normal internet access 
charges (if any).
