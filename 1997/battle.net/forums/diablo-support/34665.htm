Usually this is caused by having your desktop set to a different 
resolution than 640X480, 256 colors. Direct X has trouble switching  
desktops back when you exit Diablo Set it to this resolution before 
you play and see if that fixes it. Also, updating your vido card 
driver might help if you have an old driver. Click on the FAQ at the 
top of this forum and you should be able to find a link to your video 
card's manufacturer.   Lonara
