This is a common problem and is easily fixed.

Did you download the DRTL104.exe patch from the Blizzard site, if you 
have not, get it from here

http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/patch.htm

Put that file in your Diablo folder.

Uninstall Diablo, put the Diablo CD in the CD drive and choose 
uninstall Diablo from the auto play menu.  When prompted, make sure 
do not delete your saved games.  You do not want to lose your 
characters.

After you have uninstalled, reinstall, do not play Diablo yet.

Go to your Diablo folder and double click the patch file DRTL104.exe. 
 Try Diablo again.  If you have any more problems post back.

Hope this helps,
