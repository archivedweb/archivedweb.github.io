On Tue Jul 15 15:22, Curt Cummings wrote:

[640x480 too small]

> It is a laptop (great for going over to Diablo get togethers with 
> friends).  
> Even in 640x480 mode the screen is still small.  Since WIN95 runs on 
> the full screen, could it be a bug in the directx control?

No, this is a problem laptops with LCD/TFT-display face - it is 
because of the fact that the display has "fixed pixels" which 
cannot change size - i.e. your display has 800x600 pixels, and when 
you change the resolution to 640x480 (as Diablo does), you only use 
640x480 pixels of your 800x600, and as a result the screen is 
smaller. You cannot change this, it is a result of the display 
technology your laptop uses.
