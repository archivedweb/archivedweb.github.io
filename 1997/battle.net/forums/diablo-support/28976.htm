Dave,

If you are having trouble updating your version of Diablo by 
connecting to battle.net, then try downloading the patch from our web 
site here:
http://www.blizzard.com/library/patches.htm
Download the patch into your Diablo folder, then run it.  Your 
version will be updated to 1.04 and you should be able to connect to 
battle.net with no more problems.

If your game is still freezing, then manually change your win95 
display settings to the ones used by Diablo before playing.  DirectX 
has problem changing display settings on some systems, and manually 
changing them will fix a number of freezes in the game.  To do this:
right click on your desktop
select properties
select the settings tab
change your resolution to 640x480
change your colors to 256
click apply --reboot your computer

Thanks,
Pat N.
