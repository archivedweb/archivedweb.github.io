Patrik,

Do you notice a particular event in the game that triggers this 
(clicking on an item, casting a certain spells, etc)?  Trying turning 
off all of your sound within the game, and see if the keyboard and 
mouse still locks up.  To do this, hit <esc> while playing, 
then select Options.  Turn your sound and video all the way down.

You should also make sure you have the latest possible drivers for 
your sound and video card.  Take a look here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/directx.htm
for a list of links to updated drivers for a number of devices (a 
link to Matrox drivers is on this page).

Also, are you running any processes on your system that could be 
capturing keyboard commands (like desktop hot keys)?  If you are, 
then try disabling those before you play Diablo.

Let us know how you're doing,
Pat N.
