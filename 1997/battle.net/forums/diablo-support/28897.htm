Dark Omen,

The highest levels you can reach with your spells is 20:

15 --maximum level attainable through books/shrines
1  --Naj's light plate
2  --Thinking cap
2  --Staff with the Arch-Angel's prefix

I wouldn't suggest worrying about spells this high, however.  Because 
it takes these three items to attain it, you will be limited to your 
play strategies.  You probably will find that, if you have one of 
these items and equip yourself with other gear that fits your playing 
style, your spellpower will be high enough, and you'll have more 
freedom to configure your AC/resistances/stats/etc by equipping other 
items.
