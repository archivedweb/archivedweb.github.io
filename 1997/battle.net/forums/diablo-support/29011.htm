Thank You,

Yes, it is strange that you were killed in one hit by a creature's 
fireball with full health and mana shield.  The most common cause for 
this would be that you were actually killed by another player, but, 
because you were in a solo game, this could not be the case.

The next reason that would explain this (though I am a bit doubtful) 
is that you did _not_ have you mana shield going when you were hit.  
This could have happened if you had lost your mana shield previously, 
then drank a mana potion, but forgot to bring up your mana shield 
again.  This would make you much more vulnerable to those fireballs.  
However, if this was the case, you should still have a full mana ball 
when you died.  When you restarted in town, you mana ball would be 
emtpy, but do you remember if it was empty when you actually died.  
If it was not, then perhaps you forgot to cast mana shield after you 
lost it.

The phenonmenon you experienced when you returned to Lazarus after 
goin to town is normal, actually.  If you kill all or some of 
Lazarus' minions, then leave that level, Lazarus will go back to 
"sleep."  You will not be able to attack him again until he 
has been "woken up" again.  In this case, he will not wake up 
until you touch him (so get that Stone Curse ready :)

On rare occasions, your town portal can change it's destination after 
you've used it to go to town.  This is very rare, however, so I would 
not worry about it happening again.

Your teleport problem is strange.  I can't explain why your teleport 
spell might not take you to where you were pointing, but, if it 
happens again, can you send your system specifications and a 
description of this to support@blizzard.com?  We'll see if we can't 
figure out what caused this.

Thanks,
pat N.
