> how do you get the battlenet to work, and do you have to have your 
> online service on as you try?

Junior,

You may have to be connected before you run Diablo to connect to 
battle.net, depending on the online service you are using (if your 
ISP is not configured using Win95's dialup networking, you probably 
will have to connect before). 

In order to connect to battle.net, you must have an internet 
connection that supports 32bit applications, and a copy of the game.  
To connect:

Connect to your ISP (note: you may not have to do this -- see 
paragraph above)
Run Diablo
Choose Multiplayer
Choose or create your multiplayer character
Choose battle.net

You will then be connected to battle.net and placed in one of the 
battle.net channels.  From there, hit <F1> for help in using 
battle.net.

If you have further questions, feel free to repost here or send email 
to support@blizzard.com.
