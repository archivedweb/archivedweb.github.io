The only thing wrong with your connection is that you are using AOL.  
You should not see any latency bars by your own name.  Sometimes it 
will show one or two green, but that does not mean anything.  Latency 
is how far away you are from other players, it is not logical that 
you should see your latency to yourself.  You can pretty much tell 
what other people see your latency as by looking at the bars next to 
there name.  More than likely, if you see someone with all red bars, 
thats how they see you.
