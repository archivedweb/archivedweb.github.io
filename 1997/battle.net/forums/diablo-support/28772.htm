AOL is a very slow connection for Internet Games, not just Diablo.  
AOL uses its own network of computers, plus has millions of users.  
It is great for chatting, downloading, and surfing the net, but is 
not quite ready for internet games like Diablo.  You can play Diablo 
on Battle.net using AOL, which I think is an accomplishment, but you 
will always have lag.  Some AOL users have no problems, very few of 
them, but most AOL users experience the same thing as you.  My advice 
to you, if you got the internet to play games, get a better Internet 
Service Provider.  

If you really are just in love with AOL, try and play with other 
people who have green or yellow bars by there name or in games that 
have the same.  Try and play on AOL in non peak times, try and play 
with other AOL users, and turn off the music while you are playing.  
All of this stuff should reduce lag, but I don't know.

Good Luck,
