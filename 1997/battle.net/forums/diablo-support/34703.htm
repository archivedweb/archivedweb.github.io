Hello Andy,

indeed everything with a voodoo (not 'rush') chipset is better in 3d 
than the stingray. The 'Rush' chipset should have
been the '2nd generation' voodoo but it wasn't. Actually it does not 
have the performance of the normal voodoo.
At this time, i personally prefer a combination of a good 2d card 
(Everything with a TSENG ET 6000 like the Hercules
Dynamite 128 or a Matrox Millenium) and a voodoo add-on card (like 
the monster 3d).

Another good alternative is a card with the relatively new 'riva 128' 
chipset from nvidia (Elsa Victory Eraser, Diamond
Viper 330). It's combined 2d/3d card with outstanding performance 
both in 2d and 3d. The only problem is , that there 
are not many games that support it , besides games that support 
direct 3d (so no glquake, pod, ea sports titles at the 
moment).

If this wasn't enough, just take a look at the 'voodooextreme.com'. 
Their hardware-section should provide you with
all the information you need about 3d-cards!!!

Oh, and when you want to keep your stingray, try to install the 
latest drivers from the hercules website. I never heard
of your problem before, but probably it helps.

Good luck...
