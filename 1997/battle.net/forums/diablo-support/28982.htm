Hammerhand,

Are you trying to connect to battle.net while at school, or are you 
dialing into your school's network when you try to connect?

If you are dialing into your school's network, then make sure your 
dialup software supports 32bit internet applications.  If you are 
dialing into a shell account with your school (i.e. when you dial in, 
you see a DOS-like prompt), then you will have to use a PPP emulator 
when you connect to your school.

If you are trying to connect directly from school, then see if your 
school's network has the following ports on its router open:

ports 116 adn 118 must be open for incoming/outgoing TCP packets
port 6112 must be open for incoming/outgoing UDP packets.

If you have further questions, repost here or send email to 
support@blizzard.com.  Describe the error you receive when you are 
trying to connect to battle.net, and describe how you are connecting 
to the internet.  We should be able to help you further.
