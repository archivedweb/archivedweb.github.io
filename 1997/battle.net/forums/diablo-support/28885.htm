Aragorn,

It is true that some video and sound devices have problems using 
DirectX 3.0, and installing DirectX 2.0 can fix this.  If you cannot 
find instructions for installing DirectX 2.0 over 3.0 on Microsoft's 
web site, then download the DirectX 2.0 installer found here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/directx.htm
--look for the link at the bottom of the page

You should also, before running Diablo, manually change your win 95 
display settings to the ones used by Diablo, as DirectX can have 
trouble switching these on some systems.  To do this:

right click on your desktop
select properties
select the settings tab
change your resolution to 640x480
change your colors to 256
click Apply --reboot your computer.
