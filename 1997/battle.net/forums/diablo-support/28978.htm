Nick,

Getting a new CD-ROM drive that uses protected mode drivers should 
fix this problem, but, if you have not done so, you should take a 
look at the Microsoft's troubleshooting article regarding CD-ROMs 
running in DOS compatibility mode here:
http://www.microsoft.com/KB/ARTICLES/Q151/6/34.HTM

You should also contact the manufacturer of your computer to see if 
they have protected mode drivers for your CD-ROM drive.
