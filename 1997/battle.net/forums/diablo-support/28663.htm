> Joe,
> 
> The lag you are experiencing when playing over battle.net can be 
> attributed to your latency.
> 
> You can think of your latency to another player as your 
> "distance" to them across the internet.  Latancy is denoted 
> as the green/yellow/red bars next to another player's name in the 
> battle.net chat rooms.  
> 
> Someone with just a few green bars next to their name is 
> "close" to you across the internet.  Games played with this 
> people will have less lag, and you will be less likely to be booted 
> from the game.
> 
> Conversely, someone with many red bars next to their name is 
> "far" from you.  Games played with such people will have more 
> lag, and you will be more likely to be dropped from the game.
> 
> Thanks,
> Pat N.
