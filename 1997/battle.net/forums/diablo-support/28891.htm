Kilraw,

The maximum level you can reach for a given spells through books is 
level 15.  This can be difficult if even possible for some characters 
classes, as the Magic Attribute requirement for learning the higher 
levels of spells is very high, and beyond the range of some character 
classes.

As Lancelot points out, however, you can still raise your spells 
levels through the use of the enchanted shrine, though the level 15 
limit is still imposed.

The only exception to the level 15 limit is through the use of items. 
 Certain items, when equipped, will temporarily raise all of your 
spells levels, and this can read beyond level 15.
