From what I have seen the experience points you recieve is calculated 
acording to:

base for monster-(adjustment for monster*your level)

Let's say a monster has a base of 12000 and an adjustment of 500 then 
killing it when being level 20 you get 2000 exp points. When you 
reach level 21 you only gets 1500 exp points and so on. As I already 
said previous in this thread there is also a maximum of 200*your 
level for a monster. So in the above example should you be level 10 
you still only get 2000 exp. points insted of 12000-10*500=7000. You 
also recieve more experience points in harder dificulty levels but I 
have not tried to see if the factor is the same for all monsters (say 
10 more exp. points in nightmare) or if it is different for eaxh 
monster.
