Try these things out in order..

1. Make sure your not running any extra programs before you play 
Diablo. Hit CTRL+ALT+DEL and shut down everything except systray, 
explorer, and point32 if you have it.

2.  delete the bncache.dat file in the Diablo folder and also the 
contents of  your Windows/Temp directory. The data there might be 
corrupted and causing you this trouble.

3. If your still having trouble after step 2 then go to the control 
panel and select Add/Remove programs. Now remove battle.net from the 
list and then try to  run Diablo and connect to battlenet. I suggest 
this last because it changes your account number . It never mattered 
to me what my act # was but some people are picky about changing it.

Good luck and post back if it still acts buggy.    Lordy.
