Lag is found on all ISP's.  Some ISP's are worse than others.  Lag 
depends alot on which place you are trying to reach.

AOL is fine for surfing the web and doing chats with other AOL users, 
but when it comes to internet gaming, AOL is (probably) the worst 
provider you could use.  Changing from AOL is not going to get rid of 
the lag you experience on AOL, but it will GREATLY reduce it.  To get 
better responce on battle.net you should join games with green bars 
and try to play with other AOL users.  It would also help alot if you 
tried to get onto battle.net when AOL isn't so busy (last night, 
early morning).  Hopefully that helps!
