It is a known *feature* of the game. :)

Tech Support is fully aware of this, they say it can be attributed to 
lag, but it can happen in solo games multiplayer games also.  The way 
to keep items from disappearing on you is to only click on the item 
once.  If you click on the item, and it does not get picked up, don't 
click on it again, just wait a little while and it should appear.  
Clicking on the item several times is almost certain to make it 
disappear.
