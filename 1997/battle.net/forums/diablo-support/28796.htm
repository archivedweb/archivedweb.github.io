	Hmmm, lag? I would say the best terms for explanation is the
latency of your system (as a whole...processor/modem speed, ISP speed
and distance from other players ect.). Your 'lag' is expressed in bnet
with colored bars by your name icon. Green=low, Yellow=moderate and
Red=high.

use it complain more about the frustrations of getting the software to
perform the best w/ Diablo, not lag.
