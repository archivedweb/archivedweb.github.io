Sun,

Yes, you do need AOL for Windows 95 if you wish to connect to 
battle.net through AOL.  In order to get this, simply connect to AOL, 
click on the Keyword button, then type UPGRADE.  You will be able to 
download AOL for Win95 from here (the download file is called 
setup95.exe).  Run this file, and AOL for Win95 will be installed on 
your system.

Then, to connect to battle.net:

Connect to AOL using AOL for Win95 (to do this, click on Start--
Programs--America Online--America Online for Windows 95)
Minimize AOL
Run Diablo
Choose Multiplayer
Choose or create your Multiplayer character
Choose battle.net

If you have further questions or are having problems connecting, 
repost here or send email to support@blizzard.com
