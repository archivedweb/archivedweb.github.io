Thanks,

-When you were in Lazarus' room, was there another player nearby?  
Someone may have hit you with one of their fireballs.  This could 
have killed you (fireballs can be pretty nasty), and would be the 
reason you did not lose your gear.  When you are killed by another 
player, you only lose half of your gold and one of your ears (you do 
not lose your equipment).

-Your second problem does sound a bit strange.  On rare occurences, 
when you go to the dungeon via your Town Portal, the dungeon may 
appear different than what you remembered.  If this happens, try 
leaving the dungeon level, and entering it again through the stairs --
 this should fix things.

If your teleport spell malfuntions like you report again, can you 
repost here or send email to support@blizzard.com?  Give us your 
system specifications, and explain the glitch one more time.  I have 
not heard of this problem before, and it was probably just a random 
memory glitch (make sure you are not running any extra programs when 
you play Diablo).  It if happens again, however, please report it 
again -- we'll look into it.

-Lastly, yes, it is possible to find items with prefixes/suffixes 
that are commonly offered by Wirt, though this prefixes/suffixes are 
very rare in the dungeon.  Keep looking on levels 13-16, and you may 
get lucky :)

Thanks,
pat N.
