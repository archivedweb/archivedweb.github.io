Starlight,

Are you talking about the Microsoft TCP/IP patch?  This patch does 
fix a number of OOB attacks that can lock up your network connecting 
(which will keep you from sending/receiving data to/from the 
internet) until you reboot your computer, but it does not stop all of 
them.  People that take advantage of this flaw in MS' TCP/IP 
implementatoin can cause you to be disconnected from battle.net while 
in the chat rooms, and can cause you to be disconnected from a game 
of Diablo.  Microsoft is working on a permanent fix to this, and you 
should keep an eye on http://www.microsoft.com/security for 
information regarding this.

If you have further questions, repost here or send email to 
support@blizzard.com.
