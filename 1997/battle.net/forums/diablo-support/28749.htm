WARGASM is right, you need to make sure you have your display 
properties set to 256 colors before you play Diablo.  If you continue 
to have problems after doing that, set your display properties to 
640x480 and 256 colors before you play Diablo.  Make sure you hit 
apply after you reset your display properties, you will probably have 
to reboot your computer.  This will fix your problem.
