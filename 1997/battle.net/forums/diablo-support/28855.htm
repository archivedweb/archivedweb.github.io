That red screen you are getting trying to use AOL says that Diablo is 
not detecting your connection to the internet.  This can be caused by 
a few different issues, either you are not connected to the internet 
when you try and play Diablo or your version of AOL 95 is not 
installed correctly.  You should make positive and sure that you are 
connected to the internet using AOL 95 before you start Diablo.  If 
you verify you are, you should reinstall AOL for Windows 95 and make 
sure it is installed correctly.  If none of that works I am out of 
ideas, I hope Blizzard or someone else can take you home on this one. 
 You might want to give AOL technical support a try, find out how you 
can verify that your AOL for Win 95 is installed correctly.
