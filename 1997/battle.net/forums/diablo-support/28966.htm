Helper,

Here's a bit of feedback for you concerning connecting games via null 
modem cable.  I know what the book says and what Blizzard Tech 
Support says about requireing a null modem cable when connecting two 
machines together to play a serial game.  Well, here is my personal 
experience and you may want to include it in your FYI file for future 
posts.

I have two computers at home and wanted to play a direct connect 
game.  I read the book and purchased the necessary null modem cable.  
I attempted using a null modem cable and got the same message the 
other people are seeing about not being able to find the game.  I 
removed the null modem cable, went to a standard serial cable, chose 
the dirct connect option and BAAAAAM, had a solid connection between 
my two machines and could game it up for countless hours. To verifiy 
this, I switched the cable back to the null modem cable and ..... NO 
GAME FOUND.  Once I switched back to the plain serial cable, I could 
game it up again.  So, thought I'd share this with you.

Jas
