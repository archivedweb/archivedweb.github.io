Van_Damned,

Using Mindspring as an ISP will give you a fast connection to 
battle.net (as Mindspring hosts a battle.net server).  However, when 
looking for a game to play in, make sure you take the latency you 
have with the other players into account (your latency with the 
others you play with will have a big impact on the lag you might 
experience in the game).  Even if you are using a fast ISP, you will 
still find people on battle.net with whom your latency is high.
