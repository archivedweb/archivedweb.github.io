StrikeZone,

Here are my recommendations as well, and I also agree with Lonara 
Flamebows suggestions.  Do the CD first, if your going to get or link 
up with other peopel then upgrade the modem next, if not then flip a 
coin on the motherboard or video (that's if your already running a 
pci system [video card] [72 pin ram]).

> 486 DX w/ Pentium 89
**Is this motherboard PCI ready? Not familiar with Pent. 89.  I like 
to suggest a new Motherboard but it may require too much upgrade at 
one time. (new video if not pci already, new style ram (edo) if 
running old style ram)
$140 motherboard (SuperMicro)
$150 for a 133mZ cpu chip (intel) or $190 for a 166mZ mmx chip (intel)

> 24 MB RAM
Good ram (72 pin?/edo?)
If have old style ram and go to new motherboard add
$150 for 32 megs edo

> 2x CD-ROM
Upgrade to atleast 12x to make it worth while
$100 depends on vendor (teac, reveal, toshiba...)

> SVGA video card with 1 MB Video RAM
Go with a card that supports directx and atleast 2megs ram onboard
$145 STB Velocity 4 meg card, $140 Number Nine 4meg

> 14.4k modem
upgrade to 33.6 (nice to get a "v everything" but expensive)
$145 56.6 USRobotics internal

The best thing to do is find a good, trustworthy Computer place that 
can suggest the upgrades needed to you and have multiply selection 
for each items needed.  Check the warranty in store and labor in 
store prices (usually 1 year parts 2 years labor).  Becareful when 
buying non-namebrand items.

Good luck on your choices!!!

Revenge
