On Wed Jul 16 18:55, Wolf Jung wrote:
> How do i have to setup Diablo (or the Win95 TCP/IP?) to search for a 
> battle.net-server over the Wingate-proxy (v2.0e)? WWW, FTP, ... are 
> already working with Wingate.

Wolf,

Battle.net does not support proxy servers, though here is the 
information I can give you to help you configure Wingate:

You must map ports 116 and 118 to send/receive TCP packets
You must map port 6112 to send/receive UDP packets
If you must enter a particular domain for the connection, use 
battle.net.

-we do have instructions for configuring MSProxy to connect to 
battle.net, and they may prove useful in configuring Wingate.  For 
these instructions, take a look here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/msproxy.htm

Thanks,
Pat N.
