Rebeldog,

Uinstall and reinstall the game one more time using the instructions 
found here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/reinstall.htm

During this process, make sure you are not running any extra programs 
on your system.  Hold down ctl-alt-del to get to the End Task window, 
and end everything except Explorer and Systray.

You should also make sure that your Diablo folder is not read-only.  
To do this, Use explorer to find your Diablo folder.  Right click on 
your Diablo folder, then select Properties.  Make sure the "read-
only" tab is not checked.
