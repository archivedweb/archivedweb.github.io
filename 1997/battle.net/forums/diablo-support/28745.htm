On Tue Jul 15 10:17, VIPER THE SLAYER wrote:
> I am unable to connect to battl.net throught the game because it says 
> "you are not configured for network dialup" how do i get onto 
> battle.net then?

First step if to verify your version of AOL.  Bring up AOL and go to 
help and then about.  It should say AOL for Windows 95.  If it says 
AOL for Windows, then you need to sign onto AOL and go to keyword 
UPGRADE and download the setup95.exe program.  Double click on this 
to run the setup program.  Once you have installed this, you need to 
make sure you run the correct version of AOL (the old version is 
still on your computer).

To get onto battle.net, sign onto AOL and minimize the window.  Bring 
up Diablo and select Multi-Player, a characters then battle.net.  
That's it! Your on!  Happy hunting!
