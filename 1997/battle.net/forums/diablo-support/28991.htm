Nathan,
As long as you have a 32bit winsock compliant connection to the 
internet you need only do the following to play on Battle.net:
1) connect to the internet
2) Start Diablo
3) Select Multiplayer
4) Create/Select your character
5) Select Battle.net from the list of connection methods.

    You should then be connected to the battle.net where you can 
chat, play games, etc.
