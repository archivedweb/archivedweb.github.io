Seeker,

I know you have uninstalled and reinstalled many times now, please do 
it one more time for me please.  Do it just like this, do not skip a 
step.

Go to My Computer - Control Panel - Add/Remove Programs - Diablo and 
remove.  When prompted, make sure the also delete saved games box is 
clear.

Now choose Battle.net and remove.

Go to your Diablo folder and delete everything except for your single 
player save game file.  If you downloaded the DRTL104.exe patch from 
Blizzard, delete it.

Go to your Windows Temp folder and delete everything in there.

Restart your computer

Do a scandisk, then a *full* harddisk defragment.

Reinstall Diablo, put the CD in the drive and install.  Exit as soon 
as you have installed Diablo, do not and I repeat do not play a 
Diablo game yet, as soon as you get to the first screen exit Diablo.  
If you play Diablo before you apply the patch you invalidate 
everything I have said so far.

Download the patch again from the Blizzard Web Site, make sure you 
download that patch into your Diablo folder, do not let Battle.net do 
the upgrade for you.

http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/patch.htm

Double click the patch to run it.

If you are still having problems at this point, I do not know what 
else to say except that either you are missing a step, I am missing a 
step, or your problem is deeper rooted than normal.  If you find that 
after trying this it does not work, there is only one more thing to 
do and you will not like it.

Hope this helps,

The Helper
