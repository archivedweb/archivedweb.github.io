Gaidan,

If you connect to battle.net and are not using version 1.04 of 
Diablo, then your version will automatically be udpated for you.  To 
connect to battle.net:

Connect to your ISP
Run Diablo
choose multiplayer
choose or create your multiplayer character
choose battle.net as the mode of multiplayer game.

You will then be connected to battle.net (if necessary, your version 
of Diablo will be updated), and be placed in one of the battle.net 
chat channels.  For further help at this point, hit the <F1> 
key.

If you are having trouble updating your version of Diablo by 
connecting to battle.net, then you can download the v1.04 patch from 
our web site here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/patch.htm
download the file drtl104.exe into your Diablo folder, then run it to 
update your version.
