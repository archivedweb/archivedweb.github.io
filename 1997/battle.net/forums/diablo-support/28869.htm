Aceman,

The email I sent you described reinstalling Diablo, then, one by one, 
adding your multiplayer characters to see if one or more of them was 
causing the crash.  This appears to be the case, unfortunately.  
There is no way to fix a character that has been corrupted like this.

Did you try adding your multiplayer characters one at a time?  It 
could be that only one is causing the crash and, in this case, you 
would only have to lose the one character.  Add one of your 
characters, and see if the game crashes.  If it does not, then remove 
that character (saving it for later) and try another one.  Test each 
character in this way, making sure to remove characters from the 
previous test.  This way, you should be able to tell which characters 
are the rotten apples.  

After you know which characters are good, restore those and you 
should be able to play with them.

Thanks,
