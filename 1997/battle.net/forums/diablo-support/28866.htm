If you are troubleshooting and you get a page fault, it is necessary 
for you to delete the file BNCACHE.dat from your Diablo folder, also 
delete the contents of your Windows Temp directory, and run a 
scandisk on your hard drive.  The page fault can corrupt these temp 
areas, and even make mess up some clusters on your disk, you will not 
see valid test results unless you remove/reinitialize these temp 
areas.  If you got a page fault trying something, then tried 
something else, you might of fixed the problem but not known because 
the first page fault left old or damaged data in the temp areas, the 
result being another page fault because of that damaged data and not 
from the unknown culprit you are looking for.  Just remember if you 
are trying things that if you get a page fault, to make sure your 
system is clean for the next test.
