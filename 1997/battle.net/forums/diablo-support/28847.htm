Ok guys,
   The higher difficulty you go, the higher _base_ quality the item 
is.  The stronger magical enhancements come with depth, not 
difficulty.  Example:  You may find more rings in the church, you may 
find plate as opposed to splint mail in the church or cats, or you 
may find a crown as opposed to a cap.  HOWEVER, these are the base 
quality only.  The magical enhancements will still suck on lower 
levels.  (of the stars, sharp, steel, etc)  FYI, I am playing on ver. 
1.0.  If you want good base items (bastard swords or full plate, etc) 
then you have to go high difficulty, and if you want powerful magic 
(kings, awesome/godly, obsidian, zodiac, etc) then you have to go 
DEEP.  The characters who can make repeated Hell/Hell Laz runs are 
the ones who come out with the awesome items.
