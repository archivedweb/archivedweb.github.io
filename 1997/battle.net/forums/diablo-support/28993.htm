KingJack,

Are you having sound problems when you play Diablo?  Your audio 
drivers do not have to DirectX certied in order to be compatible with 
DirectX games.

If you are having sound problems, take a look here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/directx.htm
for a list of links to updated DirectX compatible drivers for a 
number of devices.  If you own one of these sound cards, follow the 
link provided.  Make sure you read any instructions included with the 
driver download, or on the download page.

If you have further questions, repost here or send email to 
support@blizzard.com.  Give us your system specifications (including 
sound card), describe the problem you are having, and tell us what 
you have done to try to fix things.
