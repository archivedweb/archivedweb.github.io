KH,

You need to make sure you are using updated drivers for your sound 
and video cards.  Take a look here:
http://www.blizzard.com/support/diablo/directx.htm
for a list of links to various sound and video drivers.  If you have 
one of these devices, follow the link provided.  This will take you 
to either updated drivers, or to a fix you can do yourself that will 
get Diablo to work.

If you have further problems, repost here or send email to 
support@blizzard.com.  Give us your system specifications (including 
sound and video cards), describe the problem you are experiencing 
(including any error message you receive), and tell us what you have 
done to try to fix things.
