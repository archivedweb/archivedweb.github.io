Mike,

You should contact Avance to see if they have updated DirectX drivers 
for your sound card.  If you are using the drivers that came with the 
card, then there is a good chance that they are not the most updated. 
 Avance's home page can be found here:
http://www.avance.com

You should also try turning down your sound and music to see if the 
popping goes away.  When in the game, hit <esc>, then select 
options. Turn your music and sound to a lower setting and see if that 
helps.
