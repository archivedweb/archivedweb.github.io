You won't be able to find some of the books, no matter how much cash 
you have:

Bloodboil, Blood Ritual and Etherealize do not exist in the retail 
version of the game.

Apocalypse, Nova and Resurrect can only occur as scrolls or staff 
charges, not as books.

Identify appears only as a scroll.

You'll probably have to play in the Caves or lower to find Guardian, 
Bone Spirit, Bloodstar and Golem books. You can find most of the 
others in the Catacombs or lower. And, of course, visit Adria the 
Witch a lot.

Finally, this is the worst forum to post a request like this--check 
Ogden's Tavern or the Trading Post channel on b.net if you feel you 
*must* buy the books.
