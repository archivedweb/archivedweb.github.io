Kain,

The Helper has given you some good advice:

The problem you are having connecting to battle.net can probably be 
linked to your winsock.  If it is not 32bit, then you will not be 
able to connect, and you will receive the message you are receiving 
when you try.

If you are having problems, you should contact the system 
administrator of your network, and ask them how you can configure 
your system so that it supports 32bit internet connections (you 
should also ask them if they have the ports specified by the Helper 
open).  They should be able to help you with this.
