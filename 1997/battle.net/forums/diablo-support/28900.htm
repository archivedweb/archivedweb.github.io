Lewis,

None of the Diablo patches make changes to the item generation 
schemes.  If you find this not to be the case, repost here or send 
email to support@blizzard.com.

As St. Michael mentioned (and it appears that you agree), the quality 
of the item you find in the dungeon is dependent on the level of the 
dungeon you are exploring, while the quality of the items you can 
purchase in town is dependent on the level of your character.
