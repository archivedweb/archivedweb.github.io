Eugene,

You're brought up some compelling arguments, though I'll try to 
answer this one as best I can:

-It is true that there is a price maximum on the items you can 
purchase in town (this keeps one from not being able to carry enough 
gold to actually purchase an item offered), and I honestly don't know 
the price of the Godly Plate of the whale (though this would differ 
depending on the type of plate).

-Although you and your friends have never found items containing a 
group of suffixes or prefixes, this does not mean that they are not 
obtainable in the dungeon -- it merely means that they are VERY rare. 
 Hypothically, if the chance of a given creature dropping a Godly 
item in levels 13-16 of the dungeon in a multiplayer game of Diablo 
is 1 in 20,000(please allow me this bit of hand-waving for the sake 
of argument), then it would be understandable that such an item has 
not been found by you or your friends.

So, to answer your question, Godly plate of the whales is attainable 
in the multiplayer game of Diablo, though the rarity of such an item, 
and the fact that such armor has been heavily duped, would cause its 
possession by a player to be suspect.
