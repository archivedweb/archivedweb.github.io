Hanks,

When you game freezes, does your sound starting looping, or does 
everthing just stop?

If your sound is looping, then check to see if your CD-ROM or hard 
drive is running in DOS compatibility mode:

To see if your CD-ROM or hard drive is running in compatibility mode:

Click on Start -- Settings -- Control Panel
Open System
Select the Performance tab

Win95 will tell you here if you CD-ROM or hard drive is running in 
compatibility mode.

If it is, look at:
CD-ROM
http://www.microsoft.com/KB/ARTICLES/Q151/6/34.HTM

Hard drive
http://www.microsoft.com/KB/ARTICLES/Q130/1/79.HTM

For information regarding obtaining the correct drivers for your 
devices.


If this does not seem to be the case, then repost here or send email 
to support@blizzard.com.  Give us your system specifications 
(including sound and video cards), describe the problem one more 
time, and tell us what you have done to try to fix things.

Thanks,
