He wears the undead crown.  Everytime he hits a player, it converts 
the damage it does into life points and adds it to the King's total.  
So, theoretically, King Leoric could stand there and fight forever, 
as long as he keeps hitting characters for roughly the same amount of 
damage they are doing to him.  It's very similar to using a sword of 
blood.  

Best strategy:  Stone Curse.  Adria usually sells the scrolls until 
you can find a book lower in the dungeon.
