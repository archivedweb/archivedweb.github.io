Eaglehorn is an excellent bow.  I use it most often, even in 
Nightmare /Hell and Hell/Hell.

I also have Windforce, which is also excellent, partly because it 
just does more damage than Eaglehorn and it knocks-back monsters.

I just finished Hell/Hell the other night.  I used Windforce most of 
the time, and it's knock-back feature allowed me to stay alive when 
fighting the Steel Lords on 16.  If I was using the Eaglehorn, I 
would have died.

However, I died once when fighting Diablo, because he would get 
knocked back and then hit me again with apocalypse.  So, after I 
restarted in town, I switched to the Eaglehorn and went back down 
after Diablo.

The Eaglehorn worked great!!  I was able to hit Diablo repeatedly 
without him even getting a chance to hit me back once.  He just 
stayed in one spot as arrow after arrow sank home, quivering with 
each hit.

I then developed a new respect for my Eaglehorn--it may not do the 
damage of some bows, but the monsters can't hit you if you're hitting 
them, and the Eaglehorn does the job, even if you have to fire a few 
more arrows than the Windforce.
