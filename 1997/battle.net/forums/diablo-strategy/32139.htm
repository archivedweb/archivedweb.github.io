Do you have MS Word installed on your machine. If you do, look in 
your Startup group. If it contains Microsoft Fast Find, that's what 
is causing the problem. Microsoft Fast Find re-index's your hard 
drive every so often, probably once an hour, or what ever you have it 
set to. 

You should end that task before you start Diablo, or just take it out 
of the Startup group and only run it when you need it.
