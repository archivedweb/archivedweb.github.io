> sell stuff to get bows first (duh), mages should sell things to get

An ax does preclude the use of a shield, but I’ve had a Brutal Ax of 
Speed that was not slow and dealt death at a fantastic rate.  It was 
great against the Butcher and Leoric, no need for Stone Curse because 
they were held in stun lock because of the speed.

Since AC seems to be an issue for almost everyone, more is always 
better, most people forgo them.  My biggest complaint is that the 
game does offer the variety of axes, from a prefix/suffix stand 
point, as it does with swords.  For instance, I’ve never seen a 
Soldiers Ax of the Mind, or Glorious Ax of Haste, etc.  I think more 
people would use them if the offered advantages to justify the loss 
of AC.
