     In my opinion the best 3 player game for nightmare/hell and some 
hell/hell is 2  rouges and a warrior.  Two lvl 30 rogues could do a 
pretty good job when compared to a lvl 25 mage.  When it came down to 
having a warrior or a mage in this 3 person group the warrior won 
because of one simple fact, I feel alot safer when I have a warrior 
that I can retreat behind when things get too hot, i.e. Hords of Doom 
Knights, Blood Knights and the like.  Sorry mages :)

    As for a 4 player game (same types as above) I DON'T like 2 
Rouges, 1 mage, 1 warrior  Because of the awesome destruction caused 
by arrows and magic it is harder for the warrior to become involved 
since he is not a ranged attacker.  When he does become involved it 
hinders the others from their full potential.

    As for whit I LIKE (same types as above) I am torn between;
a) 3 Rouges, 1mage
Great combo for harder levels. The amount of arrows here is         
increadible and with a mage to boot well.... need I say more?

b) 2 Rouges, 2mages
This I think is also a good choice, great amount of arrows and        
 plentiful amounts of magic, especially Stone Curse and Golem.

c) 1 Rogue, 1 mage, 2 warriors
I think this is the best all around combo.  With only 2 ranged        
 attackers the warriors can move about more freely and thus         
become more involved. It is eaiser for the rogue to adjust her        
     shots around the warrior than it is for the warrior to move 
around          her true/stray shots.  Why? Because the arrows travel 
faster,             than the warrior can move.  It is hard to fight & 
dodge arrows at the      same time.
