3 of my personal characters:

1)Shrike - lvl38 sorc:
HATES illusion weavers & acid spitters. Once you're surrounded, 
you're dead.
LOVES anything else (a high lvl sorc seems to be able to take out 
anything with the right spells, esp witches - good target practice 
for fireball. :)

2)Widow - lvl30 rogue:
HATES horned demons & acid spitters.
LOVES illusion weavers, witches, mages. Easy stun kills. :)

3)Grifter - lvl29 warrior:
HATES archers, witches, mages. Too much effort running around chasing 
them.
LOVES all close combat fighters, esp knights. :)

just a note, acid spitter bosses and their minions do not chase you. 
Rather, they simply stand at the farthest range to spit at you. A 
simple way to get rid of them is to stand just out of range and dump 
a few firewalls or launch your arrows. Simple. The normal spitters 
are worse.

happy huntin'
Shrike aka Widow aka Grifter
