Which monster do you hate the worst.  I'm not looking for the 
toughest monster in the game just which one to you hate fighting 
because no matter what you do or how strong you get they just seem to 
find a way to cause you problems.

For Merrilee it's those darn acid spitting runts.  They just seem to 
find a way to get that darn acid at your feet.  I don't know how many 
times an easy fight turned into a life threatening situation when one 
of the little pip squeeks dropped that acid at my feet in hell and I 
didn't realize it until my mana shield was gone and most of my hp 
with it.  I try to kill the runts as soon as I see them but they 
still get the acid at my feet.  If you run into one of the named ones 
and their pack forget it.  No matter what your level or what mode 
your in your only choice is to retreat and take them out at range (a 
tedious process when you can't see them) or slog it out and take a 
lot of damage.

My favorite monster is those big dumb metal knights.  Sure they do 
some damage but get a good AC and a good to hit and they just walk 
straight into the shooting gallery for the rogue.

Let me know your least and most favorite monsters.

Merrilee (level 36 rogue)
