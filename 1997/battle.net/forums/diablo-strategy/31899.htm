In hell, resistance is very important.  You want to have Max to magic 
and fire.  A good way to take care of witches and advocates is to use 
firewall.  With full resistance, you can take several hits so try to 
get a few monsters lined up and whip out a firewall or three.  On 
Lazerus, if you go into his area and run out, the two boss witches 
will follow you out.  I try to pin them in a corner and slash away.  
BTW, make sure you kill all of the normal monsters in the area first -
 nothing worse than being chased by Laz witches only to run into a 
group of soul burners.

For the advocates in the Laz area, simply go right below the opening 
to Laz and cast as many firewalls as you can to cover the entire area 
in flames.  After the firewalls die out, go in and take care of what 
ever remains.

Good Luck - it will be tough to do at your level but it can be done.
