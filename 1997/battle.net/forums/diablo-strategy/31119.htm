Darkwind,

If you have phasing, try using that, to get behind the warrior and 
maybe you can get a few hits w/ your bow in on him, before you have 
to move again. 

In looking at his weapons/armor/ and rings, i might take a guess that 
he's not resisting anything that much.  Try using Fireballs, 
Inferno's, or Lighting (or your powerfulest spell that you have)


Use a mana shield.
Use Stone Curse and you can get in a few good hits.
If you happen to have a Windforce use that, the knock back feature 
would keep him off your ass for a second or two.
Keep your belt full of healing & mana potions.


Hope this helps you,
