Yeah these are bosses (kind of).  With one exception that I have 
seen, they all look like the monsters they are surrounded by except 
they are different colored and brighter with their own light source.  
If you run into a bunch of one enemy on a level, chances are there 
will be a boss of that type somewhere too.  They always throw out 
either unique or at the least enchanted items.

As of yet, I don't know of a page anywhere that has made a list of 
all of the bosses.  Therefore I decided (since I have a lot of time 
on my hands here) I am going to try and create one.  So those of you 
with GOOD CLEAR screenshots, preferably with the monster selected so 
you get the gold writing in the status box, let me know.
