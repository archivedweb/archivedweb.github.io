Move slowly and activate as few monsters as possible. Then try to 
retreat
back to an area where you have room to fight (hit-and-run style).

The caves presents lots of opportunity to use terrain to your 
advantage - 
the lava streams, pens, and gates.

Try using the gold bow of shock. Usually, monsters that use fire-based
attacks (lava lords) are vunerable to lighting attacks.

If you have the mana shield spell, use it. It's like doubling your 
hit points.
Adria sometimes sell the scrolls.

Hope this helps.
