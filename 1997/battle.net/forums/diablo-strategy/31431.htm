Well let's see.  I know that the better equipment doesn't appear 
until you're higher up.  I found my first zodiac item last week in 
lvl 16 of normal/hell.  Sapphire ring of the zodiac, +54% resist 
lightning and +18 to all.  It's the only zodiac I've seen yet.  I was 
at level 33 or 34, not sure which.  A few days later I found my first 
obsidian item, followed the next game/day by two more. (see post 
below, "what a haul").  When I leveled up from whatever I was 
(so now I'm either 34 or 35), I immediately started seeing king's 
stuff EVERYWHERE.  Also detailed in the afforementioned post.  I've 
noticed though that there is always a preview of what is to come.  
Adria offers prefixes before either wirt or gris.  For me, a warrior, 
it's junk, but it let's you know you're close.  I don't think you've 
played your characters quite high enough (you're right on the edge) 
to find these things.  What I do to item hunt it s start a normal 
game and clear hell all the way down to diablo and whatever is in the 
room with him.  I somtimes as they say feel lucky, and go ahead and 
set up a portal, then draw out as many knights as I can.  If I trip 
diablo I just head to town through the portal.  Make sure you know 
what you're doing though, because if you die with him lose, kiss that 
stuff goodbye.  If you kill him, you lose it, and he will kill a 
naked player so fast you can't possibly get anything back.  Or so I 
believe.
