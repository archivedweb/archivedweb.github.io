I'm myself a level 35 rogue, with all stats maxed.

The adavantages of dex are:
- increasing your chance to hit (1% every 2 points)
- increasing your AC (1 point every 5 points)
- increasing your missile firing speed

To respond to your question about dodging, no u won't be able to 
dodge everything  that comes toward you. You also need a good armor, 
so a goog strength stat (a least 90).
