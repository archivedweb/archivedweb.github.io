Ok, let me address this point. The mage should not throw guardians in 
front of him, between the monsters and the party, at least in a 
multiplayer game. This is because warriors and some rogues will 
charge right into the mess of monsters and kill them, all the while 
they are getting hit in the butt by guardian. Try casting guardians 
in the midst of advancing monsters or behind monsters that are lined 
up  fighting the frontliners. It avoids that friendly fire problem.

Guardian is also really great against the non-immune (and I mean just 
non-immune, resist it works too) teleporters. The wizards are kept 
busy teleporting to fire.
