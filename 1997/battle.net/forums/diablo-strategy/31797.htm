> Hey - I just got Diablo a couple days ago and I was wondering what 
> everyone thought the best way to play a sorceror was. I've been 
> concentrating on getting his strength and dextarity up..but some

My suggestion to you would be to put every point you earn through 
leveling up into magic. You don't need dex at all and in the lower 
levels you can find equipment to give you some extra HP(so you don't 
really need vit.). As for str find equipment again that will boost 
your str so that you can wear the armor you want. Magic to a mage is 
power put every point you earn there. As soon as you get into the 
caves or reach level 25 your other stats will come up nicely and str 
will max out long before your magic does through the use of found or 
bought elixers. Oh and while I'm on my soapbox stay away from random 
shrines, 1/10 of your mana is a terrible thing to waste.
