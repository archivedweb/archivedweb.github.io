Here's something that i got from the tips pages from 
diablo.scorched.com. A neat trick is to lure the big guy out and let 
him chase you around the stairs to the next level. Eventually he will 
get stuck in the stairs while u stand on the other side. Then just 
cast fire wall, and toast his ass. I've used it a couple of times and 
i found that it can be quite dangerous if u don't have a good lead on 
him though, because u run at about the same rate he does.
