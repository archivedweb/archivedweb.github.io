I think I can help a little.  But first, What do you mean by 
sacrificing the +30 to use the dreamflange?  That "stupid" 
mace gives a total of +110 mana points! +30 to magic AND +50 to mana. 
AND you get to use a shield = better AC! Plus +1 to spell levels!  
Thats better than just about any staff (unless its a Dragons staff of 
Wiz - or an AA staff). 

As far as the CL spell goes, I have noticed that the listed damage in 
the spell book does not really represent what the spell can do.  With 
fireball, you know that one fireball is X damage.  But, since CL is a 
flow of lightning in several directions at once, its hard to say what 
damage is actually done.  I think that you have to make sure that 
what ever you are attacking is within the stream when you cast.  Soul 
Burners, Balrogs, and lava maws are difficult to kill because they 
move alot, and the CL cant keep up with them. I have level 19 and 20 
spells (dreamflange and AA staff), and either way, CL sucks down the 
mana.  Thats why hell levels with Soul Burners suck so much.

As far as triple immunes go - make sure that you have plenty of mana 
potions, because you have to use the stone curse like CRAZY!(another 
spell that sucks down the mana).  You also have to be very quick with 
the teleport, because the last thing you want is to be swarmed.  In 
conjuction with stone curse, use Golem to whack the bastards when 
they can't move.  You will then need a good melee weapon that does 
good damage to hit them while stoned.  A kings sword of whatever is 
usually good (I agree that the 'Flange is not a good weapon for 
triple immunes - not enough damage).  If you are using a melee 
weapon, then it makes sense to use a shield - hence - the dreamflange 
/ stormshield combo. Lastly, DONT BE AFRAID TO RUN!
