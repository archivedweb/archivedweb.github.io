Now.. i've played with all the classes(although my rogue and wizard 
are still low levels), and have been in games with all classes. and 
the following is my opion of the classes.

The all around easiest to play is the rogue. Its a welll rounded 
fighter, skilling in both range attacks and close up. she's good for 
almost any situation.

the warrior. My personal favorite. very easy to play on the lower 
levels of the game(normal, and into the caves on noghtmare), but then 
it becomes much more difficult to play with him. In the higher levels 
of nightmare and hell (game settings), it becomes deadly to fight 
some of the monsters up close. The range attack is essential for 
playing here, but the warrior sucks with it(in most cases).
If you die playing solo, forget it. You'll be playing for a good15 
minutes up to 1.5 hours trying to get your stuff back, from a large 
pack of archers(i did this last night.. got caught with a good 30 
archers... 20 were on the screen.. I couldn't walk in the room 
without dieing, or going through 4 potions instantly)

The wizzard. The exact opposite of the warrior. Difficult to manage 
through the lower levels because of the lack of spells. But is ggreat 
in the higher levels with his powerful range attacks. Even if he dies 
he can usually get all his stuff back with a couple trips back down.

With all this in mind, i choose the warrior as the best class. Why? 
because as the game goes on its harder for him to play and strategy 
becomes much more important.
