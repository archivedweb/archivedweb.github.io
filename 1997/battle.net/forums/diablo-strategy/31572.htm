I have been playing Diablo for a long time (acct #30415), and I must 
say the best times I've had playing the game were with some friends 
who were newbies with me and have adventured with me ever since the 
beginning.  My fighter, Zealot, is a completely legit level 43 right 
now, and I still travel with the people I started out with.

My advice is not to challenge cheating...it's going to happen, and 
you can effectively isolate yourself from it.  There are still some 
legit players out there.  Hacking and cheating is fun in its own way 
for some people, and it's not my place to judge what is right and 
wrong for others, only myself.  Truth is, I have some non-legit 
characters too, and I have fun with them as well.  I must say that 
Zealot is my favorite though...he is a symbol of hard work and 
accomplishment for me that could never be replaced by any of my 
hacked characters.
