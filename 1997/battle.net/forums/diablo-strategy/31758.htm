I have found two good bows for my Rogue. I dont use windforce because 
the knock back is not good sometimes.  I have an Emerald bow of 
Heavens  and  a Merciless bow of Heavens. Both are long war bows that 
require 80 dex. to use. They hit just as hard as Windforce. I use the 
Emerald because of its resist all.  

The only disadvantage is they cost >$10,000 to repair when down 
to 10 durability. 

For my Sorcerer I bought a Drakes staff of wizardry from the witch. 
For low level sorcerers sometimes she has AA staff of fireball. I 
never let anything get close enough to hit them with the staff. My 
damage is only 30. Fireball and chain lightning work well.  Another 
good staff is Mindcry it has 60 Guardian charges. I find it a lot.
