Another excellent post Lok! Good strategy and very well written.

One thing you wrote is not necessarily true under all circumstances.
> If you are in a hall and balrog are chasing you, 
> lead them into a room and find your corner to chain them.

This would be true if you were going to lose too many of the bolts 
into the wall because your chain was targeting unseen monsters in the 
next room. You would be much better to chain them in the hall, if you 
could backtrack down the hall so that the only monsters in targeting 
range are the balrogs following you. Instead of having your Chain fan 
out in a 90 degree wedge like you would in the room, the balrogs 
would be lined up and could each end up taking damage from all the 
bolts. This would actually be safer, faster and use far less mana.

In your description, the area to the south has not been cleared. In 
that case coming from 1 and teleporting to 3 would be the best 
method. 

On the other hand, if the south had been cleared, coming from 1, 
going to p to wake up the monsters and having them follow you back to 
1, would be the ideal place to chain them.

Going into the corner of the room is the best method when you first 
start a level and haven't cleared much yet. Later when you can set up 
a killing zone in a hallway, you would be amazed at how few times you 
have to cast chain to kill everything following you.
