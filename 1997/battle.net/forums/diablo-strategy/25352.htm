So, you think it's over heh? I don't think so. Maybe you and the 
people you have played with in the past have started to get bored 
with the game, but have you looked at how many newbies there are out 
there? I have been playing since the beginning, I also at one point 
tired of the game and hung it up, but I didn't stay away for long.
I think cheating might make the game fun for some, but they quickly 
get bored (who wouldn't if nothing in the game is a challenge). Try 
playing a different class of character, while you might see the same 
items over and over again, each class works best with different 
stuff, so it's not quite the same. This game is extremely fun when 
not taken to the point of overdose. Playing less often makes the 
times you do play much more enjoyable.
