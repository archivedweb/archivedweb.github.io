> > #). Is it Cheating to accept Gold from another player?
> 
> No, if the gold the other player obtained is legit. If they just

> fine.

> 
> 
> > #). Is it Cheating to accept Items from a higher char?
> 
> No, to a point. No level 1 character should have, say an Obsidian 
> Ring of the Zodiac.  If you are level 1, you should only accept items 
> you would be able to 1) use and 2) obtain within the next say, 5 - 7 
> levels above your current level.  If you accept items that are 
> clearly well beyond your limits at the moment, then you essentially 
> are not playing the game for what it is worth. There isn't much point 
> if you are level 5 and screaming through the dungeons not bothering 
> being attacked by anything and you can go to hell and back without 
> being hurt. Yea, that was a bit of an exaggeration but I made my 
> point.

I think any items can be accepted as long as they are legitimate.

> 
> 
> > #). Is it Cheating to have higher level chars buy items for you?
> 
> No, not if you can pay for it legitimately and use the item

> game. Not to contradict my previous statements, you are NOT accepting

> game" to stick with what I previously said and only have the

I agree.

I agree, but I think that if a level 1 went down to hell and

> > #). Is it Cheating to Teleport in town?
> 
> 
> You accomplish nothing by doing it...so no. I didn't even realize you

cheating.
