Yes, magic is the most important attribute for a sorceror.
It determines how much mana you have. The more mana you have,
the more spells you can cast. You also need magic to read
spell books. Each spell book you read increases the level of
that spell by one. As the levels increase, more magic is
required to read the books.

The least important attribute for a sorcerer is vitality
because once you get the mana shield spell, your mana is like
your hit points. Just remember to use it.

My recommendation is put everything into magic for the first
few levels. Once you start finding good armor that requires 25
or 30 strength to wear, put everything into strength until you
can wear the armor. After that, continue putting everything
into magic.
