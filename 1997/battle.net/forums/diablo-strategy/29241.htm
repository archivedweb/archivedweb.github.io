While there is no clear cut answer to which class is best, I think 
the Rogue is by far the funnest to play. I have played Warriors and 
Mages and while the Mage was OK (I hated the warrior), the Rogue is 
SOOOO flexible and fun to be with. 

Anyway I ABSOLUTELY feel the Rogue can tackle HELL HELL solo easier 
than any other char class. With a high end bow such as Windforce or 
EagleHorn (or Merc Heavens), everything takes damage with every 
arrow. Resistance/Immunity isn't even a concideration. If the Rogue 
has reasonable Mana (I have 390 mana and 375 HP on my Rogue) she can 
stone curse several Hell Spawn and kill them all quite fast. Using 
arrows as "feelers" will save your neck more than anyone 
would believe.

In Hell Hell, one of my WORST experiences was with 2 High end mages. 
the one came out of the stairs at level 14 and started 
throwing Chain Lighting around. This only attracted every creature in 
the level to our location. The 2 mages died and I came as close to 
death is possible before teleporting half a screen back to the 
stairs. It was a miserable experience.

I have played Hell Hell with 3 other rogues who were very experienced 
and let me tell you, it becomes nearly a race to laz as levels are 
quickly cleared. Oh sure, there is always an errant arrow or two, but 
then again, I have been hit nearly as many times from errant 
fireballs from Mages (I have also been accidently wacked by a warrior 
holding shift down also :(. I have also tagged along with 3 mages in 
Hell Hell.... they seemed to have a much harder time at it once we 
got to the immune creatures (they finally resulted in stoning 
everything and me taking out the chicks).

It all comes down to style and how you want to play. Diablo is a 
great game and allows the flexibility for all char types to co-exist.
Diablo has more than 1 char class so everyone can try other char 
types. I'm not saying the Rogue is the best for everyone, but I sure 
like her.
