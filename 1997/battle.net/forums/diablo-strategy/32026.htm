Here's another useful thing to do with Telekinesis.  This is for when 
you are geting tired of having to kill all of the knights and 
advocates in order to get any *good* stuff they drop in Level 16.  
Get yourself an Apocalypse staff - mine is a bountiful one (30 
charges).  When you get to level 16, travel around Diablo's room and 
the room with the two switches (without opening them) and apoc the 
baddies inside.  Pick up anything that you can put your cursor on 
with Telekinesis.  Works great.  Takes much less time to find out if 
they have any decent stuff.
