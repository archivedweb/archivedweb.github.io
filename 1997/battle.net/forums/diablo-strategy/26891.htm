As I stated in my post, there are some people who view using hand me 
downs as cheating.  Whether I agree with this or not is not pertinent 
to the question.  The question was whether the level 18 warrior 
cheated or not, and I was just stating that to some people, it is 
cheating.

Nevertheless, there are two distinctions of cheating.

1. Manipulating the game outside the boundaries to produce an outcome 
that is not intended.

2. Manipulating the game to limit the full enjoyment of the game.  
Thus, "you cheat yourself".

Ultimately, you spend $50 for the game, so only #2 matters.  
Therefore, only each persons' philosophy on cheating matters.  If 
you, Nate, use hand-me-downs, and still enjoy the game fully, who am 
I to call that cheating.  BUT, likewise, if someone does this to 
deprive others of their full enjoyment of the game, this can be 
considered cheating.

Case in point.
Bob uses 2 O.R.Z, 1 O.A.Z, GPOW, KSOH, etc... that his level 50 rogue 
found legit, and has fun with the game.  NO PROB.

Case two
Bob gets bored with the game, and starts PKilling.  The victims have 
no chance of winning, without cheating themselves, and are forced to 
abandon a game where they were almost about to kill the Butcher, or 
Lazarus, or whatever...  Cheating.

I really try not to point fingers.  I don't care what others do, as 
long as they leave me alone.
