I think the central problem most people have with Diablo is that 
there are few ethical gamers in open bnet. To me it stinks just as 
much to be offed with your low level guy by some high level guy 
decked out in the games best legit stuff than to be offed by a 
cheater. In both cases you don't stand much of a chance. You are just 
bully fodder. 

The question in this instance goes beyond cheating or legit to the 
simple matter of ethical gaming. An ethical gamer plays a game not 
only to have fun for himself, but also to enhance the enjoyments 
others get from the game. Ethical gamers do not derive pleasure at 
the expense of others. Perhaps it is the anonymous nature of internet 
play that facilitates unethical play. Its easy to be an ass to 
someone you are not sitting across the table and facing.

So for me the real annoyance is the unethical player, be they legit 
or cheater. Someone failed to teach these people simple consideration 
for others, and they are the ones making a mess of open bnet. PK is 
fine, but give the target a sporting chance.
