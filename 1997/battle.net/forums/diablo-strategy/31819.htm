All the other tips are good. I'd like to clear up something
about the lighting and chain lighting spell.

The damage listed for these spells are much lower than the
damage listed for fireball. But the lighting spells work
differently.

Fireball does full damage to the targets in the center of the
blast and does less (half damage i think) to targets at the
perimeter of the blast. I think The blast area also increases
as the level of the spell increases.

The damage done by lighting is per segment of the lighting that
hits the target. The number of segments increases as the level
of the spell increases (longer strand of lighting). Lighting
damages all targets in it's path, not just the first target; so
try to line up the baddies before you zap away.

With chain lighting, you get one strand per level. CL will target
monsters off the screen and behind walls (monsters behind walls
will not take damage). This means you will activate most monsters
in the vicinity when you use CL. So be prepared for them.

I love to use CL when I'm solo, nothing clears an area faster.
But CL is a bad thing to use when you're playing with others for
they can get fried also.

If you think CL is expensive, wait till you use the stone curse/
golem combo to take care of the immune-alls.

Hope this clears things up.
