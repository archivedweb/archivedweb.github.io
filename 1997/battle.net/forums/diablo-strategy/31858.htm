My favorites as a level 39 warrior:

Weapon/Warrior:  I have "two" favorites at this point.  I 
like using the Grandfather when I am just looking for pure damage, 
however, I find myself using the King's Sword of Speed that I found.  
I am sure the the KSOH would be even better, so I have to say that 
for the warrior, a non-unique is the way to go.

Armor/Warrior:  I have never used any unique armor for the warrior.  
In my opinion, AC is the only way to go here.  I use Awesome Full 
Plate.  I have yet to find any Godly plate, but am hoping...

Shield/Warrior:  I prefer to use Stormshield.  I like the AC and the 
fastest hit recovery.

Other/Warrior:  I go for the resistances here.  I am currently using 
a obs. amulet of the stars, emerald ring of the titans, and can't 
remember the other at this point...
