I guess for a high-level mage like yourself, the Puzzler is probably 
not such a big deal, because you've probably got a lvl 15 Teleport 
spell.  But for rogues and warriors, Teleport doesn't get any higher 
than lvl 4 or 5, so the Puzzler really helps out--though I guess a 
Bountiful Staff of Teleport would also be cool.

I agree that the magic boost isn't a lot, and a staff of Wiz +30 
would be better, but the +20 is sufficient for me (with 2 Rings of 
Wiz +28 and amulet of Wiz +27 and helm & mail of Sorc +20 both) is 
just enough to hit 212 magic, which is the highest level of magic 
that the rogue needs to max out her spells--at least as far as she is 
able to. Unfortunately, the rogue can never reach 255 magic no matter 
what she wears, so rogue spells sometimes max out at level 4, 6, 9, 
etc, depending on the spell.  Compared to a mage, that's pretty lame, 
which is why I like the Puzzler.
