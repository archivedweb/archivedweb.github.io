There are at least two forms of chatkill/crashkill. One uses a bug in 
your windows program to crash your computer(i think that's the one 
you experienced) and you have to reboot. The microsoft patch should 
fix this. The second one is some kind of long name hack or something. 
Someone hacks a character and gives him a really long name, and when 
he enters a channel, everyone inside gets crashed. This crashes only 
your Diablo program, so you don't have to reboot, just restart 
Diablo. I don't think there's a patch for this yet.

I'm not sure if all this is correct, but after getting crashed about 
10 times in 2 nights, and some discussions with other players, this 
seems to be the consensus. Please post any corrections.
