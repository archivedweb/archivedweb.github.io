Hi. I think your ideas are great! I want to add that I tend to use 
your Stone Curse strategy (and play solo for exp) the most. I will 
add to that the following however. I usually don't take any chain 
lightning in multiplayer ... I replace it with firebolt (or sometimes 
lightning). The reason is, I can curse a bunch of stuff, then tag one 
or two of them with fireBOLT, damaging the creature slightly. I then 
get shared exp. I only do this when I am clear. Various bows of 
sorcery are ideal also. :)

Another tactic I use is to STEP just in front of the RestOfParty 
(ROP) and shoot one charged bolt ... it wakes up 1-4 monsters .. by 
which time I have already stepped back in time for us to wait for the 
monsters' approach.

 My hotkeys solo are Fireball, Chain Lightning, Stone Curse, 
Teleport. For multiplay, I replace Chain Lightning with an 
appropriate spell (firebolt, charged bolt).

 Also, in multiplayer, I sometimes play with a King's Staff of 
Resurrect, and sometimes I play with a King's Staff of Haste. Then I 
can advance tightly with the ROP and stone and bash along with 
everyone else. And the KStaffofRes is a total winner in multiplay. 
When I use that style, I will then keep these hotkeys: Fireball, 
Resurrect (Staff), Stone Curse, Teleport. I let the unfortunate 
victims heal themselves after I resurrect them. I never use the 
fireball at all unless everyone else dies.
