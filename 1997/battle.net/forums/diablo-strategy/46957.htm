I don't know if this is old hat, but I haven't seen this posted for 
at least the last three months or so and it is helpful for beginning 
players.


You don't have to be a rogue to identify which chests  are trapped.  
There are actually two types of traps, those that are sprung right 
from the chest itself and those that come flying out of the wall.  If 
the former, you are out of luck, only a rogue can figure out whether 
its trapped or not.  The latter kind of chest, however can be 
detected and avoided by any class.  Before opening a chest look at 
all walls that surround the chest.  If you see a dark circle on the 
wall, the chest is trapped.  Avoiding the trap is easy.

The hole will always be placed one square away from the chest.  
(above, hole = 0 and Chest =C, the hole is one column to the right of 
the Chest.)  Standing in any of the X locations to open the chest, 
are your best bets at remaining safe.   Standing in any of the B(ad) 
locations will get you toasted/skewered.
