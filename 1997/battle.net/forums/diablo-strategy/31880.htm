Just a minor correction:

On Tue Jul 15 21:31, Skyye wrote:
 
> The Bow of the Bear also doesn't work well against The Hidden--
> Unseen, Stalkers, & Illusion Weavers.  The knock-back will just give 
> them an opportunity to vanish again.  However, with an accurate bow, 
> you can just keep plugging away at them till their heads fall off.  :)

A bear item does not knock monsters away from you.  It knocks a 
creature backwards ***relative to the direction it is facing***.  

So when the hidden turn around and try to run away, a bow of the
bear brings them right back to you!  It is great for them because
they can't run away to disappear.

If you're plugging away at the hidden from a distance I can see where 
the knockback might put them off the screen and give them a chance to 
disappear.  However, these guys are only dangerous when they swarm 
you and that means they'll be in close range, where the knock-back-to-
me effect :) of a bear item comes into play.
