I agree w/Buttkicker--get your res. up, if possible.  Res. to 
lightning will do nothing against Laz.  You need magic and fire res.
Also, I would consider switching weapons.  The last thing you want is
something that moves the witches AWAY from you!!  You'll have a hard
enough time trying to pin them in a corner as it is without that 
obnoxious knock back thing (now, with a bow, that's a different story 
. . .)

As far as tactics in Laz. room itself, I agree w/Buttkicker again-
step in to draw the witches out, and fight them separately from laz 
and his fellow magicians.  I also suggest to keep moving, as a moving 
target is much harder to hit.
To kill the advocates/mages, when I'm a fighter, I do use firewall 
when I can.  Also, remember that 9 times out of 10 they will teleport
away from where you are facing.  So, try to get them into a corner, 
with you facing the corner DIRECTLY (not along the wall, but 90 
degrees to the corner).  They will stay there, and try to teleport 
"away", but will rematerialize ritht there.  Hold the shift 
key and swing at the same spot.  When you get one, immediately move 
to the next.  Personally, I prefer to leave Laz to last; the others 
die faster, and it'll reduce the number of fireballs you have to 
dodge/eat.

Hope this helps, too
