For all you people who need HELP!!!!!

The butcher can be ONE tough customer. Especially in the beginning of 
the game, because he USUALLY appears on level 2. And most people will 
go into his room because they have NO idea who's in there. If you 
don't want to become "Fresh Meat," and you are not level 10 
yet, read this.

First, Play NEW GAMES and clear out level one until your STR is 40, 
and you have enough gold to buy a Claymore. Buy a Claymore. Buy 
leather armor. Equip your new junk. Now, fight down to level 4 (Here 
you'll usuall find the Weak Goat person) (Pleeease, No Hurt, no 
kill!) and get the Goat guy's weapon he'll give you after you spare 
him. Now, go into the blody room on level 2, and attack the butcher. 
Make sure you have LOTSA FULL HEALING potions. Just keep clicking on 
him, and press a belt potion hotkey whenever your health gets low. He 
should die, and you'll get his cleaver, which is a VERY GOOD WEAPON!
