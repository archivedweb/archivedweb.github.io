I won't bother talking about Dreamflange or staffs or spells; others 
have already done that.

Someone else also mentioned the King's Sword of Haste--this is an 
excellent sword for the mage, as it counter-balances the mage's 
weakness as a melee fighter and mage's relatively slow swing-speed.  
I've used this sword with my mage (bought the broadsword version from 
Griz for about 80k) and it worked very well.

Another excellent sword for the mage would be a King's (or Master's) 
Sword of Vampire, so you could constantly replenish your mana shield 
and spell-casting ability every time you hit an attacker.  This would 
be really, really cool.  I personally have never found or bought one, 
but I am always on the lookout for one.

As for boosting your magic levels with the sword, forget it--that's 
what rings and amulets are for.  I had a Master's Sword of Sorcery 
but I found I was much better off with a different suffix (like 
Haste) that would counter the inherent weaknesses of the mage--and 
not merely add to areas that the mage is already strong in.

hope this helps...
