Alright, comments...

The stone cursing thing is very dull. I do use it a lot however. 
Reason for this is because I have more time to play than some of my 
cohorts (particularly my fighter friend known as Damage) and end up 
being a few levels higher than them.

Generally, when I play multiplayer, I play with one other person, 
over a LAN. We are in the same room. Now I realize most of you are 
not this lucky (and believe me, I love it) but I will put forth my 
ideas. First of all, we have the advantage of being able to talk 
instead of type.

I am very fond of indirect kills. I use guardian to death. And I 
really mean it. I also like golem, because I never even have to see 
them. Damage likes golem as well, no chance of stray fire, it's an 
additional fighter in front, and my golem is pretty nuts. 

Generally, he ends up taking out the up close fighters and I take out 
the ranged guys. Golem goes and kicks some lawyer-kin a**, and the 
fighter happily kicks the crap outta the knights. Guardian slows them 
all down (well, unless they are immune). If they are fire immune I 
generally use stone curse on them (might as well be a permanent 
hotkey) and if I have to, I will swap out sticks to my king's staff 
of sorcery.

You mentioned one point about the difference between a 40 costing 
stone curse and a 50 costing stone curse. I do not think it is a 
matter so much of it costing 10 extra (although it is appreciable) as 
much that it is the fact that a 40 costing stone curse is gonna be on 
a guy who has 400 mana, and the 50 will be on the guy with 300 mana. 
Ten versus six. It can make all the difference.

guardian

use a lot? Like after you cast 1000 of a spell, the cost goes down 1
