legits" etc, but I think most of them are too long and boring to 
read, so I'll be very blunt, nonphilosophical and down to the point 
with this post.

1. Diablo is a game.  Play it and have fun.  Play it however you have 
fun. If you are not having fun play it another way.

 I have fun playing without cheating.  I find fun in getting new 
items, gaining levels, working with others to achieve a common goal, 
and other reasons.

make a choice.  It is the same thing with smoking.  Give them the 
facts and give them the CHOICE.

3.  If you don't like cheating join a noncheating guild/clan.

4.  Insulting and attacking cheaters doesn't make you any better than 
them.  It may give you a false sense of righteousness, but your 
spirit will be just as empty/full or emptier than it was before your 
attack.

5.  Cheaters are people just like noncheaters/legits
