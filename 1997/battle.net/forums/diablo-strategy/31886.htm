Only one question.  'Saintly' is something like +100% - 
+125%.  You only stated +76%, which would be something like 
'grand' armor.  If your memory of 'saintly' is correct, this is the 
plate I would use without a doubt.  In fact, Awesome plate of sorcery 
is probably (argueably) the best plate in the game (unless Godly or 
Holy full plate in multiplayer really exists).  Saintly is just a 
step below Awesome for AC.  So I would use the Saintly plate of 
sorcery over the others forever (unless you find awesome plate of 
sorcery).   Armor class GREATLY reduces your chance of being hit by 
physical attacks.  Also, to compensate for the damage that the 
witches inflict, you should boost your resistances via rings/helm/bow 
or whatever method you can.  60% or greater 'resistance to magic' 
will help you out there (75% is MAX anyway).
