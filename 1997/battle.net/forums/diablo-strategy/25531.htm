"I think I will try to find and read more books before my 
character's level gets too high."

I don't think you understand how this "book thing" works.  
The amount of magic required to read a book does not depend on your 
character's experience level.  It depends solely on what your current 
SPELL LEVEL for that spell is.  

I believe this is what everyone is trying to tell you.

A book of "X" type, when your current level of that spell is 
"Y", ALWAYS takes "Z" magic points to read.  I have 
even seen pages on the web that give tables illustrating this 
relationship, showing the required magic points for each level of 
each spell.

I SERIOUSLY doubt that starting over will do you any good.  

Hope this helps.  Do let us know if I am wrong.
