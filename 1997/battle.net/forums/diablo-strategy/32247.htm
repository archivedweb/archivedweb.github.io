On where you're headed and what level you are.

As the Rogue increases in levels, her need for Harmony decreases.  I 
found that in the mid 30's to about 40, I needed Harmony in the 
Hell/Caves and in Hell/Hell.  Not at all in NM diff though.

I gave away my Harmony ring when I reached level 42 and haven't 
really needed it since.  Now, occasionally I will get stunned by a 
dog or one of those Lightening monsters with big claws, but only when 
there's more than 2 around me.  It doesn't kill me either like it did 
when I was in my 30's and running through the Hell/Caves.

So, my advice would be to keep it and use it in Hell/Hell until you 
find that you no longer really need it.  I don't think the added Hit 
Points from a Tiger item will help you much if you can't move...
