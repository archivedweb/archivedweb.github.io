can die and loose all your stuff right near the stairway that leads
up to the previous level. So if you're not on one of the levels that 
has
3 stairs (i.e. levels 5, 9, and 13) then you can be really screwed!
    If you have a horde of guys waiting by the stairway in order to
gang bang you, you are in deep trouble. If you are in way over your
head, then you won't even have time to cast a teleport or phase or
something to get out of the way before they slaughter you. I think

stuff.
So it was, in the big picture, hardly worth it.

sometimes
you just have to say "oh, well" and start getting a set of 
new stuff. There
