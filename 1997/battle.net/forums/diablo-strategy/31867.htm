The biggest surprise for me in the responses to Code's
very interesting survey was the number of people who
don't consider the use of a Diablo backup program as
"cheating". Now just about everyone in this discussion
is on the side of the Angels, so I don't intend the use
of "cheat" here in any perjurative fashion. Is SnakeGod
a clan of "cheaters" because they sanction backup --
of course not. Am I going to label someone a "cheater"
just because they use Deye in chat occasionally -- not
at all. But, for the life of me, I cannot understand how
one can say Deye is a "cheat" but Kevin Lambert's Diablo
utility is not a "cheat". As Lok says: third party
program *wink, wink*. Obviously, there are honest disagreements
on this -- I'm simply stating my opinion as I see it.

On another point, I think Code is just dead wrong when he says
that shopping or using storage characters is cheating. You
don't have to play that way if you don't want to, but e.g.
shopping is just as much a part of the game as dungeon crawling.
