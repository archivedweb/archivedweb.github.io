Generally, I don't think this is true.  Some of the posters on this 
forum will occasionally trade within their own guild, but it is 
usually to "complete" a set rather than "upgrade" it. 
 For example, a rogue may have a nice bow, KSOV and Stormshield, but 
have terrible armor.  She may give up the KSOV and Stormshield for a 
Awesome Plate of Sorcery.  And since the trade occurred within a 
guild, the items aren't duped.

Many others don't trade at all.  They will give their stuff away to 
help people, but not as part of a trade.

My current Rogue (lvl 36) has Eaglehorn, Windforce, Stormshield, 
King's Sword of Gore, Blessed Full Plate of the Wolf, Amulet of the 
Zodiac, Obs Ring of the Zodiac, and an Obs Ring of the Heavens.  None 
of these were traded for.  However, if I meet a friend who I KNOW I 
can trust, I would gladly give up one of the bows, sword, shield or a 
combo thereof, to get a better armor.  Also, I've given many items to 
strangers I met on battle.net, including an amulet of the zodiac and 
a gold ring of wizardry (I think).
