Matt,

The Nightscape is an unique Cape. It's stats are:

Faster Hit Recovery
-40% Light Radius (That's the reason for the name...)
+3 Dexterity
+20% Resist All
and Armor Class: 15

The Nightscape made you invisible in an older version of Diablo 
(older than 1.00), but since the Invisibility Spell was removed from 
the retail version of the game, the Nightscape's stats have been 
changed to the above.
