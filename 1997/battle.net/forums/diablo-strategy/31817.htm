> I want are some suggestions for items I should be searching for. 

You need some equipment with resistances. 

> I still have another 120 magic to go before I max out, too. 
> (250 is maxed right?)

250 max without adjustments for magic items.

> What level CL do you have to have to lower that spell costs??  

It will always cost a lot. After about spell level 11 you will start 
to see gaps, so I wouldn't take it too high.

> Damage increase is minimal. Is there some point where it starts 
making a bigger difference to get CL books??  

With each level increase in the spell you get one more stream of 
lightning in your chain. The damage for each stream isn't what 
changes, the number of streams is.

> When I get in scraps with SoulBurners, and the like, I have to really 
pump out the lightning to kill em.  That bleeds the mana like a 
bucket with a hole in it. 

It is far more mana efficient if you hit them with all the streams at 
once. Don't go into the middle of the room sending streams in all 
directions, it wastes too much mana. Back out of the room forcing the 
monsters through a choke point. 

> Another thing, how do you higher end mages deal with triple immunes.  

Stone them then let a Golem bet on them and use your own melee 
weapon. Better yet work as a team with a Warrior and/or a Rogue.

> I have read of people using melee weapons, but it seems unreasonable. 
My mage swings a sword like a decrepit grandmother.  

Look for a weapon of Haste with a + to hit.
