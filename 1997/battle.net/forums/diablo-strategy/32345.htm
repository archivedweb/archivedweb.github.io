On Fri Jul 18 12:02, Cyrus wrote:
> How do you get to cow level?

After you defeat Diablo and receive the first soulstone, 
a movie will play and you'll learn about how the three cows 
are actually Horadrim wizards that trapped Diablo's two 
brothers  (Mephisto and Baal). As punishment Diablo 
transformed them into cows and banished them to the cow 
level to suffer eternally with the other condemned 
townsfolk.

With their bovine mouths, they uddered magical words to
create a dimension gate back to Tristram. But as their memory
of human existance rapidly faded, so did their magical
powers. Alas, the portal closed before any other residents
of the cow level could escape.

To get to the cow level, you must find the three soulstones
and give them to the cow wizards. Each soulstone comes from 
different levels of difficulty (normal, nightmare, and hell mode.) 
The three soulstones combined contain enough magical energy 
for the wizards to maintain an open portal.

Once there, you can break Diablo's curse, rescue the trapped
townsfolk, and restore the cow wizards to human form.
