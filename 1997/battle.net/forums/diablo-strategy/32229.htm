You are playing Single Player I guess. If you go to the Healer in the 
village he'll tell you that the well in the village has been 
poisoned. You then get a quest note and if you enter the dark passage 
you'll have to find a flow of water.
I don't know what the cold spring is, maybe the well in the village.
How to kill the Butcher depends on your character.
Fighter: make sure you got a high dex else you can't place a hit on 
him. Last man standing sorta thing.
Rogue: shoot arrows through a window. 
Mage: open the door, cast Firewall, close the door and take out those 
marshmellows.
Hope that helps,
