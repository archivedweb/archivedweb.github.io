On Mon Jul 14 22:44, MacHoChaN3 wrote:
> Is this legit? I haven't tried it yet and i would like to make sure 
> of this before walking into a room full of Fire-ball chuckers!

It is quite legit.  You can see where your area of light is --about 
four or five spaces from you.  Keep everything beyond that edge about 
an equal distance and you should have no trouble.  Also, I forgot to 
mention, that, if you do attract a horde, teleport away, around a 
corner  or into a separate room and I learned that sometimes they 
baddies forget where you went and stop chasing you.  Honestly.  You 
can see them mill around for a while, then stand ready again.  Just 
go back and start popping them.  One more thing --this is of NO USE 
playing multiplayer because your pals will draw stuff to you, but 
that is okay if you don't have any magic-users on the level --just 
use their aura of light to see and kick tail with them, enjoying the 
benefit of that AC of 60 helm.  Witches seem to want to move right up 
in your face before firing, so learn to target them quickly and knock 
them off before they get close.  Stray arrows WILL attract a horde 
sometimes, so practice well with non-magic users first.  Go for it!
