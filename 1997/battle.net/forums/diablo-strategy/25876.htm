On Tue Jun 03 08:25, chiefmo wrote:
>    Is there any way to swap items between your own multiplayers? My 
> warrior found a bumkicking bow, a much better bow than what my rogue 
> has now and I hate to waste it by selling it off (I need the 
> inventory space). Any suggestions?


There  sure is.  

If you know somebody else who plays Diablo in your city,
have them start up a game.
Join them via the modem, and give them the bow.
Leave the game and rejoin with the Rogue.

Presto!  Now she  has the bow.

I do this ALL  THE TIME with  my one of my friends,
(swapping  stuff  to our other  characters)  and it makes
getting the right equipment to the right  characters  relatively 
easy.

JUST MAKE SURE YOU CAN TRUST  THE PERSON.

ENJOY,

bONes.
