The reason flame wave doesn't always do the damage it says is because 
it has "gaps" as does fire wall.  You can walk through both a 
fire wall and a flame wave without taking damage, so don't assume 
that throwing one down the hall causes him to be hit.  Also, a minor 
bug I've noticed, if you hit monsters too fast, it seems that not all 
of the hits do damage.  But, like you said, holy bolts work best, 
since they both stun him, and also they do not hit teammates who 
might otherwise get hit with friendly fire.

2 or 3 mages all hitting him with holy bolts should keep him stunned 
enough that you could kill him without going through more than 2 mana 
potions in hell/hell
