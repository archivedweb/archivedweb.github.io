The only way to raise the spell level at your situation is the 
enchanted shrine. One spell will be decreased by 1 level but all the 
other spells will be increased by 1 level. The exception is that when 
the decreased spell is at level 15 before you hit the shrine, it will 
be down 2 levels. If one of your key spells is down, just find 
another enchanted shrine. But I will suggest that when your chain 
lightening spell reaches level 12, you should stop using the 
enchanting shrine. Level 13 and up chain lightening has gaps and is 
really annoying in nightmare/hell and hell/hell. Keep playing with 
your level 35 rogue and happy hunting for the enchanted shrines.
