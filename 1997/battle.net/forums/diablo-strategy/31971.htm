On Wed Jul 16 18:07, Vox wrote:
> i've been playing Diablo for some time now and i was wondering if 
> someone could tell me what the best equpment for a rogue, 
> sorccerer,and a warrior would be

I can only speak for the rogue but here goes:
Weapon:  Stick with a bow until high levels and then maybe            
      experiment with melee weapons on occasion but use the           
       bow as your main stay.  There are two good unique bows         
         the eaglehorn and windforce.  You can find better bows       
           with good combinations of prefixes and suffixes but the 
two                  uniques are really good if you can find one.

Armor: awsome full plate is the best.  I wear saintly full plate of   
          precision because I haven't yet found awsome full plate 
with a             suffix and the AC loss was not too bad but get 
awsome full             plate as soon as you can (you should start 
finding awsome in             the high 20 levels).

Helm: With a rogue you almost have to get some type of obsidian or    
        jade helm for your resistances but the royal circulet is just 
plain            awsome if you find it use it and get your 
resistances some                   where else.

Rings/Amulets:  Use enough strength enhancing ones to wear the full   
                        plate and then go for dex or magic/mana.  
Rings or                          amulets of the heavens or zodiac 
are the best but so                          far I've only found 
heavens stuff (the zodiac is kind of                          rare).

Merrilee (level 36 rogue)
