Although items like the DF, the Thinking cap, Naj's and AA stuff do 
raise your active spell levels, your character still knows them at 
their lower levels, and that is where you scribe them.  So if you are 
trying to scribe that book of chain lightning to level 8 on your 
fighter or rogue (you know it at 7), using the Dreamflange is 
probably the only way to scribe it (I believe it is the only one 
handed weapon item that gives +30 magic), and you will be able to 
scribe to level 8.
