On Thu Jul 17 14:20, Richard The Great wrote:
> I am currently a level 21 warrior and I just entered level 13
> and I am getting nailed by spitters and witches. Do you have any
> advice on getting rid of these bastards?

I'm a 32 level sorceror who always ends up having to fight acid 
spitters on level 14. for me this is a problem because the type of 
spitters i face are immune to fire. what i do is walk slow into the 
room. as soon as a few spot me i run around the corner and turn 
around. holding down the shift key i start swinging my melee weapon. 
the spitters come around the corner and walk right into it.  it takes 
about 2-3 hits to kill one.  make sure when you turn the corner you 
stay against the wall and close to the door way, this way when they 
come around you are on top of them. their melee attacks are alot less 
damaging then the acid. 

As far as the witches go you can use the same tactic, but the only 
problem is they dont always hug the corner like the spitters do, so 
you will have to corner them like the goat archers in the cats and 
hack away.

Hopes this helps.
