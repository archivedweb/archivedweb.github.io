I have df and use it for my warrior too in some cases:

(1) Reading, as you do, but note that since it raises your spell 
level +1, it may, in some instances make it harder to read a book, 
even with the +30 mag.

(2) When my warrior pretends to be a mage, i.e. when needs lots of 
magic, like in rooms (or levels) where there are only spell users, 
and he has to use teleport, stone curse, golem, etc..  (Surprisingly 
this happens a LOT in hell -- I usually clear out the knights first.)

(3) And when it becomes necessary for my warriorto emulates a naked 
mage (or BNM considering his natural handicaps) after the screen 
turns red, etc.  and he has to go back un-armored to get his 
equipment. 

So, all in all, Dreamflange does make a good back-up for a warrior.
