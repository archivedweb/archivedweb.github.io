    Just wanted to revise my rather hasty assessment of the situation 
you described before with the zodiacs.  While I have never lost a 
host of equiptment using my pride destroying method of recovery, I 
can see how it is possible.  In many situations, I was playing on a 
LAN and called a friend to join the game and help.  Obviously, this 
is a little tricky to do in other situations :)  In general, once you 
lose your items, you shouldn't try to beat them with "backup" 
equiptment.  I don't believe in backup equiptment.  It's weaker than 
your primary, so what makes you think you can beat these baddies when 
you couldn't do it fully loaded?  Following that logic, it's better 
to try and get in naked and lure em around the level.  Of course, 
when the monsters choose to rest their greasy asses on the bottom 
stair, it's a little tricky to manage a recovery as I suggested.

One note:  I never suggested a phase or teleport.  In that situation, 
it's better to cast a town portal.  Cast it away from the monsters 
and then die.  Restart in town and then use portal to start away from 
the baddies for a possible monster-luring-joyride
