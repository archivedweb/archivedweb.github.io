On Mon Jun 02 13:19, Jon wrote:
> Will someone help out me, a novice player, on how to defeat the King  
> on level three of the real game.  I've tried a lot, but to no 
> prefail. Please respond!


The best tactic is to use holy bolt spells on him.  If you have the 
spell memorized, buy full rejuvination potions (if you can afford) or 
full heal and full mana restore, and put them in your belt.  After 
isolating the king (kill others or run), pump the king full of the 
holy bolt spells.  If you do not have the spell memorized, get a 
staff of holy bolt (hope you can find it) and fill your belt with 
full heal potions.  Pray that your staff doesn't run out or buy two 
staffs to be sure.  It should take about 40 charges to kill him 
(including several misses).  Good luck
