On Wed Jul 16 15:08, Richard The Great wrote:
> I am playing in single player because I can't figure out how to 
> play multiplayer just yet. I was wondering if there was a diff.
>  in single vs. multi.? Also, where can I find a saintly 
> sword or armor on single player?

Yes there is a difference.  The creatures in multi-player are tougher 
to account for multiple players (although you can still handle them 
solo).  Multi-player also has three levels of difficulty (normal, 
nightmare, and hell) to provide for more challange as your character 
advances.  The multiplayer game only has the butcher and king leoric 
quest in addition to lazurus and diablo while single player has a 
number of different random quests.  A lot of the items available from 
quests in single player do not exist in multi-player.

Saintly is a prefix that applies only to armor, helms, and shields 
you can not get it on a sword.  To find it you have to have a 
sufficently high level character and then you'll either find it in 
the dungeon or you can buy it from gris or wirt (completely random 
system so you may find it right away or you may not see it for a long 
time).  If you want a full description of the prefixed and suffixes 
for weapons, armor, rings, etc check out diablo.scorched.com.

Hope this helps.

Merrilee

BTW To play multi-player you can simple use the IPX or direct cable 
connections to play solo even without a modem.
