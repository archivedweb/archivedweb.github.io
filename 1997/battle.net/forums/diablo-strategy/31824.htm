On Wed Jul 16 07:15, Drizzt the Assasin of Evil wrote:
> Is magic worth it for a warrior
> Does anyone have sugestoins

I would say: YES!
I have a level 24 warrior now and he has a couple of level 15 spells 
so that rocks. Unfortunately he's low on mana due to some shrines :(
It's always handy to be able to cast a nice Firewall or Healing.
The trick is collecting equipment which gives you +magic
Max magic for warriors is 50 so if you want to get all your spells to 
level 15 you'll need an extra 205 magic. Hmm there is a shrine that 
raises spell levels though, don't remember the name right now.
There are also three shrines that raise certain spells by 2 levels 
but they decrease your PERMANENT mana so be careful.
Another thing, healing starts to cost more when you gain in level.
Magic can certainly save your life, but the same goes for a good old 
King's sword of carnage!
So, yes try to get as many spells as high up as possible but try to 
achieve this with +magic equipment and not by raising your stat in 
the beginning.
Hope that helped a bit. I guess most of it depends on how you play 
your warrior btw.
