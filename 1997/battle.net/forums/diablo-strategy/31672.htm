#). Is it Cheating to accept Gold from another player?
> 
no as long as the gold is earn by a non cheater in the same game as
the person accepting the gold...also the characters of both must be
app. the same level, ie. a 3 level character should not recieve gold
from a player in normal/hell...
#). Is it Cheating to accept Items from a higher char?
>
no as long as the character is with in 4-5 levels of the other 
character
once again a 3 level character should not recieve items from a 
character
in hell...also items should only be accepted if the character knows
they are not duped, eithor from reputation or he saw the other pick
up the item...
 #). Is it Cheating to have higher level chars buy items for you?
>
once again only if the character buying is 4-5 levels above the 
other...
fairly selfexplainitory if you've read above...
#). Is it Cheating to have higher level chars give lower level chars 
> elixers?
>
yes, but not if the lower level character is also able to buy potions,
ie. if a 25 level character buys from a 50 level...however characters
who would not be able to buy elixers should notbe able to buy them...
#). Is it Cheating to clear levels for lower players?
>
yes if the lower level characters do not help in clearing the level...
ie. sometimes a lower level mage or rouge can help a warrior of higher
level by adding fire support...however, if the clearing is only done
by the higher level character than it is illegal.
 #). Is it Cheating to Start games with only the intent to check 
Wirt, 
> Adria or Griz for items?
>
this is legal b/c buying items is part ofthe game as blizard designed
it...also, items from wirt and the blacksmith change slower in 
multiplayer and i've gone through a entire sequence from hell/cave 
through hell/nightmare with out any items changing...if people
want to spend the time restarting go for it...i'll just play
#). Is it Cheating to use Deye in chat to look at other peoples stats?
>
got to say yes but to this one...it is cheating to find out characters
weakness...however if cheating is suspected than this can help to 
prevent characters from cheaters...don't use it myself but wouldn't
condem its use for that reason... 
#). Is it Cheating to allow lower players to portal into areas they 
> could not regularly access?
>
this is the same as clearing levels...not cheating if the lower level
is helping to clear the level...cheating if lower character just
getting items... 
#). Is it Cheating to create chars whos only purpose is to hold extra 
> items?
>
no as long as the items are real... 
#). Is it Cheating to transfer items found by 1 char to another char?
>
no aslong as the items are real and the transfer is not to characters
of significantly lower level... 
#). Is it Cheating to Pk with the Nova spell?
>
got to say no...pking is a way of life...and using the nova spell is
one way to do it...don't personally pk but if it is done by legit
character than its ok.... 
#). Is it Cheating to Portal Kill someone?
> 
no...if they go into the portal then so be it...
#). Is it Cheating to Teleport in town?
> 
yes...but if it is only to avoid tk then it becomes a grey area...
#). Is it Cheating to backup chars against system crashes?
> 
no as long as the backups are used only to restore system crashes...
if it is used when a character dies and loses items then no it is not
legal...
