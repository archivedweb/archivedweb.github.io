If you are on Level 13 in SINGLE player, the Warrior of Blood Quest 
might be in your game.  If the book that initates this quest has not 
been read yet, the room where the Warrior of Blood is which is where 
the stairs to 14 are will be probably be still closed up.  If you are 
in Single player, look for this book.  If you are in multiplayer, 
definitely go with the teleport idea, a very good solution.  Teleport 
won't help if you have the Warlord of Blood quest because until he's 
activated he is standing in the stairs to 14.

The upside to this is that if you do have the Warlord of Blood quest 
you should get some good magic weapons and armor.

Hope this helped.
