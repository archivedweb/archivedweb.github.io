Hello,

Sheesh, I think I've legitimately found 10 or 11 Zodiacs total.  
Highlights are a Ruby Amulet of the Zodiac (lev 16 -- Diablo's 
Chamber),a Garnet Ring of the Zodiac (lev 16), a Dragon's Amulet of 
the Zodiac (lev 14 -- off Dreadjudge, the Magistrate boss), and, of 
course, Obsidian Ring of the Zodiac (Laz).  Levs 15 and 16 are best 
for finding Zodiacs.  I've found 16 to be especially good.  
Difficulty doesn't matter, as far as I can tell.  Kill everything and 
check every barrel, chest, and headless body.  Don't expect to have 
the same luck I did though -- statistical theory would predict that 
some people would find many Zodiacs, and others would find zilch, 
even if they both killed everything, checked every nook and cranny, 
etc.

I started finding them in my mid-30s and am still finding them today 
with my level 44 Sorcerer, Binkley.
