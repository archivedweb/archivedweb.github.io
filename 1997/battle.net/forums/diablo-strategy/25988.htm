Diablo is a most excellent game, no question about that.

Just beware of those on BNET....

Do you have internet access at 28800bps, if yes, get the game.

If you don't have interenet access, do you have friends who own the 
game that you would be able to play multiplayer with over the modem?  
If yes, get the game.

If the answer is no, then you are missing 50% or more of the 
game.  In the case of no, I wouldn't buy the game for single player 
mode only.

Beware, if you do get on BNET playing the game, there are a whole 
bunch of idiots sitting around typing abusive language and they will 
type things at you that will be unfriendly and just plain nasty.  

Try to play private games only but if you don't have anyone to play 
in private games with, join a non-cheating guild/clan, with some 
decent players.  Cheating is rampant and cheaters will do what they 
can to try and ruin the game for you.

Remember Diablo is really only enjoyable on a P100 computer or better 
with 24mb of RAM or better and a 32bit sound card. (I don't know the 
requirements for X-WING VS. TIE FIGHTER).  Anything less than a P100  
the game slows down too much.  (If you have ever seen the game run on 
a P200 you'll know)

Just some ideas to consider in the purchase of Diablo...

Row
