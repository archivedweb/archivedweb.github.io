This sorta answers both...the dreamflange is a unique mace that 
raises your spell levels by 2 and is considered one of the best mage 
melee weapons.  Other than items like this, the best way to raise 
spell levels is to read multiple books of the same spell, or visit a 
shrine that raises your spell levels.  There are many sites devoted 
to general Diablo information, go to yahoo and look up diablo to find 
them.
