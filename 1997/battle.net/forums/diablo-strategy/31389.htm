It's really simple to do. Personally, I have taken my multi-player 
back to single player to romp around there :) it was very fun (note, 
some Multi-player items seem to "not exist" in the single 
player game! Try taking a KSOV back into single and it 
"transforms" into something else.... very strange!).

Anyway you only need the HAckerDiabloTrainer and to use it, you start 
a multiplayer game, stand in town with your char and ALT-TAB back to 
windows. Next, run the trainer and save out your char to disk. Then, 
ALT-TAB back to Diablo, exit your game and start a single player char 
with the same name as your multi-char and get into a game. When your 
new char is standing in town, alt-tab to the trainer and click 
"restore" and select your multi-char backup off of disk. Alt-
tab back to Diablo 1 last time and *BEFORE MOVING* select "new 
game". It should be fine now!

Utils avail at:

http://www.burgoyne.com/pages/dchriste/index.html
