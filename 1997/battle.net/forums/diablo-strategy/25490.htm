Ok, posting this message is contrary to my earlier message concerning 
not correcting others' grammar.  But I've got to mention MY pet 
peeve: the use of "should of", "could of", and 
"would of".  It's my impression that THIS is the most rampart 
abuse of the language today.  It's not seen as often as your-instead-
of-you're, but only because it's not needed as much.  I'd say that 
99% of the time I've seen someone try to say "should've" 
it comes out "should of".  I think contractions must not be 
stressed enough in our schools or something.  Think about it, people! 
 "Should of"??  Makes no sense at all!
