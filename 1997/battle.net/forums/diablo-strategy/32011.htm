I have spent sometime reading all of the posts about Godly plate of 
the Whale and Godly plate of Ages, it seems that it all boils down to 
this:
To be legit or not to be legit...........
I am a Legit player, but I also have not refused, "Duped 
help", be it armour, elixers, weapons, ect, ect..... The reason I 
feel that I am still Legit is because of the way I play the game. I 
play for the the adventure, the thrill of working with a team, and 
the spirit of comradery. I am also a big believer in, "Improvise, 
Adapt, and Overcome". I liken having Godly plate or any other, 
"Specialized", item to the days when a Dungeon Master would 
allow a player to receive an enchanted(duped) item to help him in his 
quest or to allow players to become so evolved that they may be the 
ones to do the enchanting(Duping), for a price of course. I use what 
I know to help out less experienced campaigners in the same way I was 
helped when I started out. The one thing I have not cut corners on is 
my level and experience. That is sacred and is best when you come by 
it the honest way.
The thrill of the campaign and the Team work involved is the real 
brass ring of any who play, whether it be those with Godly plate or 
those who chose not to take, "Duped items".

I welcome any challenges and or comments.......
