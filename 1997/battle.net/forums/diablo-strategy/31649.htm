             This post was interesting because it made me think about 
my position on cheating. Even though I am somewhat of a Diablo 
newbie, I was generally against cheating because it a) makes the game 
too easy and takes the challenge out of the game and b) being the 
victim of a cheater really sucks. I posted a few posts that were 
anticheat, flaming newbies even less experienced than myself who 
wanted to know how to use cheats and trainers. However, now I realize 
that I myself may be against cheats, but that doesn't I should preach 
my beliefs to others, who may find nothing wrong with using cheats. 
As long as cheaters don't use cheats against me, I don't think I 
should harass them anymore. This post really changed my mind about 
cheaters. If they want to, they can use cheats...its their fifty 
bucks, and I shouldn't care what they do with it. This post may sound 
stupid and corny, but Lorax's post had a great effect on my 
impressionable young mind.
