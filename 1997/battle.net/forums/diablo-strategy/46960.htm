Yes, AC reduces the chance of your being hit, and not the actual 
damage taken when you are hit.

And no, AC doesn't help against magic/lightning/fire attacks.  In 
those situations, you're better off with Obsidian rags than Godly 
Plate.  However, you need both AC and resistance to survive, so I'd 
concentrate on getting high AC armor and getting resistances up with 
Jade/Obsidian jewellry, helms, or shields.  

Some mages will hold Obsidian weapons to boost resistance while they 
fight with magic, but warriors and rogues are really better off with 
prefixes that increase %to hit or damage.

hope this helps...
