I don't believe helping ANY new character is 'cheating'.  However, 
helping out a level 1 character who is controlled by a person NEW to 
Diablo is, well . . ., kind of. . ., unethical.  Especially if you're 
giving them ridiculously good stuff.  Rings of the moon would 
probably be okay.  However, once a player has gotten ANY character 
into the level 30's and who has (or could) get their 3rd dot solo, 
then I don't think that its even unethical to give their 'new' level 
1 character good stuff (even if their new character is a different 
character class).  So, I'm not opposed to helping newbies like some 
people are, nor do I consider an experienced player starting a new 
character to be a 'newbie'.
