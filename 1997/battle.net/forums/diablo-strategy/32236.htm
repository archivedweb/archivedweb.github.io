I like the mages I play with to be alittle more offensive than just 
sitting and stone cursing everyone. But I believe the real key you 
metioned several time is group experience together in how effective 
of tactics you can bring to bear on the critters. I play a 
rogues/mages alot and when you get to a certain level your expected 
to know what you are doing. Those of us that got there without dupes, 
cheats and hacks anyways. You should know hey these are all fire 
immune monsters the mage is going to be using lightning or (gasp) 
chain lightning. Now chain only goes in the direction of monsters 
away from the mage so just position yourself where no monsters can be 
and fire away as the mage chains them. If they are lightning immune 
even easier use opening as kill zones and have one character run 
forward and draw the monsters out. When the drawing character get 
back out into position everyone opens fire this kills monsters 
quickly and is cheaper on the mages mana than stoning and most 
importantly fun for all party members. This works best for rogues and 
mages in a party. I am not trying to slight warriors its just I don't 
have alot of experience with them, so my views are from the 
rogue/mage perspective. One last point as long as lag exists in 
Diablo the accidential PK will occur so don't worry about it and have 
fun. But preplan how you are going to deal with it... ie-either res 
or restart in town. I've found restarting in town is best because:

1) You have all you gear(repair), some items to id, maybe need 
potions.

2) 150 buys several tp's in anyone's mana ball. It costs 200 per res 
and they each take up a slot.

3) you can dump any excessive gold in town check the witch get your 
bearing before going back into the fray. Also the free heal at pepins.

4) After you res someone they/you still have to get their heath and 
mana back up. Costing at least 1 potion and a healing spell.

Anyways just some thoughts but teamwork is the main key.
