how about nother newbie strat...filling your inventory :

i try and keep my inventory looking like this at lower levels

pppp  sshh   h=helm
pppp  sshh   s=staff/shield whatever (anything 3 spaces tall)
pp    ss     p=potion/scroll
pppppppppp

why do i do this?  well, when you pick up potions and scrolls in the 
dungeon they get placed in the lowest row with a space open, and in 
the leftmost spot on that row.  therefore with my inventory like this 
i can pick up two potions and then still pick up a 2x3 item and not 
have to move stuff to fit it.  plus, when you pick up gold it gets 
placed in the lowest row possible, in the rightmost space on that 
row.  any gold i pick up gets thrown under the helm (or rings, pots, 
etc.) and i don't have to worry about *that* blocking an item 
placement either.  as a bonus, since my pots are mostly on the left 
of my inventory, i can open my inventory in a battle (as a last 
resort) and drink straight out of it without moving the mouse very 
far from battle.  hope this helps, it is my time-tested strategy.
