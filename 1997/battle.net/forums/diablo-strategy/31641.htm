Basically I consider cheating to be getting something your character 
could not obtain on their own through the context of the game as set 
up by Blizzard (this includes hacking and duping items).  This means 
that if a low level char can not yet obtain an item with a certain 
prefix or suffix and has that item given to them that is cheating.  
On the other hand if a char can get a certain prefix or suffix and 
just hasn't yet found it with the right item and someone gives then 
it, that is ok.  I know this leaves a big judgement call but your 
getting into some big time gray areas with your questions so there is 
no absolutes.  I violate my rule with the elixors for this reason.  I 
don't think pumping up stats has a major effect on the game.  If a 
first level character were given enough elixors to max their stats 
that would be cheating but if you just receive some elixors here and 
there I don't see a major problem.
