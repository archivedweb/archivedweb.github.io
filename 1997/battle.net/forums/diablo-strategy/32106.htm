> Has anyone ever tried stirring up the entire level and waiting at a 
spot for chain?

I did try this once and got a terrible surprise. My chain didn't 
work!!!

Gaps in your chain are caused by exceeding the maximum number of 
animations the game can handle at one time. Each moving monster 
counts as a animation. Get enough moving monsters and there are no 
animations left over for chain.
