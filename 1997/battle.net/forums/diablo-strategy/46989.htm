A couple more ways to kill butcher

Find a room with grates in the walls and make sure the door can be 
closed. Let the butcher chase you to the room and close the door 
behind you. The butcher can not open doors so you will be safe 
inside. Through the grates you can fire arrows if you have a bow ( 
this doesn,t work for me ) Or cast firewall , lightning , charges 
bolt, or firebolt. Make sur you have plenty of blue potions, the 
butcher is tough for young mages.

Or
Open the door to the butchers room. Cast fire wall then  close the 
door. The butcher will be stuck inside and killed. Stone curse is 
good if you can buy a scroll. Stone then cast firewall under him.

Or

Wait until your more powerful. then go back and taunt him as you kill 
him. This works for me.
