I am disappointed that there are so many uniques that really do suck, 
and I do believe that the best unique ought to be better than the 
best normal magical counterpart.  For example, the best unique plates 
are naj's plate and demonspike coat.  But, I personally, would prefer 
Awesome or Holy Plate of X for any character.  Mages get mobbed once 
in awhile, and that high AC is more important than 1 spell level, in 
my opinion anyway.  What I'm really disappointed in is that there are 
no good unique rings or amulets, or hand to hand weapons for that 
matter.  Yeah, Doombringer is nice and so is Mess. Reaver, but they 
don't even come close to a king's sword of haste or king's sword of 
blood, which are just as easy to find.  However, I do think Windforce 
is the best bow, although not by much.  Merciless Bows of Thunder or 
Burning are very close.  Also, I think the Royal Circlet and the 
Thinking Cap are better than any other helms you can find.  
Stormshield is also better than any magical shield, although a good 
awesome shield of the tiger or emerald shield of X can be more 
valuable depending on resistances.  

My point is this, the uniques do not suck, they are good.  The only 
complaint I have is that many of them should have been better.
