Ok, I hope you can read this but here it goes.

1) You find other players by typing /whois PLAYERNAME , eg. /whois 
Lews
   This will give you their account number and channel they are in or
   game.  If you know the player's account number you can do a /whois 
#000000
   and it will give you the same information.

2) If the person you are looking for is in a private channel you can 
whisper
   by doing /w or /msg followed by PLAYERNAME MESSAGE, eg. /w Lews 
This is
   a whisper.

3) If a person is annoying you with whispers or otherwise you can do 
a /s
   PLAYERNAME or #000000 to squelch them and ignore messages (#000000 
replace
   this with the account number found from the /whois or /who command 
in 1.)

4) Be careful when playing in public games,  there are those out 
there that
   would rather kill you than play with you they are Player Killer's 
(PK's)
   but there are a lot of good people too.  I have meet more good 
than bad.

5) the Green, Yellow, or Red bars next to others on the channel 
represent their
   lag.  Red bad Green good, if lag is bad then if you played a game 
with
   them it might be a little jerky.  Note:  you cannot see your own 
lag.

6) The red dots above peoples heads represent the levels they have 
killed
   Diablo on.  1 norma, 2 nightmare 3 Hell.  They are also called 
Soulstones.

I hope this list helps, Enjoy!!!!
