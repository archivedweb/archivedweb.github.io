Well, my opinion Magik would be to stick with your Knight's Haste 
mace & shield.  Why?  Well because of the extra AC & resists your 
shield provides for your fighter.  Also a good shield, allow you to 
block & recover quicker.  For a warrior his weapon dam is VERY 
important, however in the heat of battle, a good shield will make all 
the difference in the world, due to the absorb facter.  The axe 
decreases your HP by 50.  Finally you have a hasted weapon, therefore 
it swings MUCH faster then that big mean axe of yours, therefore you 
can pin the enemies easier.  Therefore my opinion would be to stick 
with your mace & shield & save your jewelry for str or/and vit 
benfits.
