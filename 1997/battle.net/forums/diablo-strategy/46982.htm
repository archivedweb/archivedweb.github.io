On Thu Oct 23 17:25, Aldai wrote:
> just what exactly is a golem. are they found on a single player game 
> or a multi-player game? 

Okay a golem is a brainless humanoid made from stone that a player 
conjures up.  You basically cast a spell of golem or read a scroll of 
golem and a golem will appear.  the golem will seek out monsters and 
bash them up for you! 8-)
you have slight control of the golem as the golem will walk in the 
direction you are facing UNLESS it is seeking out to bash on some 
poor monster that it sees.  the golem can die too, the golems 
umm...hitpoints is equal to the casters maximum mana at the time of 
casting.
The golem will die if it gets beat up on too much or if the caster 
leaves the level, the golem stays alive even if the caster dies, it 
dies when you LEAVE the level (eg restart in town)

Warriors were also referred to as golems as some warriors tend to 
play brainlessly and run off into mobs but that topic was discussed 
earlier and I'll say no more about it.

Golems are really handy as it is like having a slower moving warrior 
along side you.  eg in Hell/Hell against triple immunes.  Golems are 
also known to clear out entire levels of the church while the player 
that casted it Eats a BIG MAC...;)
