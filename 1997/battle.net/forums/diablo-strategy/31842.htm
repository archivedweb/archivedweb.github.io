> Is magic worth it for a warrior

I have found magic for a Warrior to be a useful, but not essential 
tool. More than anything else, it can just break up the monotony --a 
fireball in a pack of witches can cut down the time you spend chasing 
them around. If you happen to die, telekineses can be invaluable in 
getting your gear back. What I use magic for the most is healing. 
Once you've reached around level 20 (I'm currently level 39), money 
becomes plentiful, and space is what is most valuable. I carry only 
full rejuvinations on my belt to save space. Each full rejuvination 
will give me one regular full-heal, and two magic full-heals. It's 
like getting three times the space on your belt! I'm sure there are 
some Warriors around that have a mana level high enough to squeeze 
out even three magic full-heals or more.
