Well - Let me start by saying that the Dreamflange raises your spell 
levels by one, besides giving +30 to magic and +50 to mana (I think). 
 As far as a melee weapon goes, Mages are not supposed to be going 
hand to hand.  Their strength is lower than the rogue or warrior and 
in most cases so is their armor class.  This is not to say that I 
haven't seen mages with armor class over 200, because I have.  
Generally, hand to hand combat for a mage is deadly.  If you must use 
a weapon to fight with, I prefer a Kings sword of sorcery.  I use the 
'flange because of the 110 point increase to mana (lifeblood to a 
mage).
Now - aside from using items to raise your spell levels you must read 
all the books you can (obviously).  The highest you will be able to 
go is level 15.  Once you reach spell lvl 15, the only way the 
increase your spell levels is through the use of: Naj's Light 
Plate(armor) - +1 spell levels; thinking cap - +2 spell levels; Angel 
or Arch-Angel staves - +1 and +2 levels respectively; or the 
dreamflange +1 spell levels.   The highest legit spell level is 20.
