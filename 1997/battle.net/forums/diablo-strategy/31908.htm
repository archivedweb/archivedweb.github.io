very well. I got into trouble and Hit F9 which put up the 
"crapcrapcrap..." message. She replied "I'm not crap! you 
are crap!

I am really getting trashed at this point, not even enough time to 
teleport, drinking mana directly out of my inventory. I pound F9 a 
few more times frantically which she responds harshly to agian.

Nearing Death, I frantically pound all keys and up comes the "DIE 
PK DIE" by accident. This was too much for her. She responded 
with a few quick comments then left the game! Unfortunatly, another 
person entered the game, saw the DIE PK DIE sign and promptly flamed 
me and left.

Needless to say, I died. With noone else in the game, I started back 
down naked and after about 5 min of luring creatures out 1 at a time 
with my bow, got my armor back.
