Nicely detailed request.  Good job.
1. The caves are hard for a level 16 rogue.  As you already wrote, go 
very slow.
2. Lose the armor.  You don't want to be shining +40% light.  You 
become a walking beacon.
3. Lose the empryean band.  Your ring of stability already gives you 
hit recovery (Not cumulative).  For the +20% light, look at 
number 2.  +2 to all is minimal.
4. Use the gold bow of shock.  Better base damage, better to-hit 
percentage, and lava lords and mud runners are susceptible to 
lightning.
5. Use lightning spell against monsters.  One or two can knock out 
three to six, depending on how they are lined up.
6. Since your vitality is low, get the mana shield scroll from Adria 
if possible.  This will allow you to double your hit points.
7. Nice amulet.  Keep it.
8. Sell two out of your four bows, and use the money to buy a better 
armor.  Even a normal armor with base AC over 18 is better than the 
one you have.  If your strength is too weak to wear better armor, you 
may have to forgoe your amulet, until you level up or find a 
ring/armor that increases your strength.  If you find the armor, wear 
the amulet to wear the armor, and then take off the amulet.  The STR 
bonus on the armor will allow you to keep the armor on.

Hope these will help.
