Soulburners.. I call them Manaburners.. that's what they do!
If you are on normal or nightmare difficulties, lightning... lots of 
it. Maybe toss in a golem. 
Hell diff. is another story... I know it sounds funny, but I carry a 
King's Axe of Haste (battle axe style) just for bashing stone cursed 
baddies. I solo hell / hell quite a bit, and the key to surviving 
levels 15 and 16 is the old stone and bash. Move slow, peek around 
the corner to get a few at a time to come after you, as soon as you 
see them, stone curse and WHAM! You can throw in a golem if you want, 
but a pack of Soulburners will reduce him to rubble in seconds.
I am the only sorcerer I know that uses an axe, and I get funny looks 
for it... BUT I have made it to level 42, and 43 is right around the 
corner. If you have a high damage sword, you can use a shield and 
that instead, but I prefer to do damage really fast. Just don't 
attract the attention of too many at a time, and you'll be fine. 
Ok, this was too long.. so have fun.
