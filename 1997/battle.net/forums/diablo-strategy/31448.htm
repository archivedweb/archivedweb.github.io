Hi Eric_A_Warrior,

Well, Valor (Level 5) some people have trouble finding the 3 
bloodstones to put on the pedestal.  the first one is right there and 
when you put it on it opens a room nearby that was not there before.  
More Horned Demons to fight, place the 2nd stone on the pedestal and 
another room you could not get into opens nearby, more horned demons, 
place the last stone, another room opens up, MORE horned demons then 
you get some pretty good armor.  Just try not to let those horned 
demons ram you, keep moving around.

The other quest sounds like chamber of bone.  There are stairs to a 
mini-level (on 6th floor) where you I think fight more Horned demons 
and Unseen monsters.  If you have not seen (hehehe) these monsters 
yet, you usually won't until they are right next to you attacking 
you.  Just don't let yourself get surrounded by them, they are not 
hard to kill in smaller numbers.  There are I think 2 levers to pull 
and then you get into a room with TONS of skeletons and I think 
Unseens.  A good tactic here is do not jump right in the room, stand 
just outside the doorway and take them on a couple at a time.  (or as 
a mage, I prefer casting a firewall or two just inside the door - 
that's fun!)
The end result of this quest is some magic items in a couple of 
secret rooms opened by the levers and when you read the book at the 
end of the room, you get the guardian spell.

Hope I helped, have fun.
