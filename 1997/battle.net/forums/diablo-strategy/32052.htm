Guardians are very hard on rogues.  Not only do we get
hit by their fireballs (both directly and "splash" damage it 
seems),
but three or four can fill the screen with so much moving stuff
we can't see to aim (and _nobody_ likes that! :-) and worst of
all, every blooming critter in this room and all surrounding
rooms comes charging out at you all at once.  Then Mr. Hotshot
mage panicks and starts making like Nintendo Kid with the fire
button set for Chain Lightning.  

Great.  Now your 150-200 hp lvl 30+ rogue has lightning, 
fireballs, _and_ monsters beating on her simultaneously, plus 
the screen is so cluttered she can't see what's going on.  I do 
not find this to be a very comfortable (or pleasant) situation 
to be in.  My mage and I had several intense discussions 
about this strategy before I was able to convince him that
this was a strategy best left for solo play.
