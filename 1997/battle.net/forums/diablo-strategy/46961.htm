> Does increased armor class simply reduce the chance that you will get 
> hit, or does it also reduce the damage when you get hit? Also does it 
> help at all against magic/fire/lightning or are you better off going 
> in with max resistances at the expense of high AC? 

Well from what I learnt...The chance of your opponent hitting you is:
<their chance to hit> - <your AC> = Chance they will 
hit you.

From what heard and from experience I think that AC does minimize the 
damge you take as well.  I know this cause I played sorceror with 
APOS and NLP, I saw that with NLP the hits I took really hurt!

As for magic/fire/lightning, go for max resists...I think I heard 
someone say that if you had 0 resist, then the attack does damage 
your armor...I like my resists so I've never tested this out.  But if 
I were you I'd stick to max resists...a few obs or jade items would 
do that for you 8-)
