Upon reading about a possible problem with monsters with long range 
attacks, i would suggest that a warrior would try to max on the 
resistances and get a high ac ASAP.  I personally endorse stone curse 
(cast 2 maybe 3 times from full mana ball)as a must have against the 
long range attacks.  This comes from some of the experiences that I 
have had in the past with soul burners, where low resistance means 
death and the lack of stone curse could lead to a lot of burners 
frying you in a matter of seconds regardless of ac.  Of course you 
also want to make such that you have a good weapon also, dispatch of 
baddies quickly thus minimizing the damage from the monsters not 
stoned.   U don't have to rush into rooms to battle thing that have 
long range attacks, if possible draw them out in smaller groups.  
Hope some of this helps and is somewhat coherent.
