 Well, if you only took 1 zodiac ring, that's pretty good will power 
alone-most people who get "helped out" end up with godly 
plate of the whale, a windforce, and full zodiac jewelry by level 2.

 My advice is-stay clean.  Start an IPX or modem game by yourself, 
start at the top and work your way all the way down-no stopping.  You 
should be at least level 20 by the end of the caves.  Pick up 
EVERYTHING you find and hock it for cash, and only buy what you need. 
Don't screw around with books, just get a good bow, helmet and armor.

 If you don't have alot of free time on your hands, play through 
single player 1 time, then transfer into multiplayer.  

 Whatever you do, avoid other players as much as possible-you get 
less exp if you fight with other people, and you get tempted too 
easily when higher levels try to help.

 I've found through experience that if you are pure, you tend to get 
better stuff later on, but maybe that's just me.
