On Thu Jul 17 15:12, Matt wrote:
>  I was wondering if only the sorcerror can get all his spells to 
> level 15 or above or can rogues and warriors get there spell levels 
> that high too on all there spells?

The other two classes can get 15th level spells but it is a lot 
harder.  The warrior maxes at 50 magic and the rogue maxes at 70 
magic.  The most possible additions to magic would be a helm, armor, 
and shield of sorcery (+20), a weapon or wizardry (+30), and the 
three jewerly of wizardry (+30) for a maximum boost of +180 giving 
the warrior 230 and the rogue 250.  Most books eventually hit 255 
magic to read (happens at lesser spell levels for stronger spells) 
meaning  these two can not read them anymore.  However, there is a 
shrine in the game called the enchanted shrine that reduces one spell 
a level and increases all others one level making it possible to 
obtain the 15th level spells by these classes.
