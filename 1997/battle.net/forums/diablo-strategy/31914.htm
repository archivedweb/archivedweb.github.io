Just a post about a subject which surprized me.  King's sword of 
Haste might just be the best sword in the game.  But it isn't THAT 
rare.

I say this because of the numerous sources you can attain it.  I 
bought my first (and best) off of Gris.  King's bastard sword of 
haste.  Then, a month or two later, Wirt offered my a King's Sabre of 
Haste.  I didn't buy it.  Then, the following night, (2 days ago) I 
found a King's Sword of Haste in Hell/Hell.  It was the 4-12 version, 
but I dumped some rings to keep it in my inventory for a while.

So for all of you looking for a Legit KSOH, just look everywhere.  
I've found them in the 3 most likely places.

Incidentally, here's the weapons I now carry.

Dreamflange
King's Sword of Haste (bastard- 99% to hit and 167% damage I 
think)
King's Sword of Haste (4-12 damage version)
Obsidian Sword of Haste (4-12 damage version 39% resistance to 
all)

Not bad, huh?  I'd like to have a King's Bastard Sword of the 
vampires, but my mage isn't very lucky at finding vampire stuff.
