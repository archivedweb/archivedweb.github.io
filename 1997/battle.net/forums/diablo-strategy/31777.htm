Nobody talks or asks about this unique staff very much, but I found 
another one today and I was thinking about just what a cool staff it 
realy is.  For those who haven't seen one, its stats are:

+20 magic
+10 dex
+20% resist all
-25 hit points
57 teleport charges

I love this staff for several reasons.  One obvious reason: the +20 
magic helps for reading books (with the Puzzler and 2 rings/amulet of 
Wiz, helm and mail of Sorcery, I can get my magic up to 212, which is 
all I need for a rogue to max out all my spells).   

But, more importantly, the 57 Teleport charges let me fly around Hell 
like a mage on steriods.  I can whip around the levels in no time 
flat, and I don't have to drink a drop of mana. It's great!

When I normally play games in Hell (without using the Puzzler), I 
make sure to kill every beast and open every box on levels 13 to 16, 
because you never know what you'll find.

BUT, after a normal game or two, when I'm about ready to pack it in, 
I now make it a habit a do a Laz 'speed' run with the Puzzler in hand.

It rocks!  It gives me a different experience than I normally have 
and gives a huge element of surprise to the game.  I whip around 
levels 13 & 14 until I hit the stairs (or unless I spot a unique that 
needs killing).  When I get to level 15, I keep hopping until I'm 
right next to Laz's lair, then I switch the Puzzler for my Eaglehorn 
and rush in firing like crazy, while I keep the fingers of my left 
hand hovering over the number keys as I quaff down mana like a drunk 
on a hot day to maintain my shield.  Pure adrenaline!  

(One time, I teleported right into the lair, forgetting that the 
Puzzler does not fire arrows--it was not a pretty sight.)   :)

So, next time you find a Puzzler, don't just sell it.  Try it out for 
size and make a speed run; it can add a lot of fun to your game!
