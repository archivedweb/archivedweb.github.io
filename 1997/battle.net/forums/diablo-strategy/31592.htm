Hi,

I play a Rogue but often fight with a (Legit - bought it off Wirt) 
King's Sword of Haste and Stormshield.  For one thing, those clowns 
like to circle around you stopping long enough to spout fire.  
Eventually, they will close in and attack melee (hand to hand).  Try 
this, if you fire resistance is good (but it doesn't have to be), and 
you are confronted by a horde of them --get to a corner a.s.a.p., 
preferably one that has one of those hornish stalagmite doeys 
sticking up out of the ground, where you can only be attacked by two 
or three at a time.  When they close in to melee, Stone Curse the 
closest.  Leave them alone as they block the way for others.  If you 
are free from melee, cast chain lightning if you can as much as you 
can, then hotkey back to Stone Curse and repeat the process.  The 
Balrogs take damage from lightning.  Between that and Stone Curse and 
your trusty blade (or bow) you can put them in their place --six feet 
under.  Good hunting!
