I have Demonspike and I love it, but the Awesome Plate is definitely 
the way to go, especially for Hell/Hell games--there, you need the 
extra AC, and the -6 damage is nothing compared to the total damage 
from each hit.

The Demonspike is cool, but the extra 50 AC make a difference, 
especially if you don't need the 50% Fire resist that comes with 
the Demonspike.  I'd go with the Awesome Plate.
