     As mentioned above dreamflange is in general the best weapon for 
the sorceror. However, Archangel's staves (in particular of wizardry, 
teleport, and apocalypse) are popular to some role playing purists 
and 
some duelers (for situations where your armor isn't good enough to 
stop a very well equipped rouge or extremely well equipped warrior 
anyways you might as well go for the full throttle offense and get +2 
spell lvls instead of just the +1 for dreamflange.) The weapons 
warriors like (king's swords and such)are not very effective for 
sorerors, but can be helpful in the last 2 levels of hell/hell since 
there are monsters immune to both fire and lightening there. In that 
case try to get a good haste weapon since this is nearly the 
equivalent of doubling damage for the slow swinging sorceror. So, 
basically, you want to keep a few weapons in your inventory for 
different situations.
