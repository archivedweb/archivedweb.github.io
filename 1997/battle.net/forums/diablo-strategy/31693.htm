All of my answers assume nothing is duped and all items were gained 
legitimately.  My basic philosophy is that if it fits into the realm 
of diablo it is legal.  If you were traveling with a much more 
experienced warrior who took mercy on you and let you be his 
companion then so be it.

> #). Is it Cheating to accept Gold from another player?
No.  If I am a poor traveler I will accept gifts from any generous 
player.

> #). Is it Cheating to accept Items from a higher char?
No. If they want to arm me and protect me so be it.

> #). Is it Cheating to have higher level chars buy items for you?
No.  Ever have your big brother buy you a six pack?

> #). Is it Cheating to have higher level chars give lower level chars 
> elixers?
No.  Same answer as above.

> #). Is it Cheating to clear levels for lower players?
No.  I walk through the carnage of my warrior companions often.   Or 
maybe sit back and let them fight until I get somewhere I like.

> #). Is it Cheating to Start games with only the intent to check Wirt, 
> Adria or Griz for items?
No.  The items are random.  Whatever it takes to find them.

> #). Is it Cheating to use Deye in chat to look at other peoples stats?
I am not familiar with Deye.  I don't mind anyone knowing my stats 
though.

> #). Is it Cheating to allow lower players to portal into areas they 
> could not regularly access?
No.  They are taking their chances of being killed but that's their 
business.

> #). Is it Cheating to create chars whos only purpose is to hold extra 
> items?
No.  Sidekicks are pretty common.

> #). Is it Cheating to transfer items found by 1 char to another char?
No.  Is it out of the realm of imagination for someone to give their 
friend a useful item that they no longer need?

> #). Is it Cheating to Pk with the Nova spell?
No.  I don't like Pking but people killing people is reality and who 
is to say that a gun is ok but a bomb is not.

> #). Is it Cheating to Portal Kill someone?
New to me.

> #). Is it Cheating to Teleport in town?
No. 

> #). Is it Cheating to backup chars against system crashes?
No.  As long as that is as far as it goes.

Again, when I play Diablo I step into a fantasy world.  Without the 
use of outside utilities, whatever I can get away with is just the 
way it is.  The realm itself doesn't seem to be a law abiding 
society.  It is a drunken, creature infested, blood gurgling free-for-
all.  I will beg, steal or borrow from any adventurer I come across 
to increase the chances of my success.

Overlord
