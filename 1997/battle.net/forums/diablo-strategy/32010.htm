     Well, give you some data:

AC = DEX/5 + AC of amour, helm and shield
basic Chance-To-Hit = 50% + (DEX/2)%
Damge of a rogue = (STR+DEX)*level/200 + weapon damge
Close damge of warrior and mage = STR*level/100 + weapon damge
Range damge of warrior and mage = STR*level/200 + bow's damge

Chance-To-Hit is compare to target's AC.  Higher AC means less chance 
to hit by enemy.  And you need higher CTH to fight the enemies with 
higher AC.  There is something like:

real chance to hit = (CTH - target's AC + ??) * ??%

To learn more, you should go to Diablo information web-site.
