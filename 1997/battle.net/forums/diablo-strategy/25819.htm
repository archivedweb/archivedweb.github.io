As for the elixers; when your character gets around level 26 or
27, the witch will randomly have elixers of str, mag, and dex.
I have not seen the witch sell any elixers of vit, but those
show up in the dungeon occasionally.

Good items from Gris and Wirt; that is a tough one. I think
better stuff starts to appear on nightmare difficulty. I bought
a blessed shield and an obsidian crown of harmony from Gris 
on nightmare difficulty. I forget the difficulty but I saw
an emerald mace at Wirt's one time. I do not always check
with Wirt, but the last time I really got lucky and Wirt 
had the covetted King's sword of Haste for 69k. I did happen 
to have 72k or so, so I jumped all over that. I was 
using a Lord's blade of haste that I found somewhere in hell 
on nightmare difficulty. That was also in a nightmare game.
