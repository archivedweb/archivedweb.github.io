The key here is FIREBALL.  At least in normal, and to some extent 
nightmare (though I've only been there twice).  All but one of them 
is vulnerable to fireball, the EXCEPT being soul burners (yellow and 
blue bitches : )  They require lightning.  With fireball and all the 
others, you target the one in the middle, the explosion usually kills 
all the ones around her in a single hit.  (of course your fireball 
has to be fairly decent).  With the yellow, you get them into a big 
huddling group in front of you then CL (chain lightning) away.  This 
sucks down mana fast, but is faster than tracking them down one at a 
time with a sword.
