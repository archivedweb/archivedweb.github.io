Monster-kill: when a player brings a pack of monsters
to your area in an attempt to get you killed (and steal
your goodies).

When you are pk-ed, you only lose half your gold and an ear.
But when you are mk-ed, you drop your equipped items.

If you suspect this is happening to you, best to restart in
town or leave game.
