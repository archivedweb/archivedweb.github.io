Two posts here suggest that using items handed down from a
higher level character is cheating. This is not true.
Using duped/hacked items is cheating, of course. But even if
Diablo had been completely bug free and Enigma's trainer
had never existed, high-level characters would be free to
give low-level characters powerful items. It's an inevitable 
consequence of the multi-player aspect of the game.

Now, you may choose to equip characters only with items that
they find themselves, just as you may choose to play ironman 
Diablo, or you may choose not to PK. You can play the game
anyway you want -- but the opposite choices don't constitute
cheating.

"PKing is not cheating, cheating is cheating."
