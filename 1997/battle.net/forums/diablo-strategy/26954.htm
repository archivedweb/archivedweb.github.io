     Well, I don't know much about AOL, but if you have problems 
running BNET, try this

1)Go to Dial-up Networking in "My Computer"

2)Enter "Make New Connection", unless you already have a 
connection to your server.

3)If there is no connection to your server, make one.

4)Double-click on the connection

5)now run Diablo and go to BNET.

If this doesn't work, You will need to Email Blizzard's Tech support. 
They should give you very detailed instructions. And, yes, BNET is 
worth the trouble. If it isn't responding, it means they are handling 
MANY connections, or they are temporaraly down.
