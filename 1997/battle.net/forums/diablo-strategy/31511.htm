On Mon Jul 14 17:30, Merrilee wrote:

> Which monster do you hate the worst.  I'm not looking for the 
> toughest monster in the game just which one to you hate fighting 
> because no matter what you do or how strong you get they just seem to 
> find a way to cause you problems.

Yeah, I have to agree - those spitters are simply annoying, sometimes 
life-threatening. That's why I nominate the Lava Maws to be the 
monsters I hate the most. Not only because they spit and drop their 
load where I stand. Not only because they produce the absolutely most 
annoying sounds in the game. But also because they are one of the 
most dangerous monsters. As you said, if you run into a group of 
them, they spit away most of your hit points before you can even 
think about hitting the "1" key to gulp a potion. And if they 
get near you and they are a group of more than say two or three, they 
can hold you and start ripping away like there was no tomorrow. They 
are also difficult to beat with every kind of character. The Blood 
Knights are difficult to beat with a mage, but no problem for a 
warrior. The Soul Burners / Hell Spawn / Snow Witches are difficult 
if you are a warrior, but as a rogue you only laugh about them. But 
the Lava Maws stand out as the monsters _everybody_ hates to face. 
And I _hate_ the sounds :-)
