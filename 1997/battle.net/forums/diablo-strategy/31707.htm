I've recently started over from playing Warriors (Kumquat L31 85% 
Legit, Kol'dahnn L11 100% Legit) to playing a sorceror, as rogue 
isn't my style....   (currently going by the name of Jadoor, be nice 
to him)

When you play a sorceror, you are playing a very specialized type of 
character, even more so than the warrior. Raise Str and dex JUST 
enough so he can hit something and maybe kill it when he runs out of 
mana, but you should concentrate mainly on Magic and Vitality (favor 
magic at a 2:1 ratio) This will allow you access to LOTS of mana, and 
you will be able to read every book you are powerful enough to find. 
Raising your stats will be mainly accomplished through rings, 
amulets, and other magic weapons. I also suggest using the largest 
(STR requirement) magical sword you can once you find one instead of 
your staff until you are out of mana, and hotkey the heal spell.... 
use the sword to kill little stuff, and blow up the rest with a well 
placed spell.

If you wish to "have it all" in a character that fights well 
from far away, has good magic, and is no slouch in melee either... 
take rogue.. sorcerors just aren't built for that kind of development 
if you want to be a truly powerful mage.

Just My Two Pence.
