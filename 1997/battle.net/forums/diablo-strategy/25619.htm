Hi there. Eaglehorn is a great bow for novice rogues, because it 
gives an extra +to hit % bonus. However, when you reach around 
level 30 and start thinking about Nightmare and Hell games, a bow 
with more damage is needed. I think that the best bow is Windforce, 
because it does the most damage. Also, Merciless, Ruthless and Savage 
Bows are very good, especially if they have a suffix. I was using a 
Savage Long War Bow of Perfection for a long time (till I found 
Windforce)

A good way to find bows is to get your highest level character and 
just keep on going to Wirt. He has some pretty good bows there.
