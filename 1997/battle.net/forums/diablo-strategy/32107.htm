It could be that their King's Swords of Haste are so expensive 
because they are indestructible, instead of having the normal 
maximum durability (for a bastard sword it's like 50). The game 
would never offer an indestructible KSOH, but when you make it
indestructible you increase its value.

You can make a sword indestructible through two methods:

1. Hacking (this is the most common way)
2. Get the sword's maximum durability to end in the digit 5. This 
can be done by using the warrior's item repair skill, which can lower
max durability. Then use LOTS of hidden shrines, which can increase
the max durability by 10 each time. The item will become 
indestructible when the maximum durability is 255.
