Aahz - 

I think that you made some really good points here.  All in all, I 
think it was a good post for beginning players to read and absorb.  I 
have some comments to add.  Since I tend to play with my sorcerer the 
most (I have one Char of each class), I think that being a sorcerer 
gives a player a really good handle on what monsters have what 
resistances and immunities.  This, I think, is one of the most 
important aspects of the game (if not THE most important).  I have 
played with sorcerers when using my rogue, and had to yell at them to 
use the correct spell on whatever monsters we were facing (ie.  
trying to use guardian or elemental on soul burners!!).  In a 
hell/hell game, not knowing the monsters resistances or immunities is 
certain death for one or more members of any party, no matter what 
strategy the party intends to use.  I agree that using one of your 
strategies will lessen the chance that an accidental PK will occur.  
However, I cannot stress enough to players to spend the time to 
really look at what resistances or immunities each and every monster 
has.
  
Lastly, to all the players who think that mages simply teleport 
around and fireball you in the back: Those are the inexperienced 
mages! (or the PKs )  An experienced mage causes an accidental PK 
VERY RARELY! This is because we know the magical resistances and 
immunities of each monster that we face!! And if we don't, then we 
stone curse the bastards until we know!!

My $0.02

Silmarin Level 38 Sorcerer, lvl 35 rogue, lvl 28 warrior (nothing 
artificial)
