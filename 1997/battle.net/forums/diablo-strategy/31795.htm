     I sometimes see someone on b-net said he/she was killed, and 
asked another player to give him/her some stuffs.  Why he/she not to 
try to get his/her stuffs back?
     If you die in solo game, you should remember the position you 
die and restart in town.  Go down there again, make a portal far away 
from the stair and the position you die in.  Then go to the position 
you die, the demons will go to get you.  So lead them away, let them 
kill you again.  Restart in town again, you might reach to the 
position you die in and get the stuffs back.  If not, try again.  But 
when the portal and the stair both are blocked by demons, you fail.
    You must be careful.  This is a very dangerous and exciting job.
