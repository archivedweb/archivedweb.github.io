I agree that Baal (or whatever they call Diablo II) needs more 
classes.  I think it would be interesting to see a Paladin and a 
Necromancer added to the mix.  Also it would be very intriguing (not 
to mention add more variety) if your class progressed as you moved 
further into the game.  IE when you reach a certain level or complete 
a certain quest, you have the option to train under one of a few 
'masters' in town.  These masters could help the warrior find his/her 
way onto the path of the samurai or perhaps the holy paladin.  Each 
'path' would grant the warrior different bonuses or new skills, but 
once they start down that path they cannot start another (ie you 
choose samurai, you gotta stick with samurai for the rest of your 
characters life).
