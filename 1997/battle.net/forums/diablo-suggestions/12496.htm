> I have just purchased Diablo and want to play over an IPX LAN 
> network.  Do I have to have 2 CDs.  If not how do you 
> load a multiplayer game.

Stu,

In order to play a multiplayer game of the full version of Diablo, 
you must have a Diablo CD in each of the computers in the game.  
However, if you install the spawned version of Diablo on each of the 
computers in the game, you can play a spawned multiplayer game.  

If you have multiple computers in the same household, we do have an 
offer that includes purchasing extra Diablo CD's for a reduced price. 
 If you contact our Sales department (sales@blizzard.com), they will 
be able to give you the details concerning this.

Thanks,
