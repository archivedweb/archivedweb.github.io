Death2,

Do you mean battle.net?  You shouldn't have problems connecting due 
to the capacity of the battle.net servers, though th problem may be 
rooted elsewhere.

Make sure your modem is using these settings:
Click on Start -- settings -- control panel
Open Modem
click on Modem
select properties
select the connection tab
select Advanced
make sure that Use Flow Control is selected and set it to Hardware
Turn on Error Control -- select Compress Data
Click OK
click on the Port Settings button
click on the default button
click OK

If this does not help you connecting, then try doing so at a time 
when your ISP is not too heavily loaded with users.

If you are still having problems connecting, repost in the technical 
support forum or send email to support@blizzard.com.  Give us your 
system specifications, describe the problems you are having 
connecting to battle.net and tell us what you have done to try to fix 
things.
