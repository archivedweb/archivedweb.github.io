On Wed Oct 22 02:26, The_Retainer wrote:
> I think that player should not be able to block blows from behind or 
> at a penalty, it is silly to suddenly turn around and block a blow. 
> The light radius should be increased in front of the player and 
> reduced behind the player, it is impossible to see behind your back 
> after all.

It occurs to me that, unless D2 is reduced to a turn-based system 
(shudder), it would be almost impossible to keep calculating and re-
calculating where blows are landing on a target, whether the attack 
is from the shield-side, the front, the back or whatever.  Both 
target and attacker are in motion at all times, thus the 
implementation of this suggestion would necessarily amp up the combat 
calculations a great deal.  Remember, gang, we are talking about a 
fun game where some realism is sacrificed for playability!  The 
combat system presently assumes that the target's full AC and shield 
block percentages apply at all times for any attack.  To do otherwise 
would reduce combat speeds to sluggish slow-motion pillowfights.
