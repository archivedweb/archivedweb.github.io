Markus,
You need to contact your ISP (internet service provider) and make 
sure they are not restricting TCP packets on ports 116 adn 118 of 
their router.  If they are restricting these ports, then you will not 
be able to connect.

If you are still having problems, repost in the Technical Support 
Forum or send email to support@blizzard.com.  Give us your system 
specifications, describe the error you are getting when trying to 
connect to battle.net, and tell us what you have done to try to fix 
things.
