I'm afraid I disagree with you here, there could be a work around
with this dilemma you may be having regarding light radius. Why not
separate EXTENT OF VISION from VISIBILITY so as to remove your
current "disagreement."

With the separation I mean that the ability of the AI to see you
would be wholly different from your ability to see them. That would
mean they can see you even though your back was turned on them.
It would all depend on how far their EXTENT OF VISION is set.

Then regarding the other thing visibility key. Something lacking
in Diablo is the ability to surprise monsters or to sneak up on
them. Yes that would be possible if the ANGLE OF VISIBILITY is
considered. For example if a monster was facing towards the north
but you luckily approach him from the south the AI shouldn't be able
to see you coming because of their Limited angle of visibility.
That would enable players to backstab monsters and other players
since the players also have a limited angle of visibility.
