game, if there were no cheats and it was easier to strike back.

unfriendly" player that they are immediately switched to player 
attack mode.  This would prevent someone getting in 10 whacks while 
the defender tries to switch modes.

"Friendly Fire" is good.  If it were removed I feel the game 
would suffer.  An archer or mage should be required to think and plan 
before loosing hell amongst his friends.
