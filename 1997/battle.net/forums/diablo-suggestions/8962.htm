These are some good suggestions, folks.  The ability to change your 
weapon quickly is a good idea, but, with some practice, you should be 
able to change weapons quickly enough within your inventory screen.

Keep your secondary weapon in a handy position on your inventory.  
Then, when you need to change, hit 'i', click on the weapon in your 
inventory, click on your hand to equip it.  Then, if you need to save 
time, drop the item you were using previously (it should be on your 
cursor now), hit I again, and swing away!  It does take some work, 
but it also represents the time it would take a ready a new weapon.  
If you're good, though, it should only take a second :)
