Here's a quick one. (Kind of...) You may have to work out some stuff, 
because the info I'm going to type is copyrighted by TSR, Inc., but if
you did Diablo 2 like this, it would kick some MAJOR butt.

OK, now keep in mind, I'm only making a suggestion for characters 
right now. Now, here's the tip:

Make charcter creation/play like Dungeons & Dragons!!!!!
For example:

Character Stats go like this:

Strength - Physical power
Dexterity - Nimbleness, agility
Constitution - Durability, health
Intelligence - Knowledge, overall smartness
Wisdom - Common sense, perception
Charisma - Good looks, personality

Character classes: 

Fighter - Basic warrior. No magic skills, but gains magic resistance 
per 
              level. (ie 5% at first, 10% at 2nd.)
Paladin - Holy warrior. Starts of like a Fighter, but gains healing 
spells
               and special abilities vs. Undead.
Ranger - A Fighter the specializes in a type of terrain (ie. Forest,  
             Caves, etc.), and lives in peace with nature, defending 
his
              "area". Gains healing spells later on, and has 
a special
              empathy with animals.
Cleric - Basically, a healer, but with fighting abilities as well. 
Druid - A mix between Ranger and Cleric. Lives in peace and harmony
           with nature, not as much of a fighter, but more of a 
healer.
Thief - Sneaks around, steals, assassinates, spies, etc.
Bard - Jack of all trades, Master of none. Weak fighter, closer to 
Thief
          level, weak Theif abilities, can cast some magic later on.
Wizard - Cast spells, weak fighter.

Then of course, there's stuff like Fighter/Wizard, or Fighter/Cleric, 
etc.
And, classes use different stats: 

Fighter - Strength; Paladin - Strength, Wisdom, Charisma;
Ranger - Strength, Wisdom, Dexterity; Cleric - Wisdom;
Druid - Wisdom, Inteliigence; Wizard - Intelligence; Thief - 
Dexterity;
Bard - Dexteritry, Charisma, Intelligence.

As far as the characters themselves,  you've got races.

Human - Standard person.
Elf - Infravision, +1 Intelligence, - Constitution
Dwarf - Infravision, +1 Strength, +1 Constitution, -1 Intelligence
Half-Elf - Infravision

There are more races, but that's the basic ones.
