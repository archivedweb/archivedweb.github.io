Tomatokiller,

It might be faster, if you are looking for a low latency game, to 
find others in the battle.net chat channels with whom you have a low 
latency.  When you find some, join a game with these people.  Or, you 
might want to connect to battle.net at a time when your ISP is not 
too busy.  The speed of your ISP is a major contributor to your 
latency with others, and if you play during non-peak hours for your 
ISP, you should see this improve.
