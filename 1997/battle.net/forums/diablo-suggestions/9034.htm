I think something  diablo (and actually a lot of games ) are missing 
is the ability to play multiplayer games over more than one kind of 
connection at once..  Quake is the only game so far that does this 
(that I've seen)..  I'm talking about having, say, 3 players on an 
IPX network and having a 4th dial in over a modem..  or 2 players 
connected by a null modem cable and one over a modem..  all sorts of 
combinations.   Diablo got sortof close to this in its multiple 
serial link  support, but I think the ability for a player to dial in 
to a network game would be very useful..

This isn't only meant for diablo and its (possible) sequels, but any 
game, like starcraft or warcrarft 3(if it is being made)
