To make gameplay more realistic, I would find it very interesting to 
have the monsters pick up items left by characters and use them on 
the characters according to their inherent intelligence.

Couldn't you imagine the face of that poor cheater that drops his 
King sword of haste when a skeleton bops him with it?

I think critters in a dank, musty environment would enjoy shiny, 
metallic objects.  Even if not smart enough to use them on their foe, 
at least it would make a character keep a closer eye on his 
belongings.
