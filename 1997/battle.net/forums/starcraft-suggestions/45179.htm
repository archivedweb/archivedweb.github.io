 Ok, I KNOW someone has suggested somethin' like this already, but 
just in case it hasn't gotten to the code boyz...

  Bliz-- I don't really have time to type much in battle. And about 
90% of the messages you send in battle are redundant or 
predictable.
  How about letting players be able to type their Standard Messages 
BEFORE they play a game, and then store them in a 'Preferences' file 
somewhere. Then when they go to play, they can hit 'control + F5' or 
whatever, and the message gets sent automatically, no typing. With 15 
Function keys, that's 15 standard messages that they don't have to 
type again during battle (stuff like "I need help!", 
"They got air units", "enemies headin' your way", 
"Get him, he's in the NW (SW)(SE)(NE)(Center)", and various 
assorted taunts and counters to taunts :)

  The alternative would be to have a pull-down menu that would 
display your Standard Messages Menu, and you mouse-click to select 
one. Anyway, this sure would be a time-saver in battle, Bliz.
