On the subject of computers fighting it out to balance the play:
it would not work.

The AI's have to be programmed in differently depending on the race 
(zerg would not work well if you played them like protoss)
therefore the races may be balanced but the AI's might not be.  I 
personally think the AI for zerg would be vastly superior to an AI 
programmed for the terrans (think about it, it is easy to make the 
computer create large forces and manage reasources, yet very 
difficult to have it know when to move it's base, who to pick off 
with ghosts, etc.)  Therefore since the AI's are not going to be 
equal, a fight between two computers will not be equal even if the 
races are balanced for a human(as in player, not race) game.  

I hope you can follow my logic.
