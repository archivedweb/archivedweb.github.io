<<
1) What is an Archon actually a land or air unit?

They're a land unit (we want to give those flying units somewhere to 
flee :)

2) Can the Archon engage in combat since the Templers cannot?

Yes, it can engage in combat (in fact, so can the Templars).

3) Can High Templers or Archons cloak?

They don't have cloaking powers themselves, but if they are near a 
friendly Arbiter, they will be cloaked.

4) Are the Zealots and the Dragoon things the only land units for the 
Protoss?

No.

5) Since an Arbiter can cloak other units is ther a way to hide it so 
your fleet goes undetected?

Sorry, no.  Arbiters cannot be cloaked (not even by other Arbiters).

6) Do Terran buildings walk or fly?

They fly.

7) What is the reasoning behind only being able to control small #'s 
of units at any one time?

You might think this would make the game less fun, but we feel it 
actually makes it _more_ fun, and adds to the strategic options of 
the game.  Splitting your forces into smaller groups will take more 
advantage of the unique qualities of the different units and 
stimulates the growth of different skills.

8) Does StarCraft have nights and days?

Yes, there will be both day and night missions.

9) Will ther be a way to heal infantry and vehicle units that have 
been damaged?

yep :)

10) Can multiple Nexus's be built?

Sure can.

11) Does repairing units cost money or just time as in Dark Reign?

Repairing will cost resources for Protoss and Zerg players, though 
the Zerg will heal for free.


12) Will ther be a way to stay out of the rankings on Battle.net 
while you get started?

Sure!  There will be both ladder and non-ladder games available to 
Stacraft players on battle.net.

13) Will ther be any kind of award for winning tournements?

Tournaments usually include a reward for the winner.  For player-run 
tournaments, the reward will be whatever the players decide.

14) Can StarCraft be expected by atleast early 98?

Hm...we're actually planning on releasing the game this year.
