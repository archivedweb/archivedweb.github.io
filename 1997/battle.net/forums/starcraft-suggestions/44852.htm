The subject of blind-firing of artillery style weapons has, I know, 
been pretty much run into the ground, but here's a twist that I think 
would be interesting.   For realism's sake, it would make perfect 
sense to allow artillery or missiles or whatever to fire at any 
location within their range, with or without a spotter.   However, 
when any artillery unit fires, its position should be temporarily 
displayed, immediately making it a target for counterartillery 
barrages.   

This would force the players to keep their artillery on the move and 
to avoid mere random firing on the off chance that they hit someone.  
Also, this would be a bit more realistic than forcing spotters or 
allowing completely anonymous firing into the fog of war.   

Just my two cents worth
