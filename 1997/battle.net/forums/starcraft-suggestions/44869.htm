I saw this one too... It is true that the AIs would be different in 
strength. But like I posted before, you can pit the different 
computers against each other, and figure out WHY one won and the 
other didn't. Was it the AI fault? Was it unit unbalances? Was it 
another factor? etc... Blizzard can watch with fog of war off, on 
different computers, step by step what is happening. I'm not saying 
that by doing this all their balancing issues would be solve. Just 
saying that this would give them some insight to what they might need 
to do and what they might not have to do. Maybe they'll need to tweek 
one of the AIs or change the strength of a unit or change something 
else. This does solve the problem of having to be the player and the 
one to figure out what happen... Watching the computers fight, would 
give you more concentration power on what is happening and why.
