First off, I'm sure Blizzard would not leave the Zerg with only 
Mutalisks and Defilers to go up against fleets of Terran/Protoss 
starships.  I would think that every race (Zerg included) have at 
least two or three units that no one has seen.

As for the Avengers, their name implies that their purpose is to get 
revenge for the death of an Overlord (which seems like a pretty 
important unit).  I would expect them to die out quickly, living for 
ten seconds or so to avenge the death of their host.  They could also 
ensure that a player will need more than a little SCV to take out an 
isolated and otherwise defenseless Overlord.  Probably small with a 
nasty bite, kind of like interceptors.  I don't think that Avengers 
would be like a conventional spacecraft that you can amass in 
preparation for an attack (i.e. Wraiths, Battlecruisers, Arbiters, 
Carriers, every other air unit).
