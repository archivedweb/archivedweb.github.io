Yes, memory positions will be featured in Starcraft.  If you have a 
unit, group of units, or even buildings selected, you can hold down 
the CTRL key and hit a number.  Later in the game, you can then hit 
the number you chose to select the units you chose.

There will be a good deal of interaction in the installation 
missions, though I'll see what I can find out about the details you 
had questions about.

There will be 3 basic map types in Starcraft (installation, planet, 
and space), and each basic type will have its own tile sets.  You'll 
have to wait, however, to find out just how many tile sets each map 
type will have :)

Regards,
Pat N.
