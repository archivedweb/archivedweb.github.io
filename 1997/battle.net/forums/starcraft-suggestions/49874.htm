Question.

Has Blizzard considered the physics of shooting in space?

If a vehicle is struck in a non-combustible area (anything other than 
ammo of fuel areas) it would create a hole in the unit. That unit 
would then implode by the rapid depressurization. No fireballs, 
nothing spectacular to see.

However, if a unit is struck in a combustible area there would then 
be a combination of both implosion and explosion, because of the 
competeing forces.

Will units explode different ways in accordance with the nature of 
the wound?
