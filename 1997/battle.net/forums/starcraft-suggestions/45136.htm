OK.  This is what I think you should know in order to get your foot 
in the door of the gaming industry.  First of, you need to master 
C++.  This is THE language right now and if you're a high school 
student wanting to become a programmer, you should start as soon as 
possible.  Once you learn C++, Java should be fairly simple to learn. 
 They are pretty much the same.  Java is being taught at the high end 
Universities now so get your feet wet by buying a Java book and 
trying to absorb what you can before college.  A spokesperson from 
Interplay came to our class and talked about the industry and it 
seems as though these languages are hot now.
