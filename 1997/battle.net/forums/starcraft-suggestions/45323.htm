I'm sure the Battlecruiser will be pretty tough to beat (and, 
therefore, very expensive and difficult to acquire).  But keep in 
mind that those things aren't just going to be on luxury cruises over 
enemy bases... "Ahhh, there's a Nexus... ZAP!  Why not take out 
the Pylon? ZAP!"  

The Protoss as well as the Zerg (despite everyone's claims to the 
contrary) will each have strong air units to combat the Battlecruiser 
as well as (I'm assuming) some form of anti-aircraft defense.  I 
would also expect the Battlecruiser to have some kind of weakness; 
every "super-unit" does.  It might be slow, or very 
vulnerable to a certain weapon, something to keep it from being 
invincible.
