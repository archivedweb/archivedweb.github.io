I think the three races will balance out pretty well in one-on-one 
battles, but I wonder how things would work out in larger multiplayer 
games.  For example assuming that allies were using the same race I 
would rank them as such:

1.  Protoss (strongest)
2.  Zerg
3.  Terran

In other words a team using all Protoss would be very difficult to 
defeat in my opinion if they work well together to produce units.  An 
an all Zerg team would obviously be terrifying due to sheer numbers.  
An all Terran team?  I don't know if they'd be as intimidating.

Now I know all of this is speculation since the beta isn't out but it 
does bring up the point that the racial balance might be much more 
difficult to maintain as more human players join in the battle.  I'd 
appreciate comments on what a good team would be (i.e., which races 
make the best combo) and how you feel about the rankings above.

--Andrew
