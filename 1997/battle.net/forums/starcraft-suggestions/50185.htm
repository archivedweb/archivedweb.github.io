   A few examples.
Notice that the psy/cloak units for each race are very different.  A 
few of the Zerg units can burrow.  (No movement while underground)  
The Protoss Arbiter cloaks other units except other Arbiters.  The 
terrans have the ghost and the Wraith(wraith does cloak?) that cloak 
themselves.
The zealot,marine and zergling have different weapon types and 
probably different hp#.
The farms are quite different.  The Zerg's is a transport and can 
spot cloaked enemies, so a few must be close to the front of battle.  
I believe the Protoss and Terran are very similar to WC2 farms.
The tank and the ultralisk not that similar, other than both dealing 
massive damage.  If the ultralisk gets close to the tank, the tank is 
diced.  If the tank can keep the ultralisk at distance, the zerg is 
pulp.
