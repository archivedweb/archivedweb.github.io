What I mean by that is that the terrans seem to have all the 
advantages and almost no disadvantages.  
The protoss have the enormous disadvantage of needing the portals.  
Take those away in a surgical strike and you take away their powers.  
They also have the disadvantage of having fewer(though stronger) 
units.  Anyone who plays war2 knows that 4 or 5 peons can easily kill 
a grunt, with only one peon dying in return.
  On the other hand, the zerg have the disadvantage of needing creep 
to build anything,  If you can somehow trap them, they won't be able 
to build any more stuff(tower them in with the transformer 
tank/towers?)  The zergs also have the weakest units,although they do 
grow stronger with time.
  The terrans seem to hold all the cards.  Not only are they not 
restricted by the portals or creep building wise, but they can even 
move the buildings!  They also have what appears to be the most 
balanced fighting force, having enough numbers to take care of the 
protoss while having their units strong enough to withstand a zerg 
swarm.  They also have a lot of funky(yes I said funky:) abilities 
such as the aforementioned tank/tower transformer(is it an autobot or 
deceptacon?), the ability to cloak their ships, the ability to 
scavenge materials, etc.  
I know that the other 2 races(particularly the prooss) will have 
their own special abilities, but I have heard no solid info on what 
they might be or how they will counter the terrans.  Hopefully 
blizzard will reveal more specs soon:)
