Firebrand,  you have some really good ideas, I am in total agreement 
with you on improving AI, just dont make it so that your soldiers are 
fighting the whole war without any commands from you!  : )     The 
rally unit/point and bombing zone were both good too.

Ugly,  I like that idea about the flag that transmits a signal, but 
you could take it one step farther.  You could also make it so that 
you could have units attack that 10x10 area, say for instance (woo 
hoo! another example!) the enemy has a big buttload of towers (SC 
equvalent of course), walls, and archers.  Put  flag #1 on one of the 
towers, then bring in the heavy artillary.  Tell the artillary to 
"shell" flag #1.  The artillary will then try to do maximum 
damage.  This means that they might not necissarily take out the 
tower you have the flag on, but will try to make it so that their 
blast hits as many units as possible in that 10x10 area.  
Another example (I love 'em).  Say there 2 stretches of land in a map 
that either player MUST cross to get to the other's base.  Put flag 
#1 in one stretch, flag #2 in the other.  Select 5 bombers as squad 
1.  Repeat, and name this squad, squad #2.  Tell squad one to keep 
bombing (shelling) the area contained around flag 1, and keep coming 
back for more ammo.  Repeat with Squad 2, telling them to bomb flag 
#2.  I know this is kind of like the bombing zone idea, but a little 
more practical using the flags.  Please tell me what you think
