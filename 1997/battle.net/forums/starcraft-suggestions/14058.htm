I've seen several posts here on other real-time strategy games that 
people think are cool.

Whether it's an appropriate use of the forum or not, here's my 
contribution-

Check out MYTH, Fallen Lords, by Bungie.  
http://www.bungie.com/products/myth/default.html

This game is not really competition with StarCraft and Dark Reign. 
It's a real time tactical rather than strategy game. Basically this 
means that all the focus is on the actual fighting; no building or 
training or mining.  It has an advanced 3D terrain engine and 
physics, and a LOT of blood and gore and dismemberment.  You've got 
longswords and grenades in the same game- cool.

I also mention it because, like StarCraft, it is one of the few games 
that is being made simultaneously for the Macintosh.
