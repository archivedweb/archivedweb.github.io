	WarHaven (cool name, by the way...),

  The little pictures aren't the reason why you can group only 9 
units (actually, in SC, you'll be able to group 12, more or less, 
depending on the race.) 
   The real reason is that, as Bliz has stated, they don't want it to 
be a 'swarm' game. Selecting all your units at once and telling them 
to attack a target isn't very strategic. It takes no planning or real 
thinking. The Zerg need to swarm to an extent in order to be 
effective, so Bliz will let you group select a larger number of Zerg 
units than the other 2 races. But overall, they believe that 'mob 
wars' kind of bite, and I have to agree with them.
