> Q:Any idea how big an average 256x256 .scm will in (in bytes)?
> A:Athough the average size of a .scm file is difficult to gauge (a 
> one worker map would be much smaller than one with many units and 
> such), .scm files are relatively small (approximately 200-800k).
> 
> Q:Starcraft will work over Kali, won't it?
> A:Yup.
> 
> Q:Can Terran buildings land in the Creep?
> A:The rules for where Terran buildings can land are the same as those 
> for where they can be built; only Zerg can build on the Creep.  So, a 
> Terran player will find it necessary (and probably wise) to land 
> their buildings in "cleaner" areas.
> 
> Q:Will there be extra sound support for systems with high end sound 
> cards?
> A:There probably won't be extra support for the upper end sound cards.
> 
> Q:Can the Zerg infest Protoss buildings?
> A:No.
> 
> Q:Will infested units/buildings use different art?
> A:Yes.
> 
> Q:Will Zerg players have to research building infestation?
> A:Yup.
> 
> Q:Which Zerg buildings don't have to be built over Creep?
> A:The Hatchery does not have to be built on Creep, and can be used to 
> begin new Zerg bases (remember, the Creep acts as a source of 
> nutrients for the living buildings of the Zerg).
> 
> Q:Will there be steep ramps (e.g. one that links ground level terrain 
> to terrain two levels higher)?  
> A:Ramps such as these really wouldn't work with the way Starcraft 
> maps are set up (a "steep" ramp would have to link terrain 
> that wouldn't make sense --like the peak of a mountain and the ocean 
> floor).
>

> maps with respawning items/units.
