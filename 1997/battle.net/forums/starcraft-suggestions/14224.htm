     I think that Starcraft should have lots of different buildings 
to make the game more interesting, like fuel factories, for example. 
These would do the exact same thing as farms in Warcraft 2, feed your 
machines or warriors. There could also be fuel outposts that could 
replenish your energy. The good thing about them is that you can 
build one far from you town so that when your troops are attacked on 
a long trecherous journey, they could restore thier energy before 
confronting the enemy.  The fuel out posts should blow up after one 
use though.
ALso, metal forges, or research factories could be built. the metal 
forges are like blacksmiths in Warcraft 2,(they help update your 
weapons.) Research factories would have to be built in ordrer to 
research new weapons like missiles, landmines, radars, grenades,etc. 
Research facilities would sort of resemble mage towers in Warcraft 2. 
Finally, I think that your main base like a town hall, should be 
called the star command centre. There, you could build worker droids, 
instead of chopping lumber they would collect different resources 
like zinc, colbalt, getite, emeralds etc. there should be about 7 
different resources you would need to build different units.
