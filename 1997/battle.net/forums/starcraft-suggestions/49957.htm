Hello again.  Sorry to those that have read this message before. 
I have repeated it many times without an answer, so I guess I will 
just keep on repeating it! :)

If anyone has played Red Alert, they know what I am talking 
about.  If you have a bunch of infantry grouped together, and all of 
a sudden you see a tank coming in to squish all of your hard earned 
guys, you can press 'X' to have your guys run away in opposite 
directions, scattering them.  This is a great defense for explosive 
weapons which cover more than one square of terrain.  
     
	I think this command should be included in StarCraft, since 
games like Age of Empires are sorely lacking it. 

Also, Tom (DarkClaw on #StarCraft) came up with a cool idea.  Instead 
of just having your guys scatter and run, you could also instruct 
them to scatter and attack from different directions.  This would be 
good against units whose attack covers more than one square.

 Thanks for allowing my input!
