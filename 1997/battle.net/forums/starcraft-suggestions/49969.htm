If you already have an internet account with an ISP (and by posting 
here, you must have some kind of access), then you should be ready to 
play Starcraft over Battle.net. Usage of Battle.net is free once you 
are on the internet.

If you don't have internet access (you're posting from school, etc.) 
then you will need to sign on with an ISP that supports 32-bit 
internet applications, and the costs of that vary, although $20/month 
is relatively common.
