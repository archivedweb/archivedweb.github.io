I was reading one of recent posts dealing with balanced sides. One of 
the replies pointed out that, it is a common mistake that most 
players see someone winning because of the race they choose and not 
because of their skill level. This has happen a lot in Warcraft 2, 
were bloodlusted ogres are viewed as the winning team.  However it 
got me thinking, are you guys at Blizzard gonna have it to were two 
or more AIs can fight amongst each other? Cause if you do... this 
would be a great way to also test balancing issues. You can set up 
two computers to fight each other with different species, and you 
just sit back and watch the game. Find out which AI will win... and 
figure out why it won and if it's necessary to do some more tweaking, 
etc... Just a suggestion :-)
