I don't think you have anything to worry about Firebrand.  Game 
development is usually done by fairly small, tight-knit teams that 
know a great deal about the product there making.  In other words the 
team that's sweating LOTC is not necessarily the same group of people 
beating out SC.  Blizzard currently has about 75 people in their 
roster.  I would think that with that many people they could easily 
have 2 or 3 teams developing totally disjoint products.  The only 
bottle neck might be that these different games are marketed by a 
single marketing group(probably true).  This, however, would not 
effect the playability or development of Starcraft.
