Make it possible for sides to demand resources from other sides.

For example (use WC2, I'm not very familiar with Starcraft!) an 
orcish side who is under attack from lots of other human players 
might pay off an enemy to 'undeclare' war: give the player resources 
(wood, oil, gold). This is similar to the idea of Civilisation, where 
you can also pay people to go to war for you.

(better than WC2, anyway).
