I was wondering. Considering how all the races have air units, and 
there will be quite a few of them, what sort of things will shoot 
air? There should be some sort of AA guns, for all the races. These 
should also be about the same for all the races, so as to encourage 
land battles. In War2, the balance between air and ground units was 
perfectly equal. A large amount of guard towers was more than 
necissary to defend against a Gryphon or Dragon assault. I would like 
to see the same thing in Starcraft, because if air units are 
extremely powerful, then there will be almost no reason to build 
ground units, which would lead to a lack of different strategies. A 
game with one or two basic strategies (ex. Red Alert) becomes boring 
after a while. Please impliment some types of AA guns, or I'm worried 
the whole game will become air battles.
