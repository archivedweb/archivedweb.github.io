Why wait around for Starcraft to come out, when you can play Age of 
Empires right now.  I never thought there would be a day when I would 
say War2 wasn't the best game ever made.  Damn, Age of Empires is by 
far the best strategy wargame I have ever played.  Starcraft has 3 
races....Age of Empire has 12 tribes to chose from...each with their 
own strenghts and weaknesses...Coastal maps, isalnd maps, highland 
maps, inland maps....complete random map generation.....awesome 
mulitplayer playability.  I've only played one AoE game yet that had 
enough lag to make the game uplayable.  You just have to play this 
game.  Their are so many upgrades and buildings that you typically 
can not even come close to using them all in one game.  This is where 
all the strategy comes in.  You must determine from the multitude of 
options, what to spend your resources on.  You must exploit the 
strenghts of your tribe while exploiting the weakness of the other 
tribes.  What other game can have an 8 multiplayer game, and each 
player a different race/tribe?  None I know of.  Do yourself a 
favor....forget about Starcraft and get Age of Empires...It's in 
stores now people, unlike Starcraft...
