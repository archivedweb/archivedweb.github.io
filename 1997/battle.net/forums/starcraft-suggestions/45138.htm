"We don't have a release date at this time for Starcraft or the 
shareware/demo. It's our policy to avoid
specific release dates simply because we don't know what may pop up 
at the last minute. We are 
shooting for a release before the end of this year."

"...before the end of this year." is kind of vague, since 
tomorrow would qualify and so would December 30th.  Further, 
"shooting for" implies that you might not even release it 
before the end of this year.  How about announcing non release info?  
You must be able to see at least a week into the future, so you could 
announce that "Starcraft definitely will not be available this 
week."   If your foresight extends to beyond a week, you might 
announce that "Starcraft definitely will not be available in the 
next 20 days."  This way, we the Starcraft enthusiasts would not 
have to check your site on a daily basis to see if the Starcraft beta 
or final release was available yet.  Instead we could wait X number 
of days/weeks/months/years before checking again.  Think of all the 
Internet bandwidth that could be saved by millions of people not 
going to your site to see that Starcraft is STILL NOT AVAILABLE :(
