Ok, if you want other games to keep you amused... try Tanarus:

http://tanarus.station.sony.com/

3Dfx, as well has having much improved gameplay. You might want to 
wait until .90 is out before downloading.

It takes a while to get the hang of, so beware.

PC only I'm afraid, so mac users are out of luck there for now.
