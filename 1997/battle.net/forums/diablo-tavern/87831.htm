  Well, just recently, I have been playing in an area of B.net, which 
I have been avoiding for a number of weeks: public games.  And, much 
to my surprise, I have been having a reeeaaallll good time!  Usually 
when I did, and do, join a public game, they are filled with venomous 
cheaters, rude schmuck's, duper's, and, of course, pk's.  But, I have 
been lucky enough to bump into other player's that simply want to 
play the game, and also socialize on a normal basis. (Well, as normal 
as chatting on B.net can be.)  Of course, inspite of our attempts at 
naming our games "legit no cheaters," we still get players 
that pop in, and, right off the bat, ask me if I can dupe a Godly 
Helm (hello?!?).  Also, there is still the player that, which 
happened to us last night while we were chatting in town, runs ahead 
of us, and kills Diablo (grrrrrrr)!  Errrk...annoying---new game!  
Inspite of the many trials and tribulations one must go through just 
to have a teeny little honest game with other players, it can be 
worth it in the end!
