Nicely put Foxglove.  Diablo has grown into a community of sorts.  
It's not just a game anymore.  I was telling my wife all of this the 
other day and she was amazed.  She was especially amazed when a 
package arrived for me containing a modem sent from Umbriel (Tarot). 
"Why would he do that?" she asked.  I explained to her that 
my whole immersion into Diablo does not have so much to do with the 
game, but rather with the people who play the game.  I've made 
friends from all over the world and some of them I would even call 
"close friends".  We don't limit our discussions to 
"Godly plates and KSOH"...we talk about raising kids, world 
events, and just about everything you can think of.  It's the people 
that make the game (yes, the good ones and the bad ones).  The only 
way I can see this coming to an end would be if ALL my friends left 
for other games...and considering the number of friends (I'm counting 
all Snakegods as friends as well as others), that would mean that 
about 160 people would have to leave battle.net forever.  Sorry for 
ranting on like this but I feel really strongly about the people that 
I've met here...it's been one of the better experiences of my life 
and I don't see any end in sight.  (now it would be nice if we could 
all leave together to go and play...ummm...let's see....how 
about....Diablo 2!....hehehheheheh)  Until then, expect old Kariyafan 
to be puttering around here muttering to himself.
                                      Kariyafan
