If they have the other icon in place of the lag bars, it means that
one or both of the UDP channels needed by Diablo for internet play
are unavailable (usually a configuration problem with their local
ISP), and that player will not be able to participate in a game
through bnet signup, although they may still be able to play through
other means (direct connect, direct dial modem-modem, or local LAN).
