Croaker,

I know how you feel and I been through it before. He's my tip on 
leveling up. I'll use my Rogue for an exsample. When you level up, go 
into the caves for level 14-22 normal. After that, go into hell with 
an legit party. This way, you all got you backs covered. In hell, 
make sure you learn Fireball. Fireball, good armor and fast weapons 
is the key to surive hell. My rogue founded a Full Plate version of 
Glorius plate, an titan's Amulet, Topaz of the stars ring, Azuzra of 
the eagle ring, and a Mercless long battle bow (A member founded that 
in hell for me while I was in the caves). These items are good til 
level 15. Remember this.
