A Ballad of Three Newbies and the Butcher

"Wait, wait!" I say. "Let’s set him up."
"No, please!" The door unlocked; alas!
The warrior’s in the way, trapped...
The rogue’s stuffing arrows in his *ss.

"Argh! Fresh meat," the Butcher says.
One man is down, I need a res.

The Butcher, now, Dark Overlord,
Turns on the rogue. Her bow breaks down;
Without delay, she takes a sword.
As she gets hit, I hear her moan.

"Argh! Fresh meat," the Butcher says.
Two men are down; I need a res.

When he turns back, I start to flee.
I find the stairs, and he holds still!
His little eyes just stare at me
Testing the power of my will.

"Argh! Fresh meat," the Butcher says.
Two men are down; I need a res.

I stare right back, breaking the seal.
With fire spells the dungeon’s lit:
He’s gonna pay for those he killed.
I hear him groan as he gets hit.

"Ouch! Ouch! Ouch!  Ouch!" the Butcher says.
Then he is down; I need two res.
