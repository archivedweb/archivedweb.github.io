The experience needed for Level 49 is 1,082,908,612 XP.
The experience needed for Level 50 is 1,310,707,109 XP.

Therefore, the XP needed to go from Level 49 to 50 is
227,798,497 XP (it's late and I just did that without a calculator,
so I may be off).  Being generous, let's say 225 million XP.

Assuming 10 XP/kill, achieving Level 50 would require 22.5 
million kills.  Assuming 1 kill/second, this is 22.5 million seconds,
or 375,000 minutes, or 6250 hours, or approximately 260 days of
actual playing time (playing around the clock).

Assuming 10,000 XP per Hell/Hell run, and being generous and
estimating 1 hour per run, achieving Level 50 would require 
22,500 runs, or 22,500 hours, or approximately 168 days of actual 
playing time.

Using these assumptions, going from Level 49 to 50 in only two
weeks appears unlikely.  Of course, the assumptions about
XP/kill or XP/run could be off by one or more orders of 
magnitude (I have no way of knowing until I reach level 48 or so
myself).  I receive approximately 1.2 to 1.4 million XP per Hell/Hell
level now at Level 46.  If TheMan received 1 million XP per
Hell/Hell run, for example, achieving Level 50 would require only
225 runs.  Assuming 1 hr/run, this would require 225 hours, or
9 to 10 days of around-the-clock playing.

As I said above, it's late and my calculations may be off.  Any way
you look at it, however, achieving Level 50 in only two weeks 
seems very unlikely.
