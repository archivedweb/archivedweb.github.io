Well, I'm still technically running Diablo, (ah, the wonders of

roughly this order (my memory isn't what it used to be): Steel mace 
of the mind, steel ring of light, rainbow cloak, steel scimitar of 
maiming, buckler of blocking, silver ring of v-something (+15 vit). 
Please note that I'm only listing the items that are exceptional or 
that I remember :)

headed back to chat, where I saw Zudini as *cogitates briefly*

Zudini's friend died about 15 times (I was only resposible twice),
