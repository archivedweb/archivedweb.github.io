Greetings fellow Zodiac seeker,
I have found 12 Zodiacs since I started playing in Janurary, about 9 
of those have been found by either my first Rogue (deceased) or this 
Rogue, and the other 3 by my Sorcerer. I say this, because some say 
that Rogues have the best luck finding items. I don't know if there's 
any truth to this, however it has been the case with me. I didn't 
start finding any until the 30's and all were found in hell. The 
difficulty setting does not seem to matter, and the level in 
particular yeilds more than any other. About half came from chests 
and barrels, and most of the rest came from plain old run of the mill 
baddies. The exception was the other day, when I found 2 in the same 
game.. both were from bosses, one on 14 the other on 15. SO.. my 
advice to you would be to check EVERYTHING... and when you get to 
level 16, clear that whole level too, many people just go straight to 
Laz, and then to Diablo.. missing the chances of getting a really 
good find.

Good luck,
