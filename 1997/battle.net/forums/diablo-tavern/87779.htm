Well folks I learned some valuable lessons last night. I was hanging 
out with Tarot and decided to test some of my backup equipment. 
Keyla, Kargan and Sir.DesChenes were kind enough to come along on a 
Hell / Hell run with me. These are the things I found out myself.

(1) I cannot take the punishment from monsters I used to be able to. 
I lost my GPOW and my RC to the BBLB (Big Bad Lag Bag) and because of 
that my AC has dropped about 100 points. I teleported into a nest of 
Vipers and found out very quickly how much I relied on that AC. If it 
wasn’t for the help of Sir.DesChenes, Kargan and Keyla I may very 
well have lost my replacement equipment!  O;)

(2) I have a solo mentality. I have played, hunted and fought alone 
for so long that playing with others is going to have a learning 
curve involved. I didn't accidentally hose anyone, (I left that to 
Kargan and his wicked fireballs, Heheh) but I was in the way a bit. I 
didn't get killed by another player (the next one also deals with 
that) but I was kind of hanging back or rushing forward at 
inopportune times.

(3) I am optimized for PK. I found that when Kargan nailed me with a 
fireball (accidentally) I took it with little problem. When Keyla hit 
me with a few well placed arrows I noticed I took them with little 
problem. When I got three Knights on me I noticed I was swallowing 
Full Rejuve like it was water! I need to optimize for either Co-op or 
hunting and I suppose that means I'll have a few Pack_Mules designed 
to carry my various setup EQ for me.

These are things I will need to work on if I am taken in by Tarot.
