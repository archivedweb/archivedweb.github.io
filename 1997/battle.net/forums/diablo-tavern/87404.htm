Barbarian Blatherings------


I sit on the beach shaping walls without futures

Lovingly I compact the grains of my castle of sand
Only to look on the hill at the one of stone and beauty
Virtue requires that I admit there is no comparison
Even though both are of the earth, they differ in more than scale
Do I dare comment on the straightness of the stones?

I can only offer the praise of appreciation
There is no peer, there can be no critic!
