I remember the first time I was PKilled, what an experience.  My 
warriors name was Cheyenne, (named after the software company, hehe
so original), I was in normal/caves with another guy.  We must have 
played for an hour, side by side, and all of a sudden poof im dead.
No time to react, I had no fire resistance, and the sorcerer just 
fired away.  I wondered why this person would play so long beside me 
and all of a sudden just kill me.  Was it an accident?  Surely not, 
he had fired so many times!

After that, I would always ask, are you a PK?  I think I programmed 
that into my ini file for my F-keys.  Of course, I was so stupid to 
think that they would be telling the truth.  About a month later I 
was TKilled, and I wondered what happened?  How could I be laying in 
town dead?  wasn' that a "safe zone?"  

Man it was so much fun before the cheats and hacks, I remember a guy 
giving me a shield of the wolf, and I felt like he was a god to have 
such equipment to just bandy about.

Well, you know what they say, you can never go back, but it would be 
nice, just for a little while to have the amazement back again.
