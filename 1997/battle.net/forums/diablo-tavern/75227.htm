Theater of blood:

I will sell you Golden dreams
ones that you never seen
our world ruptures at the seams
within reality so keen.
  
Existence broadens our mortality.
Redundant relation as it may be.
Fantasy finds us lonely.
Will we learn from this journey.

I will sell you dreams broken.
For just a conscience’ token.
By the cherry blossom’ pollen,
When will your song listen.

Who am I to Dream!
As I lay by the temple.
Theater of blood a gleam.
A dying son only mortal.

I see you, proud and tall
you speak of Diablo’fall.
What of yourself, are you real?  
so finely coated with false steel.

I see all, the ones who redeem
and the ones no so clean.
What will become of us?
When time flakes as purple rust.

Layers upon layers
of all the players.
Young and elders
time for answers.

What of my golden reveries?
A world of honor and chivalries.
A land free and just.
Dream I must.

I lay in my mortality.
As you walk by me.
And speak I will die.
But before I fly.

Let me sell you a golden Dream.
