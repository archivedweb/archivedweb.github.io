Before cheats there was still the fear of PK, but you kept playing 
anyway. You found people you could trust, who'd show up in channels 
at the same time of day as you and adventure with them. Then you 
didn't have to worry so much- any PK would have to take out both of 
you. Also, people were generally sympathetic to a PK victim- come 
into a game saying you just got Pkilled, and people would offer to 
replace the gold you lost.

But there weren't so many PKs back then either. Most of the godmoded 
PKs we're so familiar with today would never dream of PKing without 
cheats. Cheats have given many otherwise cowardly people the ability 
to run around killing without fear of reprisal. Used to be you could 
go into half a dozen games in a row without getting attacked, now 
you'd be lucky to find two or three in a row.
