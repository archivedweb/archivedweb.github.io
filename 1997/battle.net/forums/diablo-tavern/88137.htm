OOC

In real life, I consider myself a bit of an optimist. Many of the 
things that go on in this world greatly sadden me, and I wish that I 
could change them, in a heartbeat. Unfortunately, such instantaneous 
changes are not possible. 

I find myself feeling more and more that it is up to all of us, as 
individuals, to do what we can to make the world a better place. 
Perhaps, through this mutual effort, we can make a difference. I see 
far too many people that blindly follow the "norm" in their 
thinking, and actions. I try not to let this get me down. It is hard, 
though, for me to accept the uncaring attitudes, and downright 
stupidity people some times exhibit. For example, the issue of race. 
Even thought this is a minor one, in the greater scheme of things 
that are wrong on our planet, it is one of the ones that I feel 
*must* be addressed first.

From the time I was little, I must admit, that I have never lived in 
a really racially diverse area. But, this aside, I cannot, at all, 
understand the anti-(insert race) attitudes of others who I meet, and 
work with. However, I see a light at the end of the tunnel. Perhaps 
this is just me, but I seem to see more and more understanding in the 
people of my generation of others' differences. Without a feeling of 
unity, as the human race, and not as people of one "color" or 
another, we can't really move forward, as I see it...

Another issue is the environment. I have strong feelings about this 
subject as well. For far too long, man has raped the earth of its 
wealth. It sickens me to see the efforts of corporations to build-up 
more and more of the untouched lands that remain. It saddens me that 
humans place the almighty "buck" above all else, including 
his/her planet's environment. Sooner than most of us imagine, we will 
all have to deal with these issues. A breaking point *will* come, it 
is just a matter of time... But, I cannot lose all hope. I will make 
an effort, in my own life, to help end this terrible lack of caring 
about the isolated blue world we inhabit...

It isn't so hard to see why we haven't been visited by others yet, is 
it? They probably pity us, or despise our lack of caring, so they 
don't bother with us...

Sorry, I am kind of rambling on here, but I had to get my thoughts 
down...
