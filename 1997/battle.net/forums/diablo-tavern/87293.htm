Chapter 11

Bone Madness.

The flame receded and the madness waited.
Lurking into the fertile dark of his mind
It hissed, it barked, a mad dog hoping to find.
A bone.

"Who am I?" he asked  to the panes frosted
"Not a king nor a man!" just to remind.
" But look at your finger! and tell me how fine.
shines.

Horror entered his soul and it screamed .
And it never stopped, that terrible yell
To this day I still hear it, a touch of hell.
I have seen his madness, it  gleamed 
of crimes.

Where the mortality of his small finger held.
The immortality of bone crackled and led.
Pointing at his sanity, the last shred
 shone

And then! was gone

The last I have seen 
was the scream keen 
into the night
Forever.. 


King of Bones:

Bony fingers pointing weakly away.
From their deseased guilt.
sunken words.
drunken herds.
On the virgin morn, first ray.
Awaken the ivory fleet.
They said we had to be heard.
mute and deaf
it never occured.

At noon, burning brows.
Bloody sows.
Quickly I look back
beyond the torture rack
To hazed visions and shadowy dreams
I stare arrogant and proud.
And what do I see?
Bony fingers, broken words
Trembling at me..
