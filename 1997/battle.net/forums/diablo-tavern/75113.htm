Since you can talk to and play games with people who are on different 
Battle.net servers, you can't really tell what server a person is 
logged on through (and you really have no need to).  Because of that, 
guilds/clans work just fine for Diablo.  They mainly give you a bunch 
of like-minded people to play with.  And no, they are not built into 
the game.  People have been forming them for months, and a few have 
been around since the early days of Battle.net (like Inferno and 
Tarot).

Publicizing guilds is up to the members.  They are each responsible 
for their own recruiting.  Many of the older ones aren't recruiting 
anyway, at least not actively, since they already have many members.
