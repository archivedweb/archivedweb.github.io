P.S. I do not remember Blizzard ever stating that you only got XP 
from killing uniques and bosses after Level 48 or 49, just that the 
number of monsters from which one could get XP was small.  I believe 
that the loss of XP occurs on a monster-specific basis (perhaps with 
some factor for level thrown in).  And by monster-specific, I mean 
monsters at a particular difficulty level.  As you kill more, you get 
fewer XP per kill until eventually you get no XP for that monster.  I 
have found that I can get XP from some monsters on a dungeon level 
but not from others (presumably, I have killed far more of the 
latter).  Thus, having all monsters give you a minimum of 10 XP per 
kill doesn't make sense to me, unless the XP calculation is somehow 
reset at Level 48 or 49.
