Hello,

To answer question 1:

I usually play a Warrior. I have found that on the harder 
difficulties especially, this is the BEST method. If you play another 
character type this method may be good at times but not always. I 
know it can be time consuming, but I have been able to beat every 
level on every difficulty by myself (solo) using this method.


To answer question 2:

If you are playing a single player game, not every quest is available 
in every game. If you are on level 12 that quest is not in your game. 
I'm not sure what level the Skeleton King is on in single player, but 
in multi-player it is on level 3. Single player it should be near the 
same level.
