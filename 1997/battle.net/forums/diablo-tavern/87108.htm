Telekinesis doesn't move you (that's teleport) it moves objects to 
you, and will place them in your inventory.  Say you had died and all 
your good stuff is laying on the floor, when you get back near the 
spot you died after restarting instead of having to walk up to 
retrieve your stuff (and get killed again), you need to only go far 
enough to see it on the screen, then use telekineses to get it back 
(I've had to do this several times).  I think it also opens doors.
