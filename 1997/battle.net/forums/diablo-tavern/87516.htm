Greetings b-net denizens!

Guess what I found here in Krakow? One of those newfangled "cyber-
cafes" that'll give you access to the web for just 2 zloty an 
hour (60 cents). They even serve you beer as you type. Now THAT'S 
service!

Anyway, I checked my hotmail account and got a mailbox-full of 
messages about some offshoot of Inferno. So I decided to use my 
remaining time to come and check out my favorite soap op ... I mean 
message board! Hey, it's something to help keep me entertained until 
I come back in August. Anyway, here are my thoughts on what I've seen 
and the choices I've had to make.

Quite a lot of people know that I've been deeply unhappy with Inferno 
for some time.

First of all, I'm sick of proven cheaters -- like Flux -- clinging to 
membership and making the rest of Inferno look like liars and 
hypocrites. I've seen Flux use hacks. I've seen her dupe. And yet she 
remains. Now I read that one of the supposed TO's cheats too? What 
happened to being a "legit PK guild" ... which is why I 
joined in the first place?

Second, I see that TheMan has reacquired Inferno membership. I have 
no particular problem with that. However, he has immediately reverted 
to the very same behavior that earned him his expulsion eariler: 
picking fights, backing away from duels and arrogating authority to 
himself. Apparently he has learned nothing.

Meanwhile, I find that Haplo is strutting around as a TO. Correct me 
if I'm wrong, but isn't this the same person who quit Inferno after 
TheMan was booted -- in favor of KoR? Even worse, according to 
Mikedog and Torquemeda, this new leader has found it necessary to 
cheat in order to save his bacon in a duel. Sorry, but these are not 
the type of "leaders" that anyone could reasonably be 
expected to respect!

Third, I see that the remaining members of Inferno are snapping at 
each other over the scraps of remaining "power". Think what 
you will of Flux, but who can read Haplo's post "putting her in 
her place" and not be nauseated at its tone of childish one-
upmanship? Gee, I guess this is too far beyond what "little 
people" like me can understand.

Issues and petty conflicts like these have had me on the verge of 
quitting Inferno for months, but I have always been restrained by the 
desire not to hurt those good, legit and fun people who would remain.

Now I find that nearly all those great people who kept me in Inferno 
have shed their old, somewhat tarnished affiliations in order to 
create the new PoC -- players like Morte, Tracy, Fist, Elijah, 
Curunir, SnowRabbit and Torquemeda. The PoC has also attracted some 
pretty impressive former "independents", such as Eugene (!), 
Blz and Possum.

The players in the PoC seem to have grasped that this is a GAME ... 
you know, a fun thing to do with people you know and like. No tinpot 
dictators. No "master plans". No disgusting little power 
games.

So it turns out that there really isn't that much of a choice for me. 
Toss me a robe, Brothers! Await my return in August, for then we 
shall preach the true meaning of Chaos!
