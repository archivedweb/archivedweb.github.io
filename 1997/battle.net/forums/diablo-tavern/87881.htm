If you were playing in single player mode, jump in the crack behind 
Pepins house in town, that will take you to lvl 13. Take the stairs 
down to 15 and stand in the middle of the pentagram to port to 16. 
Then you can continue, be sure and set a port first in case you die 
you'll be able to come straight  back. IF you were in muti-play on 
the net your game is gone. Start another game, jump in the crack to 
13, fight your way down again. I never see the red portal in multi-
player mode. I have also had my portal move like you. Bugs suck. Luck 
to Ya.
