    Yes, there are a range of buildings you can buy, from a tent to a 
castle.  The only problem is that castle cost about 350,000 gold 
pieces (and that is MUCH harder to get in Diablo)  In fact, I'd be 
willing to bet it would still take at least a month for a group of 
people to save up enough for that (you have a bank account to save 
money in).  You don't actually buy the building, at first.  First you 
buy the deed to the building, which is in your backpack.  Then you 
choose the spot of land where you want to put it and you use the deed 
and you place the building when you want to.  There is a nameplate on 
the door that you can write on (1 time) and a key by the front door 
too.  You can make copies of the key to give to friends.   That is 
about the whole scoop on buildings.
