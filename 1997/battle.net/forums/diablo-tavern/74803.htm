Ok, nice list but i got more than just one thing to
add, so here's my Diablo Christmas wish (and Blizzard,
i do not want to be waiting till Christmas just to get
it)

10. more terrain variables... a forest or swamp or something
 9. more class variables... how 'bout a druid or paladin or something
 8. hey, some race variables... elves, dwarves, centaurs... you know
    the weirder stuff.
 7. Spells specialized to the class... none of this warriors able
    to cast nova crap... a spell that advanced should be way over
    the head of your average sword swinger.
 6. Weapons specialized... like a mage who spends his days studying
    would ever have the strength to heft a two handed bastard sword
    and actually swing the thing without popping every bone in his
    vertebrae.
 5. Armour specialized... what sort of rogue could fire off rounds
    from her bow at any decent rate while in full plate mail?
 4. A few more towns... Tristram the only civilized place on earth?
    The entire town completely blocked off, no way out... sure if
    you can stretch your imagination around all that...
 3. Why shouldn't a wizard cast a spell in town, he can do it
    anywhere else right... warriors can't swing a sword, rogues
    can't fire off a few shots, at least explain the phenomenum.
 2. Make the next releases story line a good one, not some chase
    after Diablo's brothers or some other nonsence like that.
 1. Create a game platform intended for easy upgrade and versatility. 
    That way as cheats inevitably come up patches can be readied and
    integrated with less difficulty.

Anyways, just a pipe dream.  It'd be nice to see a few of those
eh.
