Spell #1 -  For me was Stone Purse - Cast by accident in the heat of 
battle conjured an elderly woman who beat monsters and adventures 
alike with that painfully hard purse.

Spell #2 - Was Chain lighting when cast lights connected to chains 
fell from the ceilings of hell illuminating the adventures and 
exposing us to death by witches who could now see their targets 
clearly. 

Spell #3 - Town pothole - mages have been known to totally disappear 
in these never to be seen again.

Spell #4 - Firecall - A man in a yellow rubber suit appeared and put 
out any fire magic that was cast with his mighty water hose.(Quite 
annoying while trying to fight witches) 

Spell#5 - Moldy Bolt obviously the green clumps had little effect on 
Leoric

Spell#6 - trying to cast Heal and mispoke Hail - needless to say 
those golfball size frozen projectiles did nothing to improve my HP's.

All for now...
