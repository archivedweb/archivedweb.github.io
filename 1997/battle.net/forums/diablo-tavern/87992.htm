Greetings weary travellers,
Come join us at the Tarot Guild Hall it is open again after its 
recent rebuilding, and is better than ever.  Good conversation, and 
commeraderie are the norm within.

After a long while of being closed to new applicants Tarot is once 
again taking on new recruits for membership into the Houses of Tarot:
The House of Wands for Mages
The House of Cups for Rogues
The House of Swords for Warriors 
The House of Coins for members of all three classes

Tarot is one of the longest running traditions of the Battle.net, we 
offer Mature Co-op gaming for those of you that tire of the nasty 
gaming experience that is public Battle.net Gaming.  We are a Non-
Cheating (no duping, hacking, God-Mode etc.), generally Non-PKing 
(but do make exceptions depending on the circumstance and position in 
Tarot, We hold a few spots for Judicious Kills), and completely 
legit.  We have our own legal system for dealing with anyone caught 
cheating in any way.

Spaces are filling fast so please visit our page at:   
http://athena.3-cities.com/~tarot/

If you are interested in Joining Tarot please e-mail me.

Zaphod (Hierophant of Tarot)
