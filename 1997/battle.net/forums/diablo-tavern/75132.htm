wants to kill you, 'tis best to leave him between you and the 
monsters. Alas, I had forgotten this lesson it would seem, and just

me. It took me a few seconds and two potions to realize that the

fingers from the potion keys long enough for the fireballs to take 
their toll. Of course, with no suspicion left at all in my mind, I 
quickly restarted. Seems this was a wise move on my part.
