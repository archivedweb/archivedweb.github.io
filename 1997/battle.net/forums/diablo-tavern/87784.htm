My mage has found numerous pieces of zodiac jewelry.  My Rogue has
found a couple of zodiac rings.  The level of the character and the
difficulty of the game don't seem to matter, what matters is the
level of the dungeon.  Most of my zodiac items were found in levels
14, 15 and 16 in normal difficulty universes.  One may have been
found in nightmare or hell difficulty.  Zodiac items are rare.  You
can play for a long time not finding any, and then suddenly in a
couple of days find several of them.

The best level for finding unique items is level 15 because of the
high number of special monsters.
