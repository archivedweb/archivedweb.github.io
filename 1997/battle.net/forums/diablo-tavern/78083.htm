Psst!! Psst!!

Yes! You, come near,  closer
I can’t tread that well you know.
Share a smile maybe a laughter.
and a wondrous thing I will show.

I heed the names you mutter
Beyond the boundaries of the stream’s flow.
In town, people’s clutter.
While I stand by a tree as in a tow.

Wirt, Squirt.
Worm in the dirt.


Such a long walk you utter
as you cross the bridge narrow.
50 gold  for fleeting barter.
Maybe this time a bane for my foe.

Wirt, 
One legged pert. 

What do you have? Gold proper?
a useless rag of the crow?
or the crass cap of martyr.
You will be back, I know.

Wirt,
Pegg-leg boy.
So coy.


But what would you do?
if you stood by this tree.
Would you still hate me
but if you only knew.

I want to walk again.
Into the sun if I can

A strange bow of  slaughter
or kings,savage and holy 
I have it all even godly
so next time you wander.

I may show you something wondrous.
