A similar thing happened to me a while back.  This is probably 
because when you got your system back up, you either added Windows 95 
networking, and in doing so, you gave a new computer name for your 
computer.  Or you changed your computer name.  In either case, as far 
as I know, there is no way to retrieve your characters.  I've written 
Blizzard about this, and their reply was something like: "Sorry, 
we can't help you, Diablo is built this way to discourage software 
piracy."  You cannot change your computer name.  If you do, your 
characters will disappear.

Good luck.
