The trouble is, Wiseman, that we are all influenced by our moods.  We 
are inconstant, selfish creatures.  We feel the way you felt today, 
occasionally, and it is usually based on whatever's bringing us down 
in our own lives...  When we are happy, the world is wonderful...  
When we are down, we find depression in everything...  That is why we 
don't "do" anything about it, because we ourselves, 
subconsciously know that "it will pass"...  So we run away 
for the time being...  Into a computer game, into a book, into 
wallowing in our own depression, whatever...  Depression is sooo 
sweet, do you realise that?
  The more sensitive a person you are, the more you think for 
yourself, the more you feel the need to escape.  People who 
"DO" things, who play a part in "doing things", are 
the ones who have the ability to "feel less"...  This is just 
a thought.  I am not sure if
it's the truth...  And guess what?  Whatever I think now, is 
influenced by my own depression...  By my own "mood"...
