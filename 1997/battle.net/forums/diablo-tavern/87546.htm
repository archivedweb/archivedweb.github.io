Just a thought.  Duping is cheating, hacking is cheating, using duped 
items is cheating.  But how bout this:

Recieving gifts for your low level character?  That is, someone of a 
higher level offers your low leveler say a Zod and some cool plate.
Now normally your low leveler wouldn't get his hands on that sort of 
stuff till at least the caves, but usually not till hell.  This 
equipment would allow a level one to breaze through both the Dungeons 
and the Cats,  nothing could touch him.
Also along those lines, passing equipment down from one of your own 
characters to a new character.  Cheating?
I know this is a pretty comon practice, so many of you will say no, 
its fair.  But think about it.  That low leveler would never have 
been able to get that stuff so quick.

Just food for thought.  And not very stimulating thought at that.
