Well, I went back in this morning (I'm on vacation.. hehehe) to 
explore a little.  Found a blue moongate, and started hopping around. 
 I ended up in Magincia.   Found out that daggers don't work well on 
serpents.... a few strikes of the fangs, and I'm a dead warrior.  
ARGH... I restart with loss of skills, and decide to seek lesser 
prey.  Lo and behold, I find my dream arena..... the beach at 
Magincia is infested with ... RATS!!  Drawing my rusty... er, 
trusty.. dagger, I start hacking away at the foul creatures.  Soon 
the beach is littered with their hulking carcasses.  AHA!  My 
strength shoots up to 40, and I can finally wield that long sword 
I've been dragging around.  BEWARE me now, oh rodents of the night!

The game is actually playable now.  NO crashes in two episodes of 
playing for about an hour at a time.  Lag is bearable.  NPCs are 
stupid... seemingly more-so than in phase 1 (?)

This is going to be big... I said it before and I'm saying it again.  
UO is going to be HUGE.  The only way they can screw it up, is if 
they charge too much.  I think somebody already mentioned that there 
will NOT be an hourly surcharge.  The two options I saw listed were a 
monthly fee, and a bulk rate fee for several months.
