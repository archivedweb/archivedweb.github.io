Enter into a world where evil has corrupted the land and shackled humanity into unholy slavery. Vengeance, power, intellect, and magic -- these are the tools you will need to battle the lord of all evil, Diablo.
This shareware version lets you experience Diablo through two dungeon levels, which are randomly generated each time you play. Full multiplayer capabilities are included, as well as unlimited free access to battle.net, Blizzard Entertainment's online gaming service.
The minimum system requirements for this version are:
  • Pentium 60 with 16MB memory
  • Windows 95 or NT 4.0
  • DirectX 2.0 or higher
  • SVGA local bus video card
  • Microsoft compatible mouse
