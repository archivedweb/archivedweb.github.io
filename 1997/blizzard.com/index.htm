For Mac gamers ready for another dose of Warcraft II, their next series of missions have arrived. This week, Blizzard Entertainment shipped the Macintosh version of Warcraft II: Beyond the Dark Portal, an expansion CD-ROM. In Warcraft II: Beyond the Dark Portal, players encounter 24 new do-or-die missions across two different campaigns. The expansion set offers exciting game play set in Orcish swamplands, 50 custom maps and new Legendary Heroes to aid players in their missions.
