Are you a kick-ass artist?


Blizzard Entertainment is seeking innovative, fantasy/science-fiction 2D and 3D artists to design and develop state-of-the-art computer games. Career advancement is only limited by the artist's talent, technical skills, and raw creativity.

Required Qualifications
  • 3D artists must be experienced in Lightwave, 3DS MAX, Softimage, Alias, Hash, and/or 3DS4
  • Avid gamer with an extreme passion for creating game art and animation
  • Experience with computers, including Windows 95 and Macintosh
  • Ability to work long hours when necessary
  • Work full-time, on-site in Irvine, California
Other useful skills
Experience and high skill level in:
  • Figure drawing
  • 2D character design
  • Illustration
  • Storyboarding
  • Cinematic production
  • Background layout and tiling
  • User interface design
  • 3D Studio MAX Character Studio and MetaBalls
  • Photoshop
  • Illustrator
  • Custom animation production tools
The ideal candidate will have several years of computer animation production experience outside of school, including jobs, internships, freelance, and personal projects.
