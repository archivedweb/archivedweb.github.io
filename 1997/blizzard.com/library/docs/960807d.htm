Blizzard Entertainment Announces Diablo for the Macintosh


Maker of award-winning Warcraft series introduces new RPG in which supernatural forces and demonic creatures await players as they descend into an evil underworld

BOSTON, Aug. 7, 1996 -- At MacWorld starting today, Blizzard Entertainment announced plans for a Macintosh version of Diablo, a new role-playing game in which players descend into a sinister underworld filled with supernatural forces and demonic creatures.

Diablo transports players to a medieval village plagued with evil and darkness. In a quest for vengeance, the central character descends into a crypt and expansive labyrinth hidden beneath the village. Game play focuses on exploration, conflict and character development as players travel deeper into the underworld’s demonic halls.

Diablo will be available in Macintosh CD-ROM format at most computer and software retail chains nationwide for approximately $50. The game will also be available directly through Blizzard at (800) 953-SNOW.

Says Allen Adham, president and founder of Blizzard Entertainment, “Diablo will offer gamers a new kind of role-playing game. The game boasts not only fully rendered graphics never seen before in an RPG, but also unlimited replayability. Each time a quest begins, Diablo’s random level generator creates a completely different labyrinth. With every game, players can expect to see a room, corridor, monster, trap or treasure in a way that they have never encountered before.”

Product Overview
As the central character in Diablo, players explore a dungeon hidden beneath a desecrated church in their village. Players can choose from three different character types: a fighter, rogue or sorceror -- each with distinct abilities and strengths. As players encounter numerous levels filled with corridors, traps, weapons, magical items, non-playing characters and demonic creatures, the truth about what evil force decimated their village begins to unravel.

Diablo includes multiplayer support for as many as four players and one-click free access to on-line gaming via the company’s Battle.net service. Blizzard’s Battle.net provides an arena for Blizzard customers to chat, challenge opponents and initiate multiplayer games.

Key features in Diablo will include:
  • A fully rendered three-dimensional, isometric role-playing game of demonic horror.
  • A level-generation system that randomly places rooms, corridors, traps, treasures, monsters and stairways to maximize game play.
  • Multiplayer option that supports as many of four players via a network, the Internet and Blizzard’s Battle.net. Head-to-head play supported through direct link and modem.
  • Seamless one-click free access on-line gaming via Blizzard’s Battle.net service. Blizzard charges no hourly or monthly fees for game play. Player is responsible for any applicable Internet access fees.
  • Real-time engine allows for intense exploration and exciting battles.
  • From Skeletal Warriors and Living Corpses to Storm Riders and Overlords, over 100 creatures of the underworld haunt your every step.
