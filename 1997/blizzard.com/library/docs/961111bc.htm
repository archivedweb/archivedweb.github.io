Blizzard's Warcraft Battle Chest Hits Stores For the Holidays



Number-one selling Warcraft series now available in a three CD-ROM set for one great price

IRVINE, Calif., Nov. 11, 1996 - For holiday shoppers looking for the year's most popular computer games at an unbeatable price, the ultimate Warcraft collection has arrived. Blizzard Entertainment this week began shipping Warcraft Battle Chest, a three CD-ROM set that includes the number-one selling Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness, the Warcraft II expansion set and the gaming classic, Warcraft: Orcs and Humans.

This limited edition CD-ROM set is the first time the entire number-one selling Warcraft series has been offered in one collection. Warcraft Battle Chest includes both DOS and Macintosh versions of all three titles and is available nationwide for approximately $65. Customers can call (800) 953-SNOW for additional product and sales information.

The best-selling Warcraft series has earned top industry honors including Game of the Year, Best Multiplayer Game and Best On-line Game as well as high ratings and rave reviews from numerous gaming magazines. Warcraft II continues to be the number-one selling entertainment title of 1996, and has sold more than 1.2 million copies worldwide.

Warcraft Series Overview

Warcraft: Orcs and Humans is a real-time strategic medieval contest in which Orcs and Humans battle for survival and domination. Using an ingenious arsenal of weaponry and powerful magic, players control the fate of either the Orc or the Human civilization as the two forces face off in a quest for supremacy.

Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness is the continuation of the ruthless clash between the Orc and Human civilizations. The game boasts several enhancements including tactical combat over land, sea and air; head-to-head play against as many as eight players; and a multitude of new units such as dragons, submarines, Elven archers, gryphons, battleships, zeppelins and death knights.

The game's expansion set, Warcraft II: Beyond the Dark Portal, offers players 24 new do-or-die missions. The expansion set offers exciting game play set in Orcish swamplands, 50 custom maps and new cinematics and music. New legendary heroes aid players in their missions. Using the full version of Warcraft II, players can compete in head-to-head battles against as many as eight players.

Best known for the number-one selling Warcraft series, Blizzard Entertainment is a premier publisher of entertainment software, and a division of Davidson & Associates, Inc., a wholly owned subsidiary of CUC International Inc. (NYSE: CU).
