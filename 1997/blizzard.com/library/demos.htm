Diablo

Embark if you dare on a dark and sinister quest that will pull you into the very depths of hell itself... and beyond. Explore the first two levels of Tristram in this fully playable two level demo. Includes support for Battle.net.

Demo available for Windows 95
Warcraft: Orcs and Humans

This is a fully playable six level demo of the original real-time strategy game of fantasy combat. Command either the noble Humans or the brutish Orcs as they fight for dominance.

Demo available for IBM-PC (1.5mb) and Macintosh (2.7mb)
Warcraft II: Tides of Darkness

The battle for Azeroth continues over land, sea, and air in this six level fully playable demo. Includes support for head-to-head play over modem, serial-link, and IPX network.

Demo available for IBM-PC and Macintosh
