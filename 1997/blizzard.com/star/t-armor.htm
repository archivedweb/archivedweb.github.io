From the versatility of the Goliath Combat Walker to the awe-inspiring power of the Mjolnir Artillery Cannon, the Armored Vehicle Corps provides the Confederate Marines with greater mobility and firepower than infantry alone can provide. Positions are also available for skilled mechanics to join the Vehicle Maintenace Division.

Vulture Hover-Cycle Pilot
 PEB: Must pass Minimal Physical Fitness Exam

The reliable Vulture-Class Hover Bike, used primarily for scouting and patrol, can reach speeds of up to two hundred and thirty miles per hour. The Vultures are armed with anti-personnel grenades and mines in addition to any personal sidearm that the driver may carry.


Goliath Combat Walker Pilot
 PEB: Min. 6 Months Combat Experience

A well known sight along the Rim, the one-man Goliath Combat Walker combines all-terrain maneuverability with compu-based heavy weapons systems. The Goliath’s arsenal consists of twin anti-armor 20mm Auto-Cannons and Firebolt Scatter Missiles for anti-air support.


Arclite Siege Tank Crew Member
 PEB: Varies by position

The heavily armored Arclite Siege Tanks are renowned throughout the Confederacy for their cannons’ unrelenting barrages of plasma-charged doom and their stalwart emplacement/advance tactics. The Mjolnir Artillery Cannon is so powerful that the Tank must brace itself before each firing. Crew consists of a driver, gunner, and a commander/navigator.
