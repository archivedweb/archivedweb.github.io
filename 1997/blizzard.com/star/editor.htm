Starcraft includes a full-featured scenario editor to let you build your own worlds and campaigns for both single and multiplayer games, providing unlimited gameplay.

  • Intuitive paint-style interface for creating maps

  • Choose from a wide range of terrain types, from desolate space stations to lush jungle worlds

  • 256x256 maps for truly epic battles

  • Assign variable scenario objectives

  • Edit unit statistics, sounds, and abilities

  • Create your own mission briefings

  • Advanced interface controls include floating palettes, cut & paste, and multiple levels of undo
