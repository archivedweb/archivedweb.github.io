Are you a hard-core programmer?


Blizzard Entertainment is looking for extremely talented programmers to help design and develop state-of-the-art computer games. New software engineers will assist in the design and development of original products. Opportunities exist for rapid advancement depending on technical, design and programming abilities.

The ideal candidate will have several years of C programming experience outside of school, including jobs, internships, and "programming for fun".

Required Qualifications

  • Job seekers must be avid gamers and have a passion for programming
  • B.S. in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • Programming experience using Windows or DOS
  • Knowledge of C/C++, assembly language, and Win32
  • Knowledge of DirectX game programming
  • An understanding of the tradeoffs between 'C' and object-oriented programming
  • Experience writing multithreaded applications
  • A passion for optimizing
  • Excellent communication skills
  • Ability to work long hours when necessary
  • Full time only
  • Work on-site in Irvine, California
